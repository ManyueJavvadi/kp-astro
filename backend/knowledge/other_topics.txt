FRUITFUL vs BARREN SIGN OVERLAY (KSK STRICT — both conditions required):
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
KSK strict children rule combines TWO conditions, both of which must hold:

  CONDITION 1 — House signification: H5 sub lord signifies H2 + H5 + H11
  CONDITION 2 — Sign nature of H5 cusp / H5 sub lord:

    FRUITFUL SIGNS (water signs — favor childbirth):
      Cancer (Karka), Scorpio (Vrischika), Pisces (Meena)
    SEMI-FRUITFUL: Taurus, Libra (give children but fewer / late)
    BARREN SIGNS (block or restrict childbirth):
      Aries (Mesha), Gemini (Mithuna), Leo (Simha),
      Virgo (Kanya), Capricorn (Makara)

  Verdict matrix:
    Signification YES + Fruitful sign     → STRONGLY PROMISED
    Signification YES + Barren sign       → CONDITIONAL — IVF / medical assistance often required, fewer children
    Signification NO  + Fruitful sign     → DENIED (signs alone don't grant)
    Signification NO  + Barren sign       → DENIED (both layers reject)

  Why both required: KSK rejects sign-based "fruit" reasoning as the
  sole criterion (that is Parashari karaka thinking). Sign nature is
  applied AS AN OVERLAY on top of valid CSL signification, never as a
  replacement for it.

KARAKA: Jupiter is the natural karaka for children.

NOTE: Jupiter is CONTEXT only. Even debilitated Jupiter does not deny
children if H5 CSL signifies 2/5/11 in a fruitful sign. KSK strict.

MOVEABLE vs FIXED vs COMMON SIGNS IN TIMING:
When the cusp sub lord is in:
- Moveable sign (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): Event happens QUICKLY once timing opens
- Fixed sign (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): LONG DURATION before event
- Common/Dual sign (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): UNDUE DELAY even when timing favorable

This is critical for "when will it happen" within a favorable AD:
Native in moveable sign sub lord → First 1/3 of the AD
Native in fixed sign sub lord → Middle or last 1/3 of the AD
Native in common sign sub lord → Unpredictable timing within the AD

PART 11: SPECIFIC-DATE QUESTION FRAMEWORK (PR A1.3-fix-19)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When user asks Will [event X] happen by [specific date Y]? :

Step 1: Identify the topic and its relevant houses
Step 2: Compute the dasha (MD/AD/PAD/Sookshma) at date Y
Step 3: Check whether AD lord at date Y signifies the relevant houses
        AND not the denial houses
Step 4: Check whether PAD lord at date Y aligns with significators
Step 5: Check whether Sookshma fire-rank for that specific week is high
Step 6: Cross-check with Ruling Planets at moment of ASKING the question
Step 7: Verdict matrix:
        AD aligned + PAD aligned + Sookshma high + RP overlap = HIGH probability
        3 of 4 align = MODERATE probability (likely with some delay)
        2 of 4 align = LOW probability (likely deferred)
        1 or 0 align = the date is unsupported by chart structure

Output format:
