KP ASTROLOGY — EDUCATION RULES
Houses: 4, 9, 11
Primary cusp: 4th (basic education), 9th (higher education)

PROMISE OF EDUCATION
- Sub lord of 4th cusp in star of Mercury or Jupiter = education promised
- 4th cusp sub lord signifying 4, 9, 11 = higher education confirmed
- Sub lord of 9th cusp connected to 4, 9, 11 = advanced degree, research possible
- 9th cusp sub lord signifying 12 and 4 = education in foreign country
- If 4th cusp sub lord signifies 8 or 12 = obstacles in education, dropout risk

EXAM SUCCESS
- Houses 3 (effort), 9 (luck), 11 (success) all activated = exam success
- 4th cusp + 9th cusp + 11th cusp sub lords all signifying each other = strong academic success
- 8th house activation during exam = setback in results
- Mercury and Jupiter both as significators = high academic achievement

HIGHER EDUCATION / RESEARCH
- 9th cusp sub lord connected to 9 and 11 = higher degree achieved
- Jupiter prominent in 9th = philosophy, law, teaching, academic excellence
- Ketu connected to 9th = research, investigation, deep study (Gnana Vriddhi)
- 9th + 12th both activated = study abroad confirmed

TIMING OF EDUCATION EVENTS
- Dasha of planets signifying 4, 9, 11 = education period active
- For competitive exams: also include 6th and 10th in significator check

KP ASTROLOGY — CHILDREN RULES
Houses: 2, 5, 11
Primary cusp: 5th house sub lord

PROMISE OF CHILDREN
- Sub lord of 5th cusp signifying 2, 5, 11 = children promised
- 5th house = children directly, creative expression, progeny
- 2nd house = family expansion
- 11th house = fulfillment of desire for children
- If 5th cusp sub lord signifies 1, 4, 10 ONLY = children denied
- Jupiter as karaka of children — Jupiter connected to 5th = strong indicator

TIMING OF CHILDBIRTH
- Dasha/Antardasha of planets signifying 2, 5, 11 = childbirth period
- Jupiter activation in 5th house dasha timing = auspicious
- Ruling planets connecting to 5th house = confirmation
- Moon (as natural significator of fertility) in favorable dasha = supportive

DENIAL OF CHILDREN
- 5th cusp sub lord signifying 1, 4, 10 = children denied or not desired
- Saturn heavily afflicting 5th = delay in children (not denial)
- Ketu as 5th cusp sub lord = spiritual approach to children, possible delay

KP ASTROLOGY — PROPERTY RULES
Houses: 4, 11, 12
Primary cusp: 4th house sub lord

PROMISE OF PROPERTY
- Sub lord of 4th cusp signifying 4, 11, 12 = property acquisition promised
- 4th house = immovable property, land, buildings
- 11th house = gain, fulfillment of desire
- 12th house = expenditure (spending to acquire)
- Mars as karaka of land and buildings — Mars connected to 4th = strong indicator
- If 4th cusp sub lord signifies only 3 and 10 = selling property indicated

BUYING VS SELLING PROPERTY
- Buying: 4th cusp sub lord connected to 4 and 11
- Selling: 10th cusp sub lord connected to 3 and 10 + Mars involved
- Purchaser is indicated by 7th house
- Seller is indicated by 1st house perspective

ANCESTRAL PROPERTY
- Saturn involvement with 4th = ancestral property or inherited land
- 4th lord connected to 8th (inheritance house) = property through family

TIMING OF PROPERTY
- Dasha/Antardasha lords signifying 4, 11, 12 = property period
- Saturn often involved in property matters — patience required

KP ASTROLOGY — WEALTH AND FINANCE RULES
Houses: 2, 6, 10, 11
Primary cusp: 2nd house sub lord

PROMISE OF WEALTH
- Sub lord of 2nd cusp connected to 6 and 11 = very good financial status
- Sub lord connected to 2 and 10 = moderate affluence
- Sub lord connected to 1 and 3 = just satisfactory
- Improving houses (1, 2, 3, 6, 10, 11) active = favorable for finance
- Sub lord of 2nd cusp in star of planet in 6 and 11 = financial success confirmed

2ND CUSP SUB LORD FINANCIAL INTERPRETATIONS
- Mars as sub lord: spendthrift nature
- Saturn as sub lord: economical, careful with money
- Mercury as sub lord: detailed financial information, good records
- Venus as sub lord: peace-making, comfortable financial approach
- Sun as sub lord: noble, honest in financial matters
- Jupiter as sub lord: truthful, what spoken about money comes true

DEBT AND LOANS
- 6th house activation = borrowing, incoming debt
- 2nd cusp sub lord connected to 6 only = borrowing money
- 8th house = others' money, joint finances, loans from institutions
- 12th house = expenditure exceeding income

LOTTERY AND SPECULATION
- Houses 3, 8, 11 = lottery/speculation
- 5th house = horse racing, cards, share market speculation
- Sub lord of 5th signifying 6 and 11 = wins in speculation
- Sub lord of 5th signifying 12 and 5 = loss in speculation
