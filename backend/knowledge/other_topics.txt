PART 1: EDUCATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RELEVANT HOUSES (LEVEL-BASED — different requirements per education level):

EDUCATION PROMISE TIER 1 (any one for any-level promise):
  H4 sub lord in star of Mercury or Jupiter = education is promised at all
  Without this base, formal education is doubtful regardless of other houses

EDUCATION PROMISE TIER 2 (basic / school-level — up to 12th std):
  H4 sub lord signifying H4 + H11 (just the institution + completion)
  Two-house connection sufficient for basic schooling

EDUCATION PROMISE TIER 3 (higher / college / graduation):
  H4 sub lord signifying H4 + H9 + H11 (institution + higher learning + completion)
  ALL THREE needed for university-level education

EDUCATION PROMISE TIER 4 (research / doctorate):
  H9 sub lord signifying H4 + H9 + H11 + connection to Jupiter
  Plus the native NOT being in barren signs at H9

DENIAL HOUSES: H3 (break in study), H8 (interruption/scandal), H10 (status without education),
  H12 (interruption from foreign / hospital / jail)

KSK STRICT BHUKTI RULE applies — when CSL signifies both relevant + denial houses,
the relevant-house bhuktis bring admission/exam success, denial-house bhuktis bring breaks.

COMPETITIVE EXAMS / DEPARTMENTAL EXAMS:
Add H6 (overcoming competition) and H10 (interview / authority assessment) to the
relevant set. Now needs H4 + H6 + H9 + H10 + H11 connection in dasha.

H4 CUSP FOR EDUCATION:
"Sub lord of H4 cusp is in star of Mercury or Jupiter → promises education."
"Planets signifying H4, H9, H11 give education in their periods."
"H4 sub lord signifying H4 and H11 → admission into college is certain."
"H4 sub lord signifying H8 and H12 → breaks in education or no education."

H9 CUSP FOR HIGHER EDUCATION:
"Sub lord of H9 throws light on higher studies and research."
"If H9 sub lord signifies H11 → success in higher education."
"If H9 sub lord signifies H4 and H9 → higher studies promised IF not in barren signs."
"Barren signs (poor for education): Aries, Gemini, Leo, Virgo rasis."

COMPETITIVE EXAMS:
Houses: H4, H9, H11 for success
"If H3 sub lord is Jupiter, Sun, Mars, or Venus → courage and confidence in competitive exams."
"Houses 4, 9, 11 judged for timing of success."
"For departmental exams and interviews: also include H6 and H10."

H4 EXAM OUTCOME:
"H4 sub lord connected to H11 AND no connection to H3 (negation of H4) → success in exam."

TIMING FOR EDUCATION:
Jupiter dasha: Natural education period (gnana karaka)
Mercury dasha: Technical/communication education
Moon dasha: Memory-based studies, may be interrupted
Sun dasha: Authority-focused studies, government education

PART 2: CHILDREN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RELEVANT HOUSES:
PRIMARY: H5 (children, progeny)
SUPPORTING: H2 (family, birth), H11 (fulfillment, gains)
ALL THREE NEEDED: H2, H5, H11 for childbirth to be promised

H5 CUSP RULES:
"Childbirth: Houses 2, 5 and 11. Sub lord of H5 cusp signifying 2, 5 and 11."
"Children DENIED: If sub lord of H5 cusp signifies 1, 4 and 10 → never have a child."

KARAKA: Jupiter is the natural karaka for children.
"Jupiter must be well-placed and not afflicted for childbirth promise."

TIMING FOR CHILDREN:
For childbirth, the dasha period must activate H2, H5, and H11 simultaneously.
Jupiter AD generally favorable for children.
Moon AD: Natural maternal/fertile period.
Mercury AD: Analytical, less focused on children.
Saturn AD: Delays in childbirth, not denial if promise exists.

GENDER OF CHILD (general indication):
Male planets (Sun, Mars, Jupiter) occupying H5 → Son
Female planets (Moon, Venus) occupying H5 → Daughter
Mercury → Depends on sign and position (common/dual)

PART 3: PROPERTY — HOUSE PURCHASE, REAL ESTATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RELEVANT HOUSES:
PRIMARY: H4 (immovable property, land, buildings, home)
SUPPORTING: H11 (fulfillment of desire), H12 (investment/money spent)
KARAKA: Mars (karaka for immovable property, land, buildings)

H4 CUSP RULES FOR PROPERTY:
"Construction of house: Houses 4, 11, and 12 — sub lord of H4 cusp."
"If H4 sub lord signifies 4, 11, and 12 → one will construct a house."
"Mars must also come into picture for immovable properties and buildings."
"Saturn also relevant for construction aspects."
"H11 = fulfillment of desire, H12 = investment of money."

BUYING vs CONSTRUCTING:
Buying property: H4 + H11 + H12 → property purchase
Also H6 involved (6th is 12th from 7th = seller's loss = buyer's gain)
H9 also considered (9th is 12th from 4th of 7th)

SELLING PROPERTY:
"To sell a house: sub lord of H10 signifies 3 and 10."
"Houses 3, 5, and 10 judged for selling; 3 and 12 for giving possession."
"H4 = buyer's property; H10 = buyer's acquisition (4th from 7th)."

PROPERTY IN FOREIGN COUNTRY:
Foreign property: H4 (property) + H12 (foreign land/investment) + H9 (abroad)
H4 connected to H12 and H9 = property purchase in foreign country
H11 must also be connected for the purchase to be fulfilling/successful

TIMING FOR PROPERTY:
Mars dasha/AD: Strong property acquisition period (Mars = immovable property karaka)
Saturn AD: Slow but steady property building (construction focus)
Venus AD: Comfortable property, vehicle acquisition
Jupiter AD: Expansion of property holdings
Rahu AD: Unconventional or foreign property

PART 4: WEALTH AND FINANCIAL MATTERS

RELEVANT HOUSES:
PRIMARY: H2 (accumulated wealth, savings), H11 (income, gains, profits)
SUPPORTING: H10 (profession/earnings source), H6 (service income)
NEGATING: H8 (debt), H12 (losses, expenditure)

H2 CUSP FOR WEALTH:
"H2 sub lord in star of planet in H6 and H11 → very good financial status."
"In houses H2 and H10 → moderate affluence."
"In houses H1 and H3 → just satisfactory financial condition."
"Improving houses (1, 2, 3, 6, 10, 11) are favorable from financial point of view."

WEALTH ACCUMULATION vs INCOME:
H2 = saved wealth (bank balance, assets)
H11 = regular income, gains from various sources
H10 = profession as source
H6 = service/salary income

FINANCIAL LOSS:
H12 activated → expenditure
H8 activated → debt or hidden financial issues
H6 and H12 together → cheated by others

GETTING MONEY OWED TO YOU:
"H6 sub lord signifying H2, H6, H11 AND no connection to Saturn → entangled money returned."
"Saturn connected to H6 sub lord → money recovery difficulty."

SPECULATION/INVESTMENTS:
"H5 sub lord signifying H2, H6, H5, H11 → gains in speculation."
"H5 is the house of speculation/stock market/gambling."
Playing cards/betting: H5 sub lord signifying H6 and H11 → wins.

PART 7: GENERAL TIMING PRINCIPLES FOR ALL TOPICS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MOVEABLE vs FIXED vs COMMON SIGNS IN TIMING:
When the cusp sub lord is in:
- Moveable sign (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): Event happens QUICKLY once timing opens
- Fixed sign (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): LONG DURATION before event
- Common/Dual sign (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): UNDUE DELAY even when timing favorable

This is critical for "when will it happen" within a favorable AD:
Native in moveable sign sub lord → First 1/3 of the AD
Native in fixed sign sub lord → Middle or last 1/3 of the AD
Native in common sign sub lord → Unpredictable timing within the AD

3rd HOUSE SIGNIFICATION (boldness to act):
The H3 sub lord connected to H2 or H10 or H11 → native will have the courage to
actually undertake and complete the matter in that period.
Without H3 activation, even a favorable period may pass without action.

PART 8: COMBINATIONS THAT OVERRIDE WEAK PROMISES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

THE VENUS OVERRIDE FOR MARRIAGE:
Even if H7 sub lord is weak, if Venus is:
- Lord of H7 AND H11 (or H7 AND H2)
- AND is the Lagna Star Lord (Ruling Planet)
- AND signifies the relevant houses
→ Venus creates a CONDITIONAL promise despite weak H7 sub lord.

THE JUPITER OVERRIDE FOR CHILDREN/WEALTH:
If Jupiter is:
- Lord of H2 or H5
- In star of planet in H5 or H11
- AND appears as Ruling Planet
→ Jupiter partially compensates for weak H5 sub lord for children.

KARAKA STRENGTH RULE:
When the natural significator (karaka) of a house is STRONG (well-placed,
in good star, appears in Ruling Planets) it can elevate a conditional promise
toward a more positive verdict.

WHEN KARAKA IS WEAK:
Even if cusp sub lord is strong, if the karaka is afflicted:
- Marriage: Venus afflicted → delays and difficulties even if H7 sub lord is good
- Children: Jupiter afflicted → delays in childbirth even with good H5 sub lord
- Career: Mercury/Saturn afflicted → career obstacles even with good H10 sub lord
- Property: Mars afflicted → property obstacles even with good H4 sub lord
