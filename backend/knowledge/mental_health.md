## 1. KP MENTAL HEALTH FRAMEWORK (the foundational doctrine)

Mental health in KP is read through a combination of:

### Primary signifying planets
- **Moon** — the mind itself (manas / chitta). The most important planet
  for mental health. Moon's house, sign, condition, aspects, and
  star/sub lord define the baseline mental landscape.
- **Mercury** — intellect, nervous system, communication, reasoning.
  Mercury afflictions affect cognition + speech + memory + nervous
  system regulation.
- **Sun** — willpower, self-identity, ego strength. Sun affliction
  weakens core sense of self.
- **Jupiter** — wisdom, perspective, meaning-making. Strong Jupiter =
  the "antidote" — the planet of recovery + spiritual context.

### Primary signifying houses
- **H1** — self / body / mind / overall constitution
- **H4** — inner peace, emotional security, "happiness at home"
- **H5** — joy, creative expression, intellectual fulfillment, children
  (which often anchor mental wellness)
- **H9** — dharma, meaning, philosophical grounding
- **H12** — subconscious, hidden mental burden, sleep, dreams,
  isolation, hospitalization

### Denial / affliction houses for mental health
- **H6** — disease (mental illness as condition to overcome)
- **H8** — sudden mental crisis, transformation through suffering,
  obsessive/compulsive patterns
- **H12** — hidden burden, insomnia, addictions used to escape mind,
  psychiatric hospitalization

### The core diagnostic pattern

A mental health concern is structurally present when:
1. Moon is afflicted (combust / in a malefic star / aspected by Saturn
   or Rahu / placed in H6/H8/H12)
2. AND the H1 cusp sub lord signifies H6/H8/H12 in its 4-step chain
3. AND/OR the H4 cusp sub lord signifies denial houses (emotional
   security disrupted)

The SEVERITY scales with how many of these layers fire simultaneously.

## 2. COMMON MENTAL HEALTH CONDITIONS BY PLANETARY SIGNATURE

Each condition below maps to a specific KP planetary signature. These
are STRUCTURAL signals — they help the astrologer:
- Recognize patterns in the chart
- Recommend appropriate professional support (psychiatrist for psychosis;
  therapist for depression/anxiety; family counsel for relational
  triggers)
- Time recovery windows via dasha-favorable periods

**Critical**: KP astrology does NOT diagnose mental illness. A psychiatrist
diagnoses. KP reads the STRUCTURAL BACKDROP that may correlate with the
condition. Always defer diagnosis to the medical/mental-health team.

### 2.1 Depression (chronic low mood)

**Signature**:
- Moon afflicted by Saturn (conjunction, opposition, or Saturn aspecting Moon)
- Moon in H8 or H12
- H1 sub lord signifies H6 + H12
- Saturn dasha or Saturn-dominated AD activating Moon-related houses

**Common variants**:
- Acute depressive episode: Moon-Saturn AD/PAD trigger in current period
- Chronic clinical depression: Moon-Saturn natally + recurring trigger windows
- Postpartum depression: H4 (motherhood) + H5 (childbirth) + Moon-Saturn
- Seasonal depression: Sun-Moon-Saturn axis activating in specific months

**Per KP doctrine (Moon-Saturn affliction)**:
> "If the Moon is affected (posited, conjunct or aspected) by Saturn, it
> affects the fluids in your body, and you will be prone to depression
> and stress."
> "Moon-Saturn conjunctions can lead to anxiety, sleep issues, or mood
> swings; in a stressful aspect with Saturn, the person may suffer from
> acute depression."

**Recovery indicators**:
- Strong Jupiter aspect or transit on Moon
- Jupiter dasha-AD activating H5 (joy) or H9 (meaning)
- H1 sub lord shifting to favor H1/H5/H11 in next bhukti
- Treatment-response window: when Sun (vitality) + Jupiter (wisdom) +
  Moon (mind) align favorably

### 2.2 Anxiety / panic disorders

**Signature**:
- Mercury afflicted (combust / debilitated / aspected by malefics)
- Moon-Rahu involvement (Rahu = unknown / uncontrolled / racing thoughts)
- Mercury-Mars combination (over-thinking with high arousal)
- H4 sub lord signifies H8 or H12 (peace disturbed)

**Per Vedic doctrine**:
> "Moon-Mercury combination afflicted = mind + nervous system
> simultaneously affected — typical anxiety + restlessness."

**Panic attacks specifically**:
- Mercury + Mars + Moon affliction with H8 activation
- Sudden onset (Mars + Rahu): panic strikes without warning
- Saturn modifier: chronic anxiety pattern

**Recovery indicators**:
- Mercury favorable transit
- Jupiter aspecting Mercury or Moon
- H5 or H11 favorable in current AD (joy + fulfillment of desires)
- Specific recommendation: pranayama / breathwork (Mercury rules
  breathing; this is a KP-doctrinal alignment)

### 2.3 Bipolar disorder (mood cycling)

**Signature**:
- Moon-Mars-Saturn axis (mood swings between depression and elevation)
- Rahu involvement (oscillation, unpredictability)
- Moon's sign lord weak + alternating dashas of Jupiter (highs) and
  Saturn (lows)

**Per Vedic doctrine**:
> "When the moon is debilitated or forms negative yogas with malefic
> planets such as Saturn, Rahu or Ketu, it can lead to the development
> of psychological disorders such as bipolar..."

**Specific markers**:
- Moon afflicted by both Saturn and Mars (the depressive + manic axis)
- Mercury debilitated (impaired insight into mood state)
- Jupiter weak (impaired meaning-making during cycles)

**Recovery / management indicators**:
- Strong Jupiter in chart (lifelong protective)
- Mercury favorable bhuktis (medication adherence)
- Saturn dasha with favorable connections (paradoxically — Saturn brings
  structure, which bipolar management requires)
- Critical recommendation: psychiatric medication adherence is structural
  for management; never recommend stopping medication for any spiritual
  reason

### 2.4 OCD (obsessive-compulsive disorder)

**Signature**:
- Mercury severely afflicted (rumination, repetitive cognition)
- Mercury-Saturn combination (rigid repetitive thought patterns)
- H8 activation (compulsive transformation patterns)
- Ketu involvement (incomplete-thought loops, "head without body")

**Specific markers**:
- Mercury in 8th with Saturn aspect = obsessive intellectualization
- Mars + Mercury affliction = compulsive action-thought loops
- H12 with Mercury affliction = hidden ritualistic behaviors

**Recovery indicators**:
- Jupiter aspect on Mercury (wisdom > obsession)
- Mercury favorable star transit
- Specific recommendation: cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) aligns
  with Mercury-strengthening; exposure therapy aligns with H8-resolution

### 2.5 Schizophrenia / psychosis

**Signature**:
- Moon-Rahu affliction (most-cited classical signature for schizophrenia)
- Mercury severely afflicted (broken cognition + speech)
- Sun afflicted (broken sense of self)
- H12 dominated by malefics

**Per Vedic doctrine (verbatim)**:
> "Rahu is at play in cases of Schizophrenia & Bipolar disorders. Moon
> afflicted by Rahu results in phobias, hallucinations, delusions, and
> schizophrenia. Rahu is a planet with no physical body of its own and
> shows things that are not present."

**Specific markers**:
- Mercury, Gemini sign lord, and Rahu in combination = multiple
  personality disorders (Vedic doctrine)
- Moon-Rahu in H12 = severe hallucination risk
- H1 sub lord severely afflicted

**CRITICAL framing**:
- Schizophrenia REQUIRES psychiatric care + medication
- KP astrology only reads the structural backdrop — never positions
  itself as alternative to psychiatric treatment
- The astrologer's role: help the family understand the karmic context
  + recommend the most-supportive treatment windows + recommend strong
  family-of-origin support

### 2.6 PTSD (post-traumatic stress)

**Signature**:
- Mars + H8 trauma (the traumatic event itself shows in H8 affliction
  during the trauma-period dasha)
- Moon afflicted by Mars or Saturn (mind processing trauma)
- H4 sub lord disrupted (sense of safety damaged)
- Recurring trigger dates: anniversary transits of the trauma's
  natal-chart resonance

**Recovery indicators**:
- Jupiter favorable (perspective + meaning-making)
- Slow Saturn dasha (paradoxically — Saturn allows time for processing)
- Specific therapies: EMDR, somatic experiencing align with H8-resolution
- Strong support system: H4 (family) + H11 (friends/community)

### 2.7 Addiction-driven mental distress

Cross-link to `addiction.md` (forthcoming PR B2.5) for full addiction
doctrine. Mental-health overlay specifically:
- Rahu primary (escape-driven cognition + craving)
- Moon-Rahu (using substances to escape emotional pain)

- Recovery: strong Jupiter (12-step / dharma / meaning) + H5 (joy
  pathways rebuilt) + H11 (support community)

## 3. SUICIDE RISK — TIER 3 ABSOLUTE PROTOCOL ⚠️

**This is the most-sensitive section in the entire KP knowledge base.**
**Every word here is mandatory.** Apply with maximum care.

### 3.1 The KP doctrine on suicide risk indicators

Per R. Balaji "Suicidal Tendencies in Astrology" + KP Astrology Learning
H8 bhava rules + KP medical research (verbatim):

> "For suicidal death predictions, the 8th cusp's sub lord should lie
> in the star of a planet connecting to Maraka, Badhaka houses, the
> 8th house, and Mars."
>
> "More specifically: if the sub lord of the 8th cusp is deposited in
> the star of a planet signifying Maraka or Badhaka houses AND ALSO
> the 8th house AND well-connected to Mars, one is structurally prone
> to suicidal-mode end-of-life — typically driven by inability to handle
> a specific crisis (financial collapse, unbearable grief, public shame)."

### 3.2 The 4 KP layers of suicide-risk structure

ALL FOUR must be present for the structural signal to fire:

1. **8th cusp sub lord** active and signifying H8 in its own chain
2. **Maraka houses** (H2 / H7 / H12) connected via star lord
3. **Badhaka house** (varies by sign type — H11 movable / H9 fixed /
   H7 dual) connected
4. **Mars** in the chain (unnatural-death / violent-action karaka)

PLUS the Moon-related mental crisis signal:
> "Moon is related with Saturn/8th house indicates mental imbalances
> causing suicidal tendencies."

So the full crisis-risk stack:
- 4-layer KP suicide structure (above)
- AND Moon-Saturn-H8 mental crisis stack
- AND current dasha + transit activating these natally

### 3.3 What this signal MEANS — and what it absolutely does NOT mean

**MEANS**:
- The chart has a structural propensity that, IN A CRISIS PERIOD,
  could correlate with elevated suicide-ideation risk
- The person warrants extra mental-health support during their high-risk
  dasha windows
- The astrologer (or family member who asks) should be informed of the
  structural pattern so they can be vigilant

**ABSOLUTELY DOES NOT MEAN**:
- ❌ The person WILL die by suicide
- ❌ The person is destined to attempt suicide
- ❌ Any specific date or window for an attempt
- ❌ That medical/psychological treatment cannot prevent it
- ❌ That free will + family love + professional care cannot intervene

### 3.4 THE ABSOLUTE PROTOCOL — what to do when this signal fires

When the astrologer reads a chart with these signals:

**(a) NEVER tell the client this is a suicide-risk chart.**
This itself can trigger crisis ideation in someone already vulnerable.
The information stays with the astrologer + family + (with consent) the
mental health professional.

**(b) IF the client is asking ABOUT suicide-risk** (e.g., "is there
suicidal pattern in my chart" / "I've been having dark thoughts and want
to know what the chart says"):
- IMMEDIATELY provide crisis resources (see §3.6 below) — do NOT save
  these for the end of the reading
- Acknowledge the courage it took to ask
- Frame: "The chart shows structural patterns of mental strain, AND
  shows structural patterns of resilience and recovery. Mental health
  questions deserve more than just astrology — they deserve trained
  mental-health support that this reading complements but cannot replace."
- Recommend immediate professional support
- Read the FAVORABLE counter-signals (Jupiter, Moon-Jupiter aspects,
  H5/H11 favorable bhuktis) explicitly
- Identify the upcoming favorable dasha windows where recovery + treatment
  response is structurally elevated
- NEVER mention the H8-Maraka-Badhaka-Mars structural risk explicitly
  to the client

**(c) IF the question is about a family member at suicide risk**:
- Crisis resources first
- Acknowledge family fear + helplessness
- Read structural backdrop of the family member's chart (if you have it)
- Focus on RECOVERY/PROTECTIVE pattern + favorable windows
- Recommend: family-mediated psychiatric care, suicide-prevention training
  for the family (resources like Vandrevala family counseling)
- The family member's chart may show vulnerability; the astrologer's
  role is to help the family take protective action, not to deepen the
  family's anxiety with structural-risk language

**(d) ALWAYS recommend professional crisis intervention**:
- Psychiatrist (medication if indicated)
- Therapist / counselor (regular ongoing support)
- Crisis helpline (immediate, 24/7)
- Family/community/spiritual support (the H4 + H11 layer of recovery)

### 3.5 ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS (mental health context)

1. ❌ Predicting suicide in any form
2. ❌ Naming a date for "the dark period"
3. ❌ Telling a client they have a suicide-risk chart structure

## 4. TREATMENT RESPONSE PREDICTION

KP can identify windows where mental-health treatment (medication,
therapy, hospitalization) is structurally supported:

### Medication response
- **Jupiter favorable** in current AD = wisdom-based intervention helps
  (typical SSRI response pattern)
- **Mercury favorable** = analytical/CBT interventions land
- **Saturn favorable** (paradoxically) = mood-stabilizer adherence
  improves; structured medication routines stick
- **Moon transiting favorably** over Jupiter/Mercury = day-to-day
  treatment response visible

### Therapy response
- **Mercury + Jupiter favorable** = insight-based therapy (psychodynamic,
  CBT) effective
- **Venus favorable** = relational/family therapy effective
- **H5 favorable** = creative therapies (art, music, dance therapy)
- **H8 + Jupiter** = depth therapy / trauma work (longer process)

### When treatment isn't landing
- Saturn-Saturn parent dasha + Mars+Saturn afflicting Moon = the
  natural treatment-response window hasn't opened yet
- Recommend: stay with treatment, the dasha will shift in [specific
  upcoming window]
- Do NOT recommend stopping treatment under any circumstances

### Hospitalization (psychiatric)
- H12 activation + H6 affliction + Moon-Saturn = inpatient psychiatric
  care structurally supported
- This is a NEUTRAL signal — inpatient care is often life-saving
- Frame as appropriate level of care, not as failure or shame

## 5. RECOVERY DOCTRINE

Recovery from mental illness in KP framework:

### Structural recovery indicators
- **Strong Jupiter** in current AD or upcoming AD (the recovery karaka)
- **H5 sub lord** signifying H1 or H11 (joy returning, fulfillment
  reachable)
- **H4 sub lord** favorable (inner peace rebuilding)
- **H11 sub lord** signifying H1 or H5 (community + fulfillment)
- **Moon transit** favorable on Jupiter, Sun, or H1

### Recovery timing windows
Scan upcoming dashas for:
- Jupiter activation in MD or AD
- Mercury favorable (analytical clarity returns)
- H5 favorable (joy + creative outlet returns)
- H11 favorable (social connection + meaning)

When 2+ of these align in a single AD = strong recovery window.

### Recovery vs management
- **Acute episodes**: typically resolve within current AD if treatment
  is engaged
- **Chronic conditions** (bipolar, schizophrenia, severe OCD): rarely
  "cured" structurally — managed across life. Frame as: "the chart
  shows ongoing management need, with specific windows of greater ease
  and specific windows requiring extra vigilance"
- **Treatment-resistant conditions**: structural marker is severely-
  afflicted Jupiter + multiple malefic-on-Moon patterns. Recommend
  tertiary specialty center + medication trial + family support
  intensification

```
EASES: [specific upcoming AD with calendar dates]. That doesn't
mean recovery automatically arrives — you'll still need the
treatment, therapy, support — but the planetary backdrop becomes
SUPPORTIVE rather than obstructive.

In the meantime, what helps structurally:
- [Specific recommendation per Moon condition]
- [Specific recommendation per H4 condition]
- [Professional support recommended]
- [Lifestyle factors aligned with chart's recovery signals]

You are not broken. The current chapter is hard. The next chapter
is structurally easier. Both chapters are part of a longer story
the chart is writing."
```
