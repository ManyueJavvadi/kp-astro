## Example 1 — Marriage Promise (high-confidence YES)

**Question**: "Will I get married?"

### 1. Direct Verdict
**STRONGLY PROMISED. Engine confidence: 84/100.** Marriage is
promised — H7 CSL Venus is an A-level significator of H11 and a
B-level significator of H7 itself, with HARMONY between Star and Sub
layers.

- H7 CSL = **Venus**, in star of **Moon**, in sub of **Jupiter**.
- **STAR layer** (Moon): occupies H11, owns H4 → signifies {4, 11}.
  H11 is RELEVANT for marriage. Star says: matter is alive.
- **SUB layer** (Jupiter): occupies H2, owns H9, H12 → signifies
  {2, 9, 12}. H2 is RELEVANT, H12 is mild denial (isolation).
- Harmony score: **HARMONY** — both layers point primarily to
  relevant houses (H11 from star, H2 from sub).
- Primary cusp sign: Pisces (dual, fruitful) — supportive sign.

### 3. Fruitful Significators (significator ∩ Ruling Planets)
At query moment, RPs are: Moon, Mercury, Venus, Saturn, Jupiter.
Significators of marriage that are ALSO RP: **Venus (B), Jupiter
(D), Moon (A)**. Three fruitful significators — strong timing
signal.

### 4. Timing Windows (Joint Period Principle)
- Current MD = Venus (the karaka itself, also CSL of H7) — a once-in-a-
  lifetime alignment. MD lord has 1 RP slot.
- Current AD = Jupiter (signifies H2 + H11) — also a fruitful
  significator. AD lord has 1 RP slot.
- **Joint period now**: MD + AD both signify relevant houses AND both
  are in RP. This is the ripest window.

### 5. Pratyantardasha Precision
Within current Jupiter AD, the PAD that activates marriage:
- Moon PAD (Aug 2026 → Jan 2027) — Moon is A-level significator AND
  RP. **This is the marriage window.**
- Saturn PAD (Mar 2027 → Jul 2027) — Saturn delays but does not deny;
  if Moon PAD passes without action, this is the secondary window.

### 6. Pre-answered follow-ups
- "Where will my partner come from?" Venus (CSL) signifies H11 (gain
  from peer/friend circle) — partner likely from professional or
  social network, not family arrangement.
- "Will it be love or arranged?" H5 connection through Star layer
  weak, H7 + H2 strong — leans arranged with mutual liking.
- "Foreign partner?" H12 in sub layer is mild — possible but not
  primary.

### 7. Client summary (plain English)
"Marriage is promised in your chart. The current Jupiter sub-period
within Venus main-period is the ripe window — most likely between
August 2026 and January 2027 when Moon (your fertility/family planet)
becomes active. Your partner is likely to come through your professional

## Format Rules — what every example demonstrates

1. **Verdict tier + confidence in section 1** — never hide the score.
2. **Star-Sub Harmony explicit** — name the layers, list houses from
   each, state the harmony verdict.
3. **A/B/C/D labels cited** — say "A-level significator" not just
   "significator".
4. **Fruitful significators named** — show which sigs are also RP.
5. **Joint Period Principle applied** — MD + AD + PAD all relevant =
   firing window.
6. **KSK strict bhukti rule for CONDITIONAL** — state which bhuktis
   fire vs which block. Do NOT call it "delayed".
7. **Pre-answered follow-ups** — anticipate 2-3 questions the
   astrologer will be asked next.
8. **Client summary in plain language** — no jargon, just the
   astrologer's spoken-word version.

The LLM must produce the same structure for every Analysis-tab
question. Section ordering, depth, and explicit pattern naming are
non-negotiable.
