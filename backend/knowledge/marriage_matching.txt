KP ASTROLOGY — MARRIAGE COMPATIBILITY ANALYSIS
==============================================
Primary analysis system: KP (Krishnamurti Paddhati)
Secondary validation: Ashtakoota (36-gun) traditional scoring

---

FUNDAMENTAL KP PRINCIPLE FOR MARRIAGE MATCHING
===============================================
In KP system, the sub-lord of the 7th house cusp is the key determinant of marriage promise.

Marriage promise exists when:
  - 7th cusp sub-lord signifies houses 2, 7, or 11 (positive)
  - If 7th cusp sub-lord signifies houses 1, 6, or 10 — delay or denial
  - If 7th cusp sub-lord signifies houses 8 or 12 — complications, secret relationships, or loss in marriage

Houses for marriage analysis:
  House 2: Family life, second marriage indicator, family support
  House 7: Primary marriage house — partnership, spouse
  House 11: Fulfillment of desires, gains from marriage, longevity of marriage

Denial houses for marriage:
  House 1: Self-focused, may delay marriage or prefer independence
  House 6: Conflicts, disputes, obstacles in marriage
  House 10: Career over marriage, divorce (as 4th from 7th)
  House 12: Separation, foreign spouse (positive aspect), spiritual detachment

---

STEP-BY-STEP KP COMPATIBILITY ANALYSIS
=======================================

Step 1 — Individual Promise Check
Each chart is analyzed independently first.

For Chart 1 (Person 1):
  - Find sub-lord of 7th cusp
  - Find houses signified by this sub-lord (occupied house + ruled houses + star lord's houses)
  - If 7th sub-lord signifies 2, 7, or 11 → marriage is promised in this chart
  - Note any denial house involvement (1, 6, 10)

For Chart 2 (Person 2):
  - Same analysis as above

If BOTH charts promise marriage → strong compatibility indicator
If one chart denies marriage → the relationship may not lead to marriage

Step 2 — Significator Resonance
Marriage significators for Chart 1:
  - Find all planets signifying houses 2, 7, and 11 in Chart 1
  - These are Chart 1's marriage significators

Marriage significators for Chart 2:
  - Same for Chart 2

Cross-resonance check:
  - Chart 1's marriage significators appearing in Chart 2's Ruling Planets → strong resonance
  - Chart 2's marriage significators appearing in Chart 1's Ruling Planets → strong resonance
  - When significators overlap → the charts are strongly linked for marriage

Ruling Planets (RP) at a given moment:
  1. Day lord
  2. Moon sign lord
  3. Moon star lord
  4. Moon sub-lord
  5. Lagna sign lord
  6. Lagna star lord

Step 3 — Dasha Timing Analysis
Marriage timing in KP: Marriage happens during:
  - Dasha/Bhukti/Antara of planets signifying 2, 7, 11 in the natal chart
  - Moon, Venus, Jupiter dashas are often favorable if they signify marriage houses

Cross-period analysis:
  - Find periods in Chart 1 favorable for marriage (next 5 years)
  - Find periods in Chart 2 favorable for marriage (next 5 years)
  - Overlapping favorable periods = most likely timing for this marriage

Step 4 — Venus Analysis (Key for marriage timing)
  - Venus is the natural significator of marriage and relationships
  - Check Venus in both charts: which houses does Venus signify?
  - Venus signifying 7th house in natal → naturally inclined toward marriage
  - Venus as Dasha lord during favorable transit → marriage timing

Step 5 — 7th Lord Analysis
  - Find the lord of the 7th house sign
  - Find houses signified by this 7th lord
  - Strong 7th lord in 1, 2, 7, 11 = good for marriage
  - 7th lord in 6, 8, 12 = challenges in marriage

---

COMPATIBILITY VERDICT LOGIC
============================

Strong Match indicators:
  1. Both charts promise marriage (7th sub-lord signifies 2/7/11)
  2. High significator resonance (more than 3 common significators)
  3. Overlapping favorable dasha periods in both charts
  4. Ashtakoota score ≥ 25 (out of 36)
  5. No severe doshas (Nadi dosha absent, Kuja dosha absent or cancelled)

Good Match indicators:
  1. Both charts promise marriage
  2. Some significator resonance (1-3 common significators)
  3. Overlapping periods (at least one)
  4. Ashtakoota score 18-24
  5. Mild doshas with cancellations

Caution indicators:
  1. One chart has marriage denial
  2. Low significator resonance
  3. No overlapping favorable periods in near future
  4. Ashtakoota score below 18
  5. Severe doshas without cancellation (especially Nadi dosha or Shadashtak Bhakoota)

7TH HOUSE SUB-LORD CHARACTERISTICS
====================================
What different sub-lords of the 7th cusp indicate:

Sun as 7th sub-lord: Signifies government/authority, proud spouse, may delay marriage
Moon as 7th sub-lord: Emotional, nurturing spouse, early marriage possible
Mars as 7th sub-lord: Active, energetic spouse, potential for conflict, check Kuja dosha
Mercury as 7th sub-lord: Intellectual spouse, good communication, late marriage possible
Jupiter as 7th sub-lord: Wise, traditional spouse, very auspicious for marriage
Venus as 7th sub-lord: Beautiful, artistic spouse, early and happy marriage generally
Saturn as 7th sub-lord: Delay in marriage, mature/older spouse, hardworking
Rahu as 7th sub-lord: Unconventional marriage, foreign element, Rahu's star lord guides further
Ketu as 7th sub-lord: Spiritual element, detachment, Ketu's star lord guides further

---

SECOND MARRIAGE INDICATORS
============================
Look for these when advising on second marriage:
  - 2nd house involvement (2nd house = second marriage partner also)
  - Saturn in 7th or aspecting 7th (delay or separation from first)
  - Multiple planets signifying 7th (can indicate more than one marriage)
  - Sub-lord of 7th having dual significations (7th + 12th or 7th + 6th)
  - Rahu in 7th or as sub-lord of 7th (foreign or unconventional connections)

---

PRACTICAL NOTES FOR KP ASTROLOGER
====================================
1. Always start with individual promise before comparing charts
2. Ashtakoota score gives traditional validation but KP analysis overrides in case of conflict
3. If KP says strong match but Ashtakoota is low — trust KP, suggest remedies for doshas
4. If Ashtakoota is perfect but KP shows no marriage promise — explain marriage is delayed or may not happen
5. Timing is crucial — even matched charts need the right dasha period
6. Always check the current running dasha of both individuals
7. If Nadi dosha present — strongly recommend puja and health checkups
8. If both are Manglik — dosha cancels, this is a good sign
9. For remarriage cases — analyze 2nd house sub-lord as primary significator
10. Venus transit over 7th house or natal 7th significator often triggers marriage events
