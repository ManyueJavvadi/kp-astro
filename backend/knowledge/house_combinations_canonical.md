# Canonical KP House Combinations — Comprehensive Event Reference

**Source**: KSK KP Readers I-VI cross-referenced with the JyotishPortal
KP House Grouping table and the KP-tradition LinkedIn authority article
"Grouping of events in life as per KP astrology" (CA Partha Pratim Mitra).
All entries are KSK canon — no Khullar CIL extension, no Parashari yogas.

**Why this file exists**: Many events look identical at the primary-house
level (e.g., promotion, job loss, retirement, break in service all use
H10 as primary) but have DIFFERENT supporting houses. Conflating them
gives the wrong verdict. A 20-year KSK astrologer doesn't say "career
event coming" — they distinguish promotion vs change vs loss vs retirement
based on which supporting houses are activated. This file is the
distinguishing reference.

**How to use**: When the user asks a question, identify the SPECIFIC
event (not just the topic), look it up here, get the primary + supporting
houses, then run the standard CSL + multi-cusp confirmation analysis
against THAT specific house combination.

## 1. RELATIONSHIP EVENTS

### Marriage (first marriage)
- **Primary**: H7 (spouse, partnership)
- **Supporting**: H2 (family, addition to family), H11 (gain of relationship/fulfillment)
- **House group for CSL test**: {2, 7, 11}
- **Denial check**: H1 (self-only), H6 (separation), H10 (status without partner)
- **Note**: Without H2 connection → relationship without legal marriage
  (e.g., live-in). Without H11 → marriage without fulfillment / arranged-
  forced patterns.

### Second marriage
- **Primary**: H9 (the 3rd from H7 — the partner's elder sibling axis;
  also represents "second venture")
- **Supporting**: H7 (partnership generally), H11 (gain of new relationship)
- **House group for CSL test**: {7, 9, 11}
- **Why H9, not H7**: KSK rule — for a SECOND marriage, count from the
  end of the first marriage. The 3rd from H7 = H9 = "next partnership
  after the first ended". Some practitioners use H2 (= 8th from H7 =
  death/end of first partner) as additional supporting; both are valid.
- **Use**: Only assess this AFTER the first marriage is established as
  ended/separated. Don't mix with "first marriage" analysis.

### Divorce
- **Primary**: H6 (litigation, separation, dispute) OR H7 (the relationship
  itself failing)
- **Supporting**: H1 (self-reclaim), H12 (loss of relationship), H8
  (transformation/end)
- **House group for CSL test**: {1, 6, 7, 12}
- **Note**: Divorce question is DELICATE. If the user asks "will I divorce?",
  also check H7 CSL — if it strongly signifies {2, 7, 11} (marriage promise),
  the marriage is structurally intact and divorce is unlikely. Always
  state both readings.

### Sexual relationship without marriage / live-in
- **Primary**: H7 (intimacy/partnership) without H2 connection
- **Supporting**: H5 (romance, pleasure), H11 (gain of companionship)
- **House group for CSL test**: H7 CSL signifying {5, 7, 11} but NOT H2

## 2. CHILDREN EVENTS

### Childbirth (first child)
- **Primary**: H5 (children, progeny)
- **Supporting**: H2 (family expansion, addition), H11 (gain/fulfillment)
- **House group for CSL test**: {2, 5, 11}
- **Sign overlay (KSK strict)**: BOTH the H5 CSL signifying {2, 5, 11}
  AND the H5 cusp falling in a fruitful sign (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) are
  required for natural conception. Promise + barren sign (Virgo, Leo) =
  IVF / medical assistance likely. See `children_detailed.md`.

### Pregnancy (confirmation event)
- **Primary**: H5 (conception)
- **Supporting**: H2 (life-force entering family), H11 (gain)
- **Same house group as childbirth** but evaluated at CURRENT dasha-bhukti
  level for "is pregnancy happening NOW or imminent".

### Caesarean / surgical delivery
- **Primary**: H5 (childbirth)
- **Supporting**: H2 (life-force protection), H8 (surgery, intervention)
- **Distinguishing feature**: H8 connection = surgery. Without H8 →
  natural delivery. With H8 in the chain → caesarean or surgical assistance
  likely.

### Second child
- **Primary**: H7 (= 3rd from H5, the next-after-first-child axis)
- **Supporting**: H2, H11
- **House group**: {2, 7, 11}

### Third child
- **Primary**: H9 (= 5th from H5 OR 3rd from H7)
- **Supporting**: H2, H11
- **House group**: {2, 9, 11}

### Adoption / IVF
- **Primary**: H5 (children)
- **Supporting**: H8 (intervention, transformation), H11 (gain)
- **Distinguishing**: H8 connection without H2 fully signified → adopted
  or IVF child. H2 + H8 both → IVF biological. See `children_detailed.md`
  for the full framework.

## 3. EDUCATION EVENTS

### Basic education (school level)
- **Primary**: H4 (formal education, foundational learning)
- **Supporting**: H11 (completion, recognition)
- **House group**: {4, 11}

### Higher education (college, university, postgrad)
- **Primary**: H9 (higher learning, philosophy, advanced study)
- **Supporting**: H4 (formal study basis), H11 (degree/completion)
- **House group**: {4, 9, 11}

### Specific stream specialization
| Stream | Primary | Supporting (over and above 4, 11) |
|---|---|---|
| Engineering / Technology | H4 | H10 (technical professional path) |
| Medicine / Healthcare | H4 | H6 (health discipline, service to body) |
| Law / Legal | H4 | H6 (dispute) AND/OR H9 (justice, dharma) |
| Commerce / Business studies | H4 | H2 (wealth/finance), H11 |
| Fine arts / Creative | H4 | H5 (creativity), H3 (expression) |
| Spiritual / Theology | H4 | H9 (dharma), H12 (transcendence) |
| Sciences / Research | H4 | H8 (depth research), H9 |

### Failure / repeat year / dropout
- **Primary**: H4 (formal education)
- **Supporting** (denial-side): H3 (break), H8 (interruption), H10 (status
  without academic completion)
- **House group**: H4 CSL signifying {3, 8, 10} = problems

### Education abroad
- **Primary**: H4 (study) + H9 (higher learning) + H12 (foreign land)
- **House group**: {4, 9, 12} ALL must be signified

## 4. CAREER / EMPLOYMENT EVENTS

### Service / Employment (first job)
- **Primary**: H10 (career, profession)
- **Supporting**: H6 (service relationship), H2 (income), H11 (gain through work)
- **House group**: {2, 6, 10, 11}

### Self-employment / Business
- **Primary**: H10 (career)
- **Supporting**: H7 (partners, clients), H2 (capital/income), H11 (gain)
- **House group**: {2, 7, 10, 11}
- **Distinguishing from service**: H10 CSL signifying H7 instead of H6 →
  business; signifying H6 → service.

### Government job
- **Primary**: H10
- **Supporting**: H6 (service), H1 (authority/self in service)
- **Strong indicator**: Sun and/or Saturn prominently in the H10 CSL
  chain. Sun = government/authority; Saturn = bureaucracy/service.
- **House group**: {1, 6, 10, 11}

### Promotion (within current job)
- **Primary**: H10
- **Supporting**: H2 (raise), H6 (current service preserved), H11 (gain)
- **House group**: {2, 6, 10, 11}
- **Distinguishing from job change**: H10 CSL connection is STABLE;
  signification doesn't shift to NEW houses with the upcoming AD.

### Job change (to new employer, same field)
- **Primary**: H10
- **Supporting**: H6 (new service relationship), H9 (change/transit), H10
- **House group**: {6, 9, 10, 11}
- **Distinguishing**: Upcoming AD lord SHIFTS the CSL connection to NEW
  H10/H6 thread (different planet activates).

### Career field change
- **Primary**: H10
- **Supporting**: H3 (new direction), H9 (change of dharma), H10
- **House group**: {3, 9, 10}
- **Distinguishing from job change**: H3 + H9 BOTH signified = field change,
  not just employer change.

### Job loss
- **Primary**: H10 (career disruption)
- **Supporting**: H5 (separation from current work), H8 (sudden cessation)
- **House group**: H10 CSL signifying {5, 8}
- **Note**: VERY sensitive prediction. State as "stress window for career"
  not "you will lose your job". Apply RULE 28 (anomaly detection) and RULE
  31 (intent-shaped output with remedies).

### Break in service (gap, not termination)
- **Primary**: H10
- **Supporting**: H5 (pause), H9 (change phase)
- **House group**: H10 CSL signifying {5, 9}

### Retirement
- **Primary**: H10 (career conclusion)
- **Supporting**: H5 (rest after work), H9 (next chapter, dharma shift)
- **House group**: H10 CSL signifying {5, 9}
- **Distinguishing from job loss**: Age + dasha-period appropriate; retirement
  bhuktis tend to be Saturn-Jupiter combinations (settling-down planets).

### Fame / public recognition
- **Primary**: H10 (status)
- **Supporting**: H1 (self projected), H11 (multitude/network)
- **House group**: {1, 10, 11}
- **Distinguishing from career**: H1 connection is critical — fame is about
  the SELF being recognized, not just the work being done.

## 5. FINANCIAL EVENTS

### General financial status / wealth accumulation
- **Primary**: H2 (accumulated wealth)
- **Supporting**: H6 (debt repayment, daily flow), H11 (gain/income)
- **House group**: {2, 6, 11}

### Sudden wealth / windfall / inheritance
- **Primary**: H8 (inherited / unearned wealth, transformation)
- **Supporting**: H2 (added to wealth), H11 (gain)
- **House group**: {2, 8, 11}

### Inheritance — cash / ornaments / liquid assets
- **Primary**: H8
- **Supporting**: H2 (movable wealth), H11
- **House group**: {2, 8, 11}

### Inheritance — property / immovable assets
- **Primary**: H8
- **Supporting**: H4 (immovable property), H11
- **House group**: {4, 8, 11}

### Sudden financial loss
- **Primary**: H8 (sudden change of fortune)
- **Supporting**: H5 (gambling/speculation loss), H12 (outflow, loss)
- **House group**: H8 CSL signifying {5, 12} = loss
- **Distinguishing from gain**: H8 CSL signifying {2, 11} = gain;
  signifying {5, 12} = loss.

### Bank loan (acquired)
- **Primary**: H7 (counter-party, the bank as "the other")
- **Supporting**: H6 (debt service), H2 (received funds), H11 (loan
  approved/gain)
- **House group**: {2, 6, 7, 11}
- **Counterintuitive note**: H7 is primary because the LENDER is the
  counter-party. KP reads "borrowed money" via H7 (other person's wealth
  coming to native), distinct from H2 (own wealth).

### Stock market / speculation gains
- **Primary**: H5 (speculation, short-term trading)
- **Supporting**: H2 (received gain), H6 (winning over competition), H11 (gain)
- **House group**: {2, 5, 6, 11} for gains; {5, 8, 12} for losses
- **Saturn-on-H5-CSL-chain rule**: Saturn signifying H5 + H6 + H11 is
  classically considered favorable for sustained stock-market success
  (slow disciplined trading).
- **Long-term investment / value investing**: Read from H12 (long-term
  outflow → asset growth) instead of H5.

### Financial loss / bankruptcy
- **Primary**: H8 (transformation of wealth)
- **Supporting**: H12 (outflow), H6 (debt overwhelming)
- **House group**: H2 CSL signifying {8, 12} = loss of accumulated wealth

- **Primary**: H4 (immovable property, home)
- **Supporting**: H11 (gain through acquisition), H12 (outflow = purchase)
- **House group**: {4, 11, 12}
- **Note**: H12 here is FAVORABLE (outflow = purchase = asset acquired)

### Vehicle purchase
- **Primary**: H4 (vehicles fall under home/possessions)
- **Supporting**: H11 (gain), H12 (outflow)
- **House group**: {4, 11, 12}

- **Primary**: H4 (the asset)
- **Supporting**: H3 (transfer/movement), H5 (parting), H10 (status change)
- **House group**: H4 CSL signifying {3, 5, 10}
- **Distinguishing from purchase**: H11 + H12 thread = purchase; H3 + H10
  thread = sale. Some KP authorities use H10 for "selling" (the act of
  trade); consistency check via H7 (the buyer = the other party).

### Construction / building of new home
- **Primary**: H4
- **Supporting**: H11 (completion), H10 (material work and effort)
- **House group**: {4, 10, 11}

## 8. LEGAL / LITIGATION

### Court case filing (the act of filing)
- **Primary**: H3 (act of communication, document filing)
- **Supporting**: H6 (dispute), H11 (initiation)
- **House group**: {3, 6, 11}

### Court case / litigation success (winning)
- **Primary**: H6 (mastery over enemies/opposition)
- **Supporting**: H1 (self-prevailing), H11 (gain through victory)
- **House group**: {1, 6, 11}
- **Distinguishing from filing**: Filing happens regardless of outcome;
  winning needs the H6 CSL to ALSO signify H1 + H11 in the dasha-bhukti
  of the verdict.

### Settlement (out of court)
- **Primary**: H7 (partnership, agreement with the other party)
- **Supporting**: H6 (dispute resolution), H11
- **House group**: {6, 7, 11}

### Imprisonment / arrest (very sensitive)
- **Primary**: H12 (confinement, isolation)
- **Supporting**: H3 (legal documentation), H8 (severe restriction)
- **House group**: H12 CSL signifying {3, 8}
- **AI policy**: NEVER predict specific imprisonment dates. State as
  "challenging legal/freedom-restriction window — recommend legal
  counsel during this period." Apply RULE 15 (sensitive predictions
  protocol).

### Theft / loss to dishonesty (counterintuitive — read from H7)
- **Primary**: H7 (the "other person" who took)
- **Supporting**: H2 (loss of possession), H12 (loss/outflow)
- **House group**: H7 CSL signifying {2, 12} = theft event possible
- **Note**: Counterintuitive because we usually associate H7 with
  marriage. In KP, H7 = "the other" — partner OR adversary OR the person
  who took something.

## 9. HEALTH

### General good health
- **Primary**: H1 (self/body/vitality)
- **Supporting**: H11 (improvement, gain of strength)
- **House group**: {1, 11}

### Disease / illness (general)
- **Primary**: H6 (disease, daily affliction)
- **Supporting**: house corresponding to organ system involved (see
  `health_detailed.md` for organ-house mapping)
- **House group**: H6 CSL + organ-house

### Surgery (medical procedure)
- **Primary**: H8 (surgical intervention, life-altering procedure)
- **Supporting**: H2 (life-force, recovery), H11 (gain through procedure)
- **House group**: {2, 8, 11}
- **Distinguishing from disease**: H6 → disease itself; H8 → the
  intervention/operation.

### Recovery from illness
- **Primary**: H1 (self regaining strength) + H6 (disease resolution)
- **Supporting**: H5 (improvement), H11 (gain of strength)
- **House group**: H1 CSL signifying {1, 5, 11} = recovery.
- **Conversely**: H1 CSL signifying {6, 8, 12} alone = continued affliction.

### Chronic illness
- **Primary**: H6 (disease)
- **Supporting**: H8 (long-term struggle), H12 (hospitalization risk)
- **Indicator**: Saturn prominent in H6 CSL chain → chronic, slow-healing.
  Mars prominent → acute, fast-onset/fast-resolution.

### Mental health concerns
- **Primary**: H1 (self/mind)
- **Supporting**: H4 (emotional core), H8 (deep psyche), H12 (subconscious)
- **Indicator planets**: Moon afflicted by Saturn (chronic depression),
  Mercury-Saturn (anxiety), Mars-Mercury (impulsivity)
- **AI policy**: Always recommend professional mental health support FIRST,
  astrology second. See `health.txt` for detailed framing. Never claim
  astrology can substitute for mental health care.

### Accident / sudden injury (very sensitive)
- **Primary**: H1 (body/self affected)
- **Supporting**: H8 (sudden event), H12 (loss/hospitalization)
- **House group**: H1 CSL signifying {8, 12}
- **AI policy**: Predict as "stress window — extra caution recommended" not
  "you will have an accident". Apply RULE 15.

## 11. SENSITIVE EVENTS — POLICY (recap from RULE 15)

These events require EXTRA CARE in framing. Never deterministically predict
them. Always:
- Frame as "challenging window / extra caution" not "this WILL happen"
- Recommend professional consultation (medical, legal, mental health)
  appropriate to the topic
- Cite the structural pattern but NOT as fate-claim

Sensitive event list:
- Death / longevity (NEVER predict — RULE 15 absolute)
- Accident / unnatural events
- Imprisonment / legal trouble
- Severe illness / surgery / chronic disease
- Mental health crises
- Miscarriage / abortion (extreme sensitivity)
- Marital breakdown / divorce
- Job loss / financial ruin

For ALL of these, the house combination READING is just a structural
read of the chart. The OUTPUT must frame it as a window of caution,
not a deterministic prediction.

## 13. How NOT to Use This Reference

1. **Don't memorize all combinations as a checklist**. Use this when the
   user's question is SPECIFIC. For general questions, default to KSK
   primary house only.

2. **Don't treat unfavorable house signification as automatic denial**.
   "H10 CSL signifying H8" doesn't mean job-loss-guaranteed. It's ONE
   structural signal that combines with dasha, RP, transit, multi-cusp
   confirmation for a verdict.

3. **Don't predict sensitive events deterministically**. Even a TIER 3
   structural-promise of (e.g.) imprisonment doesn't mean you predict
   imprisonment. Frame as "stress window for legal/freedom matters".

4. **Don't confuse different events that share the primary house**.
   Promotion, job change, job loss, retirement all use H10 primary.
   The supporting houses differentiate them. Always check supporting
   houses before giving the verdict.

5. **Don't apply this without `kp_multi_cusp_confirmation.md`**. The
   house combination tells you WHICH cusps to evaluate. Multi-cusp
   confirmation tells you HOW STRONG the agreement is. Both are needed
   for accurate prediction.
