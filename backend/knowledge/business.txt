SECTION 1: HOUSES FOR BUSINESS (KP CANONICAL)
================================================================================

PRIMARY GATE: H10 (profession / career / status)
  Per KP Astrology Learning H10 cusp page:
    "If H10 sub lord signifies 7 → profession is BUSINESS."
    "If H10 sub lord signifies 6 → profession is SERVICE (job)."
    "Dual sign + both H6 and H7 → both service and business."

CRITICAL DISAMBIGUATION (vs job):
  - Service / job: H10 sub lord signifies H6 (service house)
  - Business: H10 sub lord signifies H7 (public dealings / partnerships)
  - The DIFFERENTIATOR is whether H6 or H7 fires from H10.

SUPPORTING HOUSES:
  H7 = partners, public dealings, customers
  H11 = profits, fulfillment of business desire
  H2 = accumulated business wealth (vs H10 which is the source)

DENIAL HOUSES (12th-from-relevant logic):
  H1  = 12th from H2 (loss of business wealth)
  H6  = 12th from H7 (loss of partners / customers — confusingly H6 is good
        for SERVICE jobs but bad for BUSINESS partnerships)
  H9  = 12th from H10 (loss of profession / status)
  H12 = universal loss (expenditure / hidden / abroad-hidden-money)

WHEN H10 CSL SIGNIFIES PRIMARY DENIAL SET ({1, 6, 9, 12}) AND HAS NO RELEVANT TOUCH:
  → Business venture DENIED / fails to launch.

WHEN H10 CSL SIGNIFIES BOTH RELEVANT AND DENIAL HOUSES:
  → CONDITIONAL — apply KSK strict bhukti rule:
    - Bhuktis of relevant-house significators → business opens / profits arrive
    - Bhuktis of denial-house significators → business fails / partnership dissolves

SECTION 2: STARTING A NEW VENTURE
================================================================================

Required for business launch:
  H10 + H7 + H11 + H2 (profession + public + profits + wealth)
  ALL FOUR must connect in the activating dasha
  H3 also relevant (boldness/effort/courage to act)

Per KP Astrology Learning H7:
  "If the 7th house is stronger than the 6th, advisors counsel
   clients to start their own venture."

TIMING TRIGGER (joint period for business launch):
  Look for an AD lord (and ideally PAD lord) that signifies H10+H7+H11
  simultaneously. The launch fires in that window.

PER PLANET FOR BUSINESS LAUNCH:
  Mars dasha → trades, manufacturing, real estate, defense
  Mercury dasha → trading, commerce, IT, consulting, agencies
  Jupiter dasha → finance, banking, education, legal services
  Venus dasha → luxury, entertainment, fashion, hospitality
  Saturn dasha → mining, heavy industry, agriculture, structured services
  Rahu dasha → unconventional, foreign-tied, technology-driven, MNC partnerships
  Sun dasha → government-tied, authority-driven, leadership ventures
  Moon dasha → public-facing, hospitality, retail, customer-emotion-driven

SECTION 3: PARTNERSHIP ANALYSIS (KP READER IV — DEFINITIVE)
================================================================================

H7 cusp sub lord is the PRIMARY gate for partnership outcome.
Per KP Astrology Learning H7 (and KSK Reader IV verbatim):

PARTNERSHIP RULES (H7 cusp sub lord):

(a) LASTING BUSINESS PARTNERSHIP:
    "7th cusp's sub lord, when in a planet's constellation representing
     6 and 11" → lasting partnership.

(b) PARTNERSHIP DISSOLUTION:
    "Signifying 6 and 12 → the partnership ends."
    This is the structural dissolution signal — flag explicitly.

(c) STRONG LASTING BOND:
    "Indicating 5 and 11 ensures a lasting bond."
    H5 (heart connection) + H11 (mutual gain) = bond endures.

(d) ⚠ PARTNER PROFITS, YOU LOSE (CRITICAL — partner fraud signal):
    "Signifying 5, 8, and 12 → benefits the partner but causes your loss."
    This is the KP doctrinal signature for partner cheating you out of
    money. When this fires, the partner GAINS while you LOSE — even
    without overt fraud, the structure is wealth flowing partner-ward
    and loss flowing native-ward. Cross-link to money_recovery.md.

(e) MULTIPLE PARTNERS / SCALE:
    "Mercury sub lord indicates multiple partners, with 11 signifying
     a strong bond."
    Mercury rules partnerships in dual signs; with H11 = scalable.

(f) HARMONIOUS PARTNERSHIP:
    "Venus connection promising harmony."
    Venus involvement in H7 chain = smooth interpersonal dynamics.

PARTNERSHIP COMPATIBILITY (cross-chart — for partner selection):
When choosing a business partner, examine BOTH charts:
  - Each chart's H7 sub lord signification (should hit 6+11 or 5+11)
  - Cross-resonance: native's H10 lord = partner's H11 lord (or similar
    cross-overlap) → mutually beneficial structural compatibility
  - Check D9 (Navamsa) for the partnership's resilience

KSK READER IV CROSS-RULE (stricter — when applicable):
"H7 Cusp Sign/Star/Sub of one partner should ideally appear in the
Ruling Planets of the other partner at the moment of partnership."
When 3-of-3 match: EXCEPTIONAL business compatibility (rare).
When 2-of-3: STRONG compatibility.
When 1-of-3: ORDINARY (typical business arrangement).

SECTION 4: PARTNER CHEATING / FRAUD RECOVERY (COMPOUND READING)
================================================================================

This is the 4-house compound question — pure money_recovery.md alone
is insufficient because the PARTNER is involved as the perpetrator.

COMPOUND READING required (cite all four for completeness):

(1) H7 CSL — Partner intent / structural signal:
    Does H7 sub lord signify {5, 8, 12}? → Per rule (d) above, structural
    "partner profits you lose" signal is firing. The cheating is
    CHART-EMBEDDED, not just a personality failure of the partner.

(2) H6 CSL — Recovery / dispute mechanism:
    Per KP Astrology Learning H6: "H6 sub lord signifying 2, 6, 11
    excluding Saturn → entangled money returned."
    - If H6 CSL signifies the recovery trinity {2,6,11} cleanly without
      Saturn: recovery probable
    - If Saturn is in chain: recovery delayed / difficult
    - If H6 CSL signifies {6, 12}: legal expense (litigation costs
      will be high)

(3) H8 CSL — Loss recovery / sudden gain channel:
    Per KP Astrology Learning H8: "If H8 sub lord signifies 2, 10, or 11
    → the borrowed money is returned."
    - H8 CSL touching H2/H11 = lost money structurally returnable
    - H8 CSL signifying {6, 11} = lender's loss (the partner loses too,
      mutual destruction outcome)

(4) H2 CSL — The native's own wealth resilience:
    Does H2 sub lord signify {2, 6, 11}? → Native's wealth gate is open,
    the loss is RECOVERABLE structurally even if not all from this partner.
    If H2 sub lord signifies {1, 8, 12} primarily → loss is part of a
    broader wealth-denial period; recovery via THIS channel less likely.

DASHA TRIGGER (when does recovery fire):
  Scan upcoming ADs for one that signifies {2, 6, 11} simultaneously
  and is NOT a Saturn-dominated AD. That window is the structural
  recovery point.
  Cross-check with Ruling Planets at the moment the question was asked
  (RP overlap with H6/H11 significators confirms timing).

OUTPUT FRAMING (Tier 2 question — apply RULE 52):
  - Show both branches:
    BRANCH A (recovery): conditions for full or partial return
    BRANCH B (no recovery): conditions for permanent loss
  - Cite specific calendar windows for each branch's firing
  - Give falsifiable date: "If by [DATE] no recovery action has fired,
    audit the reading"
  - Do NOT predict the partner's behavior; predict the chart structure
  - Recommend: legal counsel for the legal mechanism, KP for the timing

================================================================================

⚠ BANKRUPTCY QUESTIONS = Tier 2-3 escalation per sensitivity_tiers.md
  Apply maximum protective framing. Mention financial counselor
  recommendation. Do NOT predict specific bankruptcy date.

STRUCTURAL BANKRUPTCY SIGNALS:
  H10 sub lord signifying primary denial {1, 6, 9, 12} AND
    H2 sub lord signifying {1, 8, 12} AND
    H8 (sudden loss / debt obligation) heavily activated
  → Structural risk of shutdown / bankruptcy in the firing dasha

VOLUNTARY EXIT vs FORCED SHUTDOWN:
  Voluntary exit: H10 sub lord + H4 (settling down) + H12 (winding up)
  Forced shutdown: H10 sub lord + H8 (sudden) + H12 + opponent (H7)
    or court (H6+H7) involvement

BUSINESS LOSS RECOVERY (different from bankruptcy):
  Even if H10 + H11 in current AD is unfavorable, look at next 2-3 ADs.
  If a future AD has H10 + H11 + H2 alignment → business recoverable.
  Cite that window as the structural recovery period.

SECTION 11: PUSHBACK DISCIPLINE FOR BUSINESS QUESTIONS
================================================================================

If user pushes back ("my CA said I should start trading instead of
consulting"):
  Re-run the H10 + H7 + H11 chain analysis. Check which business type
  aligns with the planetary combinations.
  Cite which signals support which path. KP identifies STRUCTURAL FIT
  for the business type, not the only viable path.

If user says "but I'm already losing money in this business":
  This may be the structural-loss period (Section 7). Scan upcoming
  ADs for the H10+H11+H2 recovery window. If a recovery window exists
  in the next 2-3 years, cite it explicitly with calendar dates.
  Recommend FINANCIAL COUNSELOR consultation alongside KP reading.

If user says "should I trust my partner with the next deal":
  This is a Section 3 partnership question + possibly Section 4 cheating
  signal check. Cite the H7 CSL signification verbatim. If {5, 8, 12}
  fires, flag the structural risk explicitly.
