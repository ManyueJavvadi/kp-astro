## 1. THE PREGNANCY FRAMEWORK

### Primary houses

- **H5** — children, conception, pregnancy progress (the primary house)
- **H8** — sudden / surgical events in pregnancy (C-section, complications)
- **H11** — fulfillment of pregnancy (live birth)
- **H1** — mother's body (her vitality through pregnancy)
- **H2** — family (extension via birth)
- **H4** — comfort / home / safe delivery
- **H12** — confinement (hospital delivery, post-delivery rest)

### Primary planets

- **Jupiter** — karaka of children + healthy pregnancy + grace
- **Moon** — fluids, hormones, maternal body, emotional state
- **Mars** — energy / inflammation / surgical intervention
- **Venus** — reproductive system, female karaka, hormonal balance
- **Saturn** — chronic conditions, delays, structural difficulty

### Karaka condition for pregnancy

**Jupiter strength is paramount.** A weak / afflicted Jupiter
weakens pregnancy protection:
- Jupiter debilitated (Capricorn) → hormonal imbalances / delays
- Jupiter combust → karaka strength reduced
- Jupiter in H6 / H8 / H12 → karaka in disease/transformation/
  loss houses
- Jupiter retrograde → developmental complications possible

## 2. MISCARRIAGE RISK SIGNATURES

### Per canonical KP + Vedic doctrine:

**The classical miscarriage stack**:
1. H5 lord afflicted
2. H5 lord debilitated AND in 6/8/12 OR in Rahu-Ketu axis
3. Jupiter (karaka) afflicted (combust / debilitated / hemmed by
   malefics / aspected by Saturn-Rahu-Mars)
4. Mars + Saturn conjunction on H5 or H5 lord
5. Multiple malefics aspecting H5

### Per Bejan Daruwalla doctrine (verbatim):
> "Malefic planets like Ketu, Mars, and Sun influence over the 5th
> house can give miscarriage."
> "Multiple miscarriages mean the 5th house and its lord, Karak Jupiter
> is badly afflicted in the D1 chart or D9 chart."
> "Mars-Saturn conjunction may lead to abrupt complications during the
> second or third trimester."
> "Debilitated or afflicted Jupiter can lead to miscarriage, hormonal
> imbalances, or delayed conception."

### KP-specific miscarriage indicators

- H5 sub lord signifying H8 (sudden loss) + H12 (hospitalization)
- H5 sub lord WITHOUT touch to H2 / H11 (no anchoring in family-fulfillment)
- Current AD lord = malefic on H5 chain
- Pattern D2 signal on H5 (engagement-then-withdrawal — pregnancy
  conceives but doesn't sustain)

### Trimester-specific risk windows

| Trimester | Common KP risk pattern |
|---|---|
| First (1-12 wks) | H5 + Ketu (early loss, often unexplained) |
| Second (13-27 wks) | Mars-Saturn affliction (most cited for mid-pregnancy abrupt complications) |
| Third (28-40 wks) | H8 activation + H12 (delivery-anticipation complications, premature labor) |

## 3. HIGH-RISK PREGNANCY SIGNATURES

### When H8 is affliction-active

> "Affliction to the 8th House may cause surgical birth (C-section),
> high-risk pregnancy, or delay in delivery."

H8 affliction during pregnancy signals:
- C-section likelihood (planned or emergency)
- Gestational complications requiring monitoring
- Possible NICU stay for newborn
- Delivery date may shift from EDD

### High-risk indicators

- H8 sub lord signifies H5 + H6 simultaneously
- H8 + H12 stack in mother's current AD
- Saturn or Mars dominant in pregnancy-period dasha
- Moon afflicted heavily (hormonal/blood pressure issues)
- H1 sub lord weak in pregnancy-period (mother's vitality stressed)

### Specific high-risk patterns

- **Gestational diabetes**: Jupiter in earth sign + Saturn affliction +
  H2 (food/throat) activation
- **Preeclampsia / hypertension**: Mars + Sun afflictions + H1 stressed +
  Moon-Saturn afflicted
- **Placenta previa / abruption**: H5 + H8 + Mars sudden activation
- **IUGR (intrauterine growth restriction)**: Saturn dominant + Jupiter
  weak + H5 in barren sign

### Sensitivity tier

High-risk pregnancy = TIER 2 default; escalate to TIER 3 if:
- Doctor has indicated mother's life at risk
- "Will I survive this pregnancy" framing
- Both mother + baby at structural risk simultaneously

## 4. DELIVERY TIMING

### Normal-delivery signature

- H5 sub lord active in delivery-month PAD
- Moon transit favorable on H5 or H1
- Jupiter favorable aspect = smooth delivery + healthy baby
- H11 (fulfillment) activating = "the baby arrives"

### C-section / surgical delivery

- H8 sub lord active in delivery-period
- Mars favorable (clean surgical intervention) — paradoxically positive
  if surgery indicated
- Mercury favorable (good medical team / documentation)
- Per doctrine: H8 affliction → planned or emergency C-section

### Delivery timing scan procedure

Step 1: Identify the expected due date (EDD)
Step 2: Compute mother's MD/AD/PAD for EDD ±2 weeks
Step 3: Find the PAD where H5 + H11 + (H8 if surgical) align
Step 4: That PAD = the structural delivery window
Step 5: Most-likely delivery date = the day within that PAD when
        Moon transits the natal H5 or H1 OR the EDD itself if those
        coincide

### Delivery character by dominant planet

| Planet | Delivery character |
|---|---|
| Moon | Smooth, water-flowing (literal: water breaks naturally) |
| Mars | Quick, intense, possible C-section if afflicted |
| Mercury | Multiple medical interventions; talkative-active labor |
| Jupiter | Easy, expansive, healthy baby + favorable prognosis |
| Venus | Comfortable delivery setup, beautiful birth experience |
| Saturn | Long labor, structured medical environment |
| Sun | Authority-supported (senior doctor), high-profile setup |
| Rahu | Unconventional (water birth, home birth, foreign hospital) |
| Ketu | Sudden — labor starts unexpectedly, fast progression |

## 6. SPECIFIC SCENARIOS

### "I had a miscarriage. Will I be able to have a successful pregnancy?"

**Approach**:
1. Tier 2 NOTE (Tier 3 if compound grief language)
2. Acknowledge loss compassionately
3. Validate that miscarriage was not the mother's fault
4. Re-read H5 chain in CURRENT chart (not the chart of when miscarriage
   happened — that period has passed)
5. Identify the next favorable conception window (H5 + Jupiter + H11
   alignment in upcoming AD)
6. Recommend: medical follow-up (rule out treatable causes) +
   grief support if needed
7. Close on hope branch + structural support for next pregnancy

### "Doctor says I have high-risk pregnancy — will it be OK?"

**Approach**:
1. Tier 2 NOTE
2. Read H8 affliction pattern (validates medical concern)
3. Read H5 + H11 chain (favorable counter-signals)
4. Identify the delivery window structural support
5. Recommend: high-risk obstetrician + maternal-fetal medicine
   consultation + bed rest if indicated + family support
6. Both branches articulated (favorable + adverse)
7. Close on hope + medical agency

### "I'm pregnant again after losing my last baby — am I safe?"

**Approach**:
1. Tier 2-3 NOTE (high emotional weight from previous loss)
2. Acknowledge the courage of trying again
3. Re-read CURRENT chart H5 chain (not historical pattern)
4. Identify the protection factors in current period
5. Recommend: closer medical monitoring + perinatal support
6. Avoid trauma-anchoring on previous loss
7. Close on the structural support for THIS pregnancy

### "When will I deliver?"

**Approach**:
1. Standard Tier 2
2. Identify EDD ±2 weeks window
3. Read PAD-level for that window
4. Cite most-likely date with ±3-day band
5. Note that medical factors can shift actual delivery
6. Recommend: birth-plan flexibility
