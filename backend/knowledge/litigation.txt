SECTION 1: HOUSES FOR LITIGATION (KP CANONICAL)
================================================================================

PRIMARY GATE: H6 (disputes, enemies, litigation, legal disputes)

SUPPORTING HOUSES:
  H1 = self (native's case strength)
  H11 = winning, gains from litigation (the prize / favorable verdict)
  H3 = courage to file / pursue / appeal

OPPONENT HOUSES:
  H7 = opponent (other party in the case)
  H12 = loss to native = gain to opponent

WIN HOUSES SET: {6, 11} (primary) + H1, H3 (supporting)
DENIAL / LOSS HOUSES SET: {7, 8, 12}

THE PRIMARY GATE RULE (per KP Astrology Learning H6, verbatim):
  "A 6th cusp's sub lord signifying 1, 6, and 11 promises litigation success."

THE SECONDARY GATES:
  "H2 sub lord signifying H6 and H11 → success in litigation (or
   promotion in service)."
  "H3 sub lord deposited in star of significators of H6 and H11 →
   success in appeal/petition/courts."

LOSS GATE:
  "If dasha activates significators of H12 (loss) and H7 (opponent
   wins) = loss in case."
  "When H7 is strongly activated in dasha = opponent's period;
   native's case weakens."

SECTION 2: CIVIL vs CRIMINAL — KP DISTINCTION (CRITICAL)
================================================================================

KP doctrine treats civil and criminal cases with different intensity
and different supporting cusps:

CIVIL CASE (property / contract / family / financial):
  Primary: H6 + H11 (standard litigation framework, Section 1)
  Supporting: H2 (if money is at stake), H4 (if property),
              H8 (if inheritance), H7 (always — the other party)
  Sensitivity: TIER 2
  Outcomes: win / lose / settle / withdraw / out-of-court

CRIMINAL CASE (criminal liability / FIR / arrest / trial):
  Primary: H6 + H11 (win = acquittal)
  Plus CRITICAL: H12 (imprisonment) + H8 (sudden loss of freedom)
  Sensitivity: TIER 3 (life-impact = jail risk = liberty deprivation)
  Outcomes: acquittal / conviction / bail / jail / settlement (rare)

KEY DIFFERENCE FOR CRIMINAL:
  In criminal, the cost of LOSING is liberty (jail) — orders of
  magnitude more serious than civil. The KP doctrine adds H12 +
  Rahu involvement check (Section 5). Tier 3 framing is MANDATORY.

SECTION 3: PLAINTIFF vs DEFENDANT — ROLE-DEPENDENT READING
================================================================================

In KP, the CSL chains read slightly differently depending on whether
the native is the one suing (plaintiff) or being sued (defendant):

PLAINTIFF (you initiated the case — suing someone):
  - H6 is YOUR weapon (your dispute / your case)
  - H7 is your OPPONENT (defendant)
  - H11 = your gain from winning
  - H1 + H3 strong = you have the energy and standing to pursue
  - Standard "win" doctrine (Section 1) applies cleanly

DEFENDANT (you are being sued — responding to a case):
  - H1 is YOUR defense (your self / self-protection)
  - H7 is the PLAINTIFF (the one suing you)
  - H11 = you successfully defending (their case fails)
  - H12 = YOUR loss / having to pay damages / conviction
  - REVERSAL of cusps: H7 strong = plaintiff strong = bad for you
  - Win = your H1 strongly signified AND H7 (plaintiff) blocked

CROSS-CHART CONSIDERATION (when opponent's chart is available):
  If you have opponent's birth data:
  - Compare your H6 strength to their H6 strength
  - Whichever native's H6 CSL more strongly signifies {6, 11} → that
    party prevails
  - Whichever native is in a stronger AD for H6 + H11 = that side
    has the upper hand in current period

  - Sub Lord in common/dual sign → unpredictable timing

SECTION 5: LOSS / OPPONENT WINS / JAIL RISK (TIER 3 — MAXIMUM CARE)
================================================================================

⚠ TIER 3 SECTION — apply absolute protective framing per RULE 52.

STRUCTURAL LOSS:
  H7 sub lord signifies H6 + H11 from the opponent's side
  (i.e., opponent's cusps fire while yours don't)
  Or: native's H6 + H11 are denied in current dasha while H7 + H12
  are activated

DASHA-DRIVEN LOSS:
  "If dasha activates significators of H12 (loss) and H7 (opponent
   wins) = loss in case." (KP Astrology Learning H6)

JAIL / IMPRISONMENT RISK (CRIMINAL CASES ONLY):

KP doctrine on imprisonment (per Vaastuinternational + Dhirendranath
Misra KP research + AstroSage):

  "If the sub lord of the 12th cusp is Rahu, one must go to jail."

  Refined version: H12 cuspal sub lord = Rahu AND H6/H8 affliction
  signals present = high structural risk of imprisonment.

  Other malefics on H12 sub lord (Saturn especially) increase risk
  of prolonged confinement.

  Research note (544 prisoner charts, AstroDatabank study): Rahu
  appears as H12 CSL in significant majority but NOT universally.
  Other malefic sub-lord patterns also indicate imprisonment.
  Apply with caution — never as a certainty.

DOCTRINE ON HOW TO READ IT (MANDATORY framing):

When H12 CSL Rahu pattern fires:
  - State as STRUCTURAL risk, NOT as verdict
  - Frame: "the chart's structural caution windows for adverse legal
    outcomes are dasha-X and dasha-Y"
  - Cite the COUNTER-SIGNALS that mitigate (H1 strength, H6 favorable
    in same dasha, RP overlap with H11)
  - NEVER name a specific incarceration date
  - ALWAYS recommend the lawyer's professional assessment as primary
  - Cite KP's role: "structural orientation, NOT legal advice"

BAIL PROBABILITY:

Bail is the temporary release pending trial. KP doctrine:
  - H11 sub lord signifying H1 + H3 → bail likely (gain of freedom
    via active legal effort)
  - H12 sub lord signifying H1 (self-release) → bail granted
  - H6 sub lord signifying H11 → favorable interim outcome
  - Mercury / Jupiter strong in chain → legal documentation succeeds
  - Saturn in chain → bail delayed even if eventually granted
  - Mars in chain → bail with conditions / aggressive opposition

BAIL TIMING:
  Scan upcoming PADs (week-level precision) within the active AD
  for H1 + H11 alignment. That PAD is the bail-application-success
  window.

SECTION 6: SETTLEMENT vs TRIAL — DECISION SUPPORT (RULE 29)
================================================================================

For "should I settle or go to trial?" questions, apply decision-support:

SETTLEMENT FAVOURABLE STRUCTURE:
  - H6 sub lord signifies H6 + H7 (dispute resolved with opponent)
    without firing H11 (no clean win) → settlement is the realistic path
  - Mercury strong in H6 chain → negotiation succeeds, paperwork
    smooth
  - Venus involvement → amicable terms
  - Both parties' charts (if available) show simultaneous H11 weakness
    = mutual exhaustion → settlement
  - Saturn-heavy dasha for both sides = neither will win cleanly →
    settle to avoid prolonged drain

TRIAL FAVOURABLE STRUCTURE:
  - H6 CSL signifies H6 + H11 cleanly (per Section 4) → trial wins
  - Sun involvement → judge favorable, authority on your side
  - Jupiter in chain → just verdict
  - Mars + Mercury → fast aggressive litigation succeeds
  - H3 + H6 + H11 lineup → appeals will also succeed if first
    judgment goes against

WHEN BOTH PATHS WEAK:
  - Chart shows neither clean win nor clean settlement → consider
    withdrawing case (if plaintiff) or seeking out-of-court compromise
  - Cite this candidly: "Per the chart, neither path is structurally
    promised. The most resource-efficient option is [withdrawal /
    out-of-court]. Discuss with your lawyer."

SECTION 9: VERDICT TIMING WINDOW SCANNING
================================================================================

For "when will the verdict come?" questions, apply the structured scan:

STEP 1: Identify all planets signifying H6 (or H7 if you're defendant)
        in the chart's significator table.

STEP 2: List the next 24 months of ADs / PADs / Sookshmas.

STEP 3: Find the first AD where:
        - AD lord signifies H6 (verdict house)
        - PAD lord signifies H11 (favorable) OR H12 (unfavorable)

STEP 4: That AD-PAD intersection = the verdict timing window.

STEP 5: The verdict POLARITY depends on whether the firing planet
        signifies H11 (win) or H12 (loss). State both branches.

STEP 6: Cross-check with Ruling Planets at moment of asking:
        If RP includes the firing planet AND H11 significators →
        the favorable branch fires.

OUTPUT FRAMING:
  "Per the chart, the structural verdict window is [AD-PAD range].
   Within that window:
   - If H11 significators dominate (currently RP-supported): favorable
   - If H12 significators dominate: unfavorable

   Final outcome depends on legal strategy + evidence + trial dynamics
   — KP gives the structural window, not the human-decided outcome."

SECTION 10: PLANETARY MODIFIERS (TIMING CHARACTER)
================================================================================

When these planets dominate the H6 chain, expect:

Saturn:
  - Lengthy legal battles (multi-year)
  - Procedural delays, postponements
  - Heavy filing / documentation work
  - Eventual outcome but with much friction
  - Often appeals to higher courts

Mars:
  - Aggressive, fast-moving litigation
  - Confrontational legal style
  - Quick decisions (for or against)
  - Risk of escalation / counter-suits
  - Physical / violent dispute origin (if criminal)

Mercury:
  - Documentation-heavy
  - Negotiation succeeds
  - Settlements likely
  - Written proceedings dominate
  - Articulate legal argumentation

Jupiter:
  - Justice prevails (favorable when on your side)
  - Honest judgment, fair-minded judge
  - Religious / dharmic angle if applicable
  - Senior counsel / wise lawyer
  - High court favorable

Venus:
  - Family-related disputes
  - Amicable resolution preferred
  - Mediation succeeds
  - Romantic / spouse-related cases
  - Comfortable settlements

Sun:
  - Government-tied cases
  - Authority-driven outcome
  - Public-eye attention
  - Powerful adversary if Sun is against you
  - Father-related inheritance cases

Moon:
  - Public sentiment matters (jury / media)
  - Emotional / fluctuating case dynamics
  - Mother-related disputes
  - Family-court cases

Rahu:
  - International / foreign-jurisdiction angle
  - Unconventional case (cyber-crime, novel charge)
  - Hidden / underground elements
  - JAIL RISK if H12 CSL (Section 5)

Ketu:
  - Spiritual / occult angle
  - Sudden termination (case dismissed)
  - Past-life-karma reading appropriate
  - Headless wandering of case (no clean resolution)

SECTION 11: PROTECTIVE FRAMING (CRITICAL — TIER 2 + TIER 3 ABSOLUTE RULES)
================================================================================

LITIGATION QUESTIONS ARE TIER 2 BY DEFAULT.
CRIMINAL CASE QUESTIONS ARE TIER 3 BY DEFAULT.
JAIL / BAIL / IMPRISONMENT QUESTIONS ARE TIER 3.
Apply RULE 52 framing throughout.

ABSOLUTE RULES (NEVER VIOLATE):

(1) NEVER name a specific date for:
    - Conviction
    - Acquittal as a guarantee
    - Jail term length
    - Bail rejection

(2) ALWAYS frame as STRUCTURAL, NOT LEGAL OPINION:
    "This is a chart-based structural reading, NOT legal advice. The
     KP doctrine on [H12 cusp sub-lord Rahu involvement / H6 affliction]
     shows [structural verdict]. Your lawyer is the authority on legal
     strategy. This reading can COMPLEMENT — never substitute for —
     their assessment."

(3) ALWAYS show BOTH branches with equal weight (RULE 52 TIER 3):
    "BRANCH A (favorable — if X significators fire): [acquittal /
     win / settlement scenario]
     BRANCH B (adverse — if Y significators fire): [conviction /
     loss / appeal-needed scenario]
     Counter-signals to mitigate each: [list]"

(4) ALWAYS close on the favorable branch — never leave the page on
    a negative outcome alone.

(5) ALWAYS recommend professional consultation:
    "Please discuss this reading with your lawyer. The chart's signals
     inform; they don't replace the people qualified to act on your
     specific case."

(6) ALWAYS acknowledge KP's limits:
    "KP shows structural tendencies. It does not measure: lawyer
     skill, evidence strength, witness reliability, judge's personal
     disposition, opponent's resources, jury sentiment. These all
     interact with the chart's promise."

(7) PUSHBACK DISCIPLINE:
    If user pushes back ("my lawyer says I'll win for sure"):
    Re-run the H6 + H11 chain analysis. Cite which signals support
    which outcome. State openly if your reading and the lawyer's
    assessment diverge; you are STRUCTURAL second opinion, lawyer is
    the actual legal expert with case-specific knowledge.
