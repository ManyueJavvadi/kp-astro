SECTION 1: RELEVANT HOUSES FOR DIVORCE/SEPARATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PRIMARY HOUSE: H6 (disputes, enmity, separation from others, legal conflicts)
SUPPORTING: H10 (public/formal divorce, legal dissolution of marriage)
             H12 (loss of partner, physical separation, bed pleasures denied)

DENIAL/RECONCILIATION HOUSES: H2 (family unity), H7 (partner bond), H11 (fulfillment, continuation)
If dashas activate H2, H7, H11 instead of H6/H10/H12 → reconciliation, not divorce.

KP LOGIC FOR DIVORCE:
"H6 is 12th from H7 = loss of the spouse/partner relationship."
"H10 is 4th from H7 = the foundation of marriage being disrupted."
"H12 is 6th from H7 = disease/dispute within the marriage."
When H6, H10, and H12 are all activated together → strong divorce indication.

SECTION 2: H7 CUSPAL SUB LORD IN DIVORCE CONTEXT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

H7 SUB LORD ANALYSIS:
"If H7 sub lord signifies H6 → disputes/disagreements in marriage."
"If H7 sub lord signifies H10 → formal/public end of marriage."
"If H7 sub lord signifies H12 → physical separation, partner leaves."
"If H7 sub lord signifies H2, H7, H11 → marriage is stable; separation unlikely."

WHEN DIVORCE IS PROMISED (cusp sub lord check):
H7 cusp sub lord in star/sub of planet signifying H6, H10, H12 → divorce is possible.
H7 cusp sub lord signifying H2/H7/H11 → marriage will survive difficulties.

SECTION 3: TIMING OF DIVORCE/SEPARATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For divorce to happen in a specific period:
The MD + AD lord must signify H6, H10, or H12 (at least two of these).
The AD lord must be a significator of the divorce houses (H6/H10/H12).

STRONGEST DIVORCE ADs:
1. AD lord signifies H6 AND H12 (disputes + physical separation)
2. AD lord signifies H6 AND H10 (dispute + public/legal action)
3. AD lord signifies all three H6, H10, H12

WEAKER INDICATIONS (marital discord but not divorce):
- AD lord signifies only H6 → disputes without separation
- AD lord signifies only H12 → temporary separation
- AD lord signifies H8 → major crisis but may not divorce

RECONCILIATION TIMING:
When the following ADs come, reconciliation is possible if chart promises:
- ADs whose lord signifies H2, H7, H11 → reunion/reconciliation periods
- If a subsequent AD brings H2/H7/H11 after the divorce-indicating AD → remarriage or reconciliation

SECTION 4: MARITAL DISCORD WITHOUT DIVORCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DISCORD WITHOUT SEPARATION:
H6 activated alone (without H12) → disputes, arguments, but living together
H8 activated → deep crisis, trauma in marriage, not necessarily separation
H6 + H8 → severe suffering in marriage

TEMPORARY SEPARATION vs PERMANENT DIVORCE:
Temporary: H12 alone (sleep separately, travel apart, brief estrangement)
Permanent: H6 + H10 + H12 all activated together in a major dasha period

SECOND MARRIAGE INDICATION:
H7 sub lord signifying both H7 and H11 (and possibly H6 for the end of first marriage)
→ Multiple partnerships. After divorce (H6/H10/H12 period), second marriage comes
in a subsequent period where H2, H7, H11 are reactivated.

SECTION 6: ANALYSIS STEPS FOR DIVORCE QUESTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1: Check H7 sub lord — does it signify H6, H10, or H12? → divorce possible?
Step 2: Check if H2, H7, H11 override (these deny divorce)
Step 3: Check H6 sub lord for dispute intensity
Step 4: Check H12 sub lord for separation possibility
Step 5: Collect all significators for H6, H10, H12
Step 6: Check current MD + AD significations vs divorce houses
Step 7: Identify if current/upcoming dasha period triggers divorce houses
Step 8: Check for reconciliation periods (H2/H7/H11 ADs after discord)
Step 9: Give timing verdict — when is the peak discord/separation period?
Step 10: Advise on reconciliation potential if H2/H7/H11 are strong
