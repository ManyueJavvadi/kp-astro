================================================================
KP TIMING CONFIRMATION — The 3-Level Certainty Method
================================================================

FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLE
In KP astrology, a predicted event MUST be confirmed at THREE levels:
  Level 1: PROMISE — The CSL of the relevant house signifies the topic houses
  Level 2: TIMING — Current or upcoming Dasha lords are significators
  Level 3: RULING PLANETS — The ruling planets at the time of query confirm

All three must align for the event to manifest. When only 2 align = likely
but delayed. When only 1 aligns = wait for the other two to open.

================================================================
LEVEL 1: PROMISE VERIFICATION (CSL Analysis)
================================================================

Already covered in kp_csl_theory.txt.
The CSL of the relevant house cusp must signify the topic's "yes" houses.

Promise strength:
  STRONG: CSL directly occupies a "yes" house (L1 signification)
  MEDIUM: CSL rules a "yes" house or its star lord connects to "yes" houses
  WEAK: Only sub-lord chain gives the "yes" connection (L3/L4)
  DENIED: No connection to "yes" houses whatsoever

================================================================
LEVEL 2: DASHA TIMING VERIFICATION
================================================================

The Vimsottari Dasha system cascades from large to small time periods:
  Mahadasha (MD) = 6-20 year period (sets the general theme)
  Antardasha (AD) = months to years within MD (activates a sub-theme)
  Pratyantardasha (PAD) = weeks to months within AD (triggers the event)
  Sookshma (SD) = days within PAD (exact timing)

DASHA RELEVANCE RULE:
A dasha lord is RELEVANT for a topic when it signifies the topic's "yes" houses.

Cascade Logic:
  MD lord: Sets the broad decade theme. For the event to happen, MD lord
           must EITHER directly signify the topic OR allow it by not denying.

  AD lord: The actual EVENT happens in an AD whose lord signifies the topic.
           This is the most important period to identify.

  PAD lord: The specific MONTH or WEEK within the AD when the event happens.
            PAD lord must ALSO be a significator of the topic.

KEY RULE: The event happens when MD + AD + PAD lords ALL signify the topic.
  If MD lord doesn't signify → wait for the next MD
  If MD signifies but AD doesn't → wait for the right AD within this MD
  If MD + AD signify but PAD doesn't → wait for the right PAD

SCANNING THE FULL AD SEQUENCE:
Always scan ALL remaining ADs in the current MD, not just the current one.
The event window is the AD period where the AD lord is a topic significator.

Example for marriage (topic houses H2/H7/H11):
  Current MD = Saturn (signifies H7, H11 in this chart → RELEVANT)
  Current AD = Mercury (signifies H3, H6 → NOT relevant for marriage)
  Next AD = Ketu (signifies H7, H2 → RELEVANT) [2026-2027]
  AD after = Venus (signifies H7, H11 → STRONGLY RELEVANT) [2027-2030]

  Prediction: Marriage likely in Venus AD (2027-2030), possibly Ketu AD end (2026).
  Within Venus AD, look for PAD lords that also signify H2/H7/H11.

================================================================
IDENTIFYING THE EXACT PERIOD WITHIN THE AD
================================================================

Within each Antardasha, scan all PAD (Pratyantardasha) periods:

Step 1: List all PAD lords in the relevant AD period
Step 2: Check each PAD lord's significations
Step 3: PAD lords that signify the same topic houses = candidate windows
Step 4: Among those, the one whose running period coincides with the right
        ruling planets = THE TIME OF THE EVENT

PAD timing for marriage example (within Venus AD):
  Moon PAD (2 weeks): Moon in H11, rules H4 → H11 ✓, not denial → CANDIDATE
  Mars PAD (10 days): Mars in H7, rules H7 → H7 ✓ → STRONG CANDIDATE
  Rahu PAD (1 month): Rahu conjoins Venus in H7 → H7 ✓ → CANDIDATE

Prediction refinement: Event most likely in Mars PAD or Rahu PAD within Venus AD.

================================================================
LEVEL 3: RULING PLANET CONFIRMATION
================================================================

Ruling Planets (RPs) at the time of query confirm WHEN the event will happen.
RPs consist of:
  1. Day Lord (lord of the weekday)
  2. Hora Lord (planetary hour at time of query)
  3. Lagna Sign Lord (lord of the rising sign right now)
  4. Lagna Star Lord (lord of the lagna nakshatra)
  5. Moon Sign Lord (lord of Moon's current sign)
  6. Moon Star Lord (lord of Moon's current nakshatra)

THE RULING PLANET CONFIRMATION METHOD:
The event will happen in a dasha period where the ruling planets AT THE TIME
OF THE EVENT match the significant planets for the topic.

In practice for timing:
  If current RPs include planets that signify the topic → event is NEAR (within months)
  If current RPs are mostly denial planets → event is not imminent

Using RPs to pin the EXACT date:
  The event occurs on a day when:
  - The day lord is a topic significator
  - The Moon is in a nakshatra whose lord signifies the topic
  - The hora at the time of event is a significator
  These "triple confirmation" days within the right dasha period = event dates.

SHORTCUT RULE: If 4+ of the 6 ruling planets are significators of the topic
= event is imminent (within current AD or next PAD period)

================================================================
DASHA HIERARCHY AND RESULT TIMING
================================================================

RULE: The stronger the dasha lord's connection to the topic, the earlier
in its period the event manifests.

Strong connection (occupies "yes" house OR rules it directly):
  → Event tends to happen in the FIRST THIRD of that dasha period

Medium connection (via star lord chain):
  → Event in the MIDDLE THIRD of the dasha period

Weak connection (only sub-lord chain):
  → Event in the LAST THIRD, or might not happen in that period

COMPOUND TIMING (when multiple significator ADs are available):
  Pick the AD whose lord has the STRONGEST connection to the topic.
  Strong = L1 (occupies) > L2 (rules) > L3 (star of occupant) > L4 (star of lord)

================================================================
TRANSIT CONFIRMATION (OPTIONAL THIRD LAYER)
================================================================

KP uses transits only to CONFIRM timing already found through dasha analysis.
Transits do NOT independently predict events — they only validate.

TRANSIT SIGNIFICANCE RULE:
A transit is significant ONLY when:
  (a) The transiting planet is the CURRENT MD or AD lord, AND
  (b) It transits over a natal planet that signifies the topic, OR
  (c) It transits through a nakshatra whose lord signifies the topic

DOUBLE TRANSIT THEORY:
When BOTH Jupiter AND Saturn simultaneously transit houses related to the topic
= event of that nature is nearly certain.
  Example: Jupiter in H7 + Saturn in H3 → marriage (H7 = marriage, H3 from H7 = 9th = fortune of marriage partner)
  Example: Jupiter in H2 + Saturn in H11 → significant financial gain

MOON AS TRANSIT TRIGGER:
On the actual event day, the Moon (fastest planet) will be in a nakshatra
whose lord is one of the topic significators.
This is often used to narrow down the exact day within a PAD period.

================================================================
REJECTING FALSE PROMISES
================================================================

A CSL may appear to support the topic but the event still doesn't happen.
Reasons and how to identify:

1. TIMING MISMATCH: CSL promises but no suitable MD/AD is active for years.
   → Correct: "H7 CSL supports marriage but the dasha timing doesn't align
     until Venus AD (2028). Marriage promised but timing is distant."

2. RETROGRADE CSL: Promise is there but with a retrograde CSL, the event
   gets delayed or goes back and forth.
   → Correct: "H7 CSL Jupiter is retrograde. Marriage will eventually happen
     but expect at least one disruption, delay, or second attempt."

3. DENIAL PLANET IN DASHA: Even if CSL promises, if the running MD is a
   pure denial lord (only signifies 1st, 6th, 10th for marriage) → wait.
   → Correct: "CSL promises marriage but current MD lord signifies only H10
     (career). Marriage will wait until a marriage-significator's dasha begins."

4. CSL IN STAR OF DENIAL PLANET: The CSL itself occupies or is in the star
   of a denial planet → the "promise" is actually conditional.
   → Correct: "H7 CSL Venus is in the star of Saturn (H10 lord). Career/work
     obligations may delay marriage. Timing will improve when work pressure eases."

5. BADHAKA OR MARAKA: Certain houses are inherently obstructive.
   Badhaka = H11 lord for movable lagna, H9 lord for fixed, H7 lord for common.
   If the CSL is the badhaka lord = the event comes with complications.

================================================================
PRACTICAL TIMING PREDICTION FORMAT
================================================================

When timing a prediction, always state:
  1. Is the event PROMISED? (CSL verdict)
  2. If promised, WHEN? (first suitable MD+AD+PAD combination)
  3. STRENGTH of that timing (how many significators align)
  4. Any DELAYS or COMPLICATIONS to note (retrograde, denial house involvement)
  5. The RULING PLANET confirmation (do current RPs support imminent timing?)

Sample format:
  "Marriage is PROMISED in this chart (H7 CSL Venus signifies H7, H11).
   The most suitable timing is Venus AD (Jan 2027 – Jan 2030) within the current
   Saturn MD. Within Venus AD, the Mars PAD (March-April 2027) shows strongest
   alignment as Mars signifies H2 and H7. Current ruling planets (Moon star lord
   = Venus, day lord = Venus) confirm this topic is active. Expect marriage
   discussions to intensify in late 2026 leading to an event in early-mid 2027."

================================================================
COMMON TIMING ERRORS TO AVOID
================================================================

❌ "The event will happen in [current MD] because the MD lord signifies the topic"
✓ RIGHT: The MD just sets the background. The actual event happens in a specific AD+PAD.
         Always name the AD and PAD period with exact dates.

❌ "Marriage will happen soon because Venus is transiting H7"
✓ RIGHT: In KP, transit alone is insufficient. Dasha must corroborate.
         Venus transit over H7 only matters if Venus is the current MD or AD lord.

❌ "Saturn dasha = delayed marriage"
✓ RIGHT: Saturn is neither good nor bad — it depends entirely on Saturn's
         significations in THIS specific chart. Saturn ruling H7 and H11 = perfect
         for marriage regardless of Saturn's "slow" nature.

❌ Giving a single year as the prediction without naming the AD+PAD
✓ RIGHT: Always state both the AD period AND the PAD within it for precise timing.
         "Between March 2027 and August 2027 (Mars PAD within Venus AD)"
