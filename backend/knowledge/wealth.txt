RELEVANT HOUSES (KSK STRICT — wealth is 2/6/11, NOT 2/6/10/11):
PRIMARY: H2 (accumulated wealth) + H6 (gains via competitors / loans / service / debt collected) + H11 (gains/profits)
NOTE: H10 is RELEVANT FOR PROFESSION, not for wealth. KSK separates them:
  - 2/6/10 = job/service salary (career)
  - 2/7/10 = business income (career)
  - 2/6/11 = WEALTH ACCUMULATION (this section)
DENIAL: H1 (12th from H2 — loss of wealth), H8 (debt), H12 (loss/expenditure)

H2 CUSP FOR WEALTH (KSK Reader, verbatim):
"H2 sub lord in star of planet in H6 and H11 → very good financial status."
"In houses H2 and H10 → moderate affluence (career-driven, not accumulated wealth)."
"In houses H1 and H3 → just satisfactory financial condition."

The 2/6/11 trinity is the KSK-canonical wealth set:
  - H2 = the wealth itself (savings, capital)
  - H6 = the mechanism by which wealth flows in (debt collection,
        service-based income, gains via overcoming competitors)
  - H11 = realised gain (the wealth has actually arrived)
The IMPROVING houses (1, 2, 3, 6, 10, 11) are a CONTEXTUAL overlay
useful as tiebreaker — they are not the primary verdict.

WEALTH ACCUMULATION vs INCOME:
H2 = saved wealth (bank balance, assets)
H11 = regular income, gains from various sources
H10 = profession as source
H6 = service/salary income

FINANCIAL LOSS:
H12 activated → expenditure
H8 activated → debt or hidden financial issues
H6 and H12 together → cheated by others

GETTING MONEY OWED TO YOU:
"H6 sub lord signifying H2, H6, H11 AND no connection to Saturn → entangled money returned."
"Saturn connected to H6 sub lord → money recovery difficulty."

SPECULATION/INVESTMENTS:
"H5 sub lord signifying H2, H6, H5, H11 → gains in speculation."
"H5 is the house of speculation/stock market/gambling."
Playing cards/betting: H5 sub lord signifying H6 and H11 → wins.

WEALTH TYPE CLASSIFICATION (was PR A1.3-fix-19 in other_topics.txt §9)
================================================================

Real questions need wealth-TYPE distinction:

EARNED WEALTH (self-acquired through career):
  H2 sub lord signifying H6 + H10 + H11 (service + career + gain)
  Strong Mercury or Sun in chain (skill/authority)
  H10 + H11 dasha alignment

INHERITED WEALTH (from parents/family):
  H4 (mother property) or H8 (legacy/transformation) + H2
  H4 sub lord signifying H2 + H8 = mother-side inheritance
  H9 sub lord signifying H2 + H8 = father-side inheritance
  Saturn or Jupiter often involved (long-term family wealth)
  Specific timing: parental dasha (Sun/Moon) + H8 activation

WINDFALL / SPECULATION (lottery, stocks, gambling):
  H5 (speculation) + H8 (sudden) + H11 (gain)
  H5 sub lord signifying H2 + H6 + H11 = profitable speculation
  Rahu involvement often = unexpected windfall
  Jupiter + Mercury + H5 = stock market gains
  CAVEAT: Speculation gains are dasha-specific; don't predict permanent wealth from this alone

REAL ESTATE / PROPERTY-DERIVED WEALTH:
  H4 + H11 + Mars combination
  Property appreciation: H4 sub lord active in long-running dasha
  Selling property for gain: H10 + H11 (10th = sale; 11th = gain)

BUSINESS / SELF-EMPLOYMENT WEALTH:
  H7 + H10 + H11 (partnerships + profession + gain)
  Lord of H10 strong + H7 sub lord active

LOAN / DEBT EXPOSURE:
  H6 + H8 (debt + obstacle)
  H6 sub lord signifying H2 (incoming loan) or H12 (outgoing repayment)
  Saturn affliction = long-term debt
  Mars affliction = aggressive collection / disputes
