RULE 1 — THE PRIMARY MARRIAGE RULE (from KP Reader):
"If the sub lord of the 7th cusp signifies 2, 7 and 11 and with unafflicted Venus,
marriage is PROMISED and fructifies in the conjoined period of the significators of 2, 7 and 11."

RULE 2 — MARRIAGE STOPPED:
"If the sub lord of the 7th cusp is the significator of 1, 6, 10 or 12,
marriage will not take place with the party in question. Position of Venus also to be checked."

RULE 3 — SECOND MARRIAGE:
"If the sub lord of the 2nd cusp is well connected to 7, second marriage is possible."

RULE 4 — ONLY ATTACHMENT (not legal marriage):
"If connected only to 11th house, one will have a companion as attachment.
H7 shows legal bondage whereas H11 indicates attachment and friendship only."

COMPLETE H7 SUB LORD SIGNIFICATION RULES:
- Signifies 2, 7, 11 → MARRIAGE PROMISED (primary rule)
- Signifies 1, 6, 10, 12 → Marriage delayed or not with this party
- Signifies 5, 7, 11 → Strong love marriage (H5=love, H7=legal, H11=fulfillment)
- Signifies 2, 7, 11 + H8 also → Marriage but after obstacles/karmic tests
- Signifies 6, 11 → Business partnership possible, not necessarily marriage
- Signifies 6, 12 → Partnership will break if it forms
- Signifies 5, 11 → Permanent loving bond
- Signifies 5, 8, 12 → Partner benefits while native loses

KSK STRICT TIMING RULE FOR MARRIAGE (Reader, Marriage chapter):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
"Marriage fructifies in the joint period (Mahadasha-Antardasha-
Pratyantardasha) of the SIGNIFICATORS of houses 2, 7 and 11.
Specifically, when the AD lord IS THE SUB-LORD of one of 2, 7, or 11
AND signifies the other two relevant houses through its star/sub
chain, that AD is the PRIMARY MARRIAGE-TRIGGER WINDOW."

This rule explicitly does NOT require the karaka (Venus) to be
the AD lord. Mercury, Jupiter, or any other planet can trigger
marriage IF it is the sub-lord of 2, 7 or 11 AND its chain signifies
the other two cusps.

H2 CUSP SUB LORD FOR MARRIAGE:
"If the sub lord of the 2nd cusp signifies 7 and 11, predict marriage."
H2 sub lord connected to H7 and H11 = marriage arrangements will be fulfilled.
H2 sub lord connected to H5 and H11 = childbirth (family creation aspect).
H2 sub lord connected to H7 only = marriage for financial/practical reasons.

→ TIMING APPLICATION: When the H2 sub-lord is itself the running AD
   lord (or PAD lord), THAT is a primary marriage trigger window.
   Do not wait for the karaka's AD to show up — H2-sub-lord's own AD
   is sufficient if its chain signifies 7 and 11.

H11 CUSP SUB LORD FOR MARRIAGE:
H11 is the FULFILLMENT house — for marriage to COMPLETE (not just be promised),
H11 must also signify the relevant houses.
H11 sub lord connected to H7 and H2 = marriage fulfills family desires.
H11 sub lord connected to H7 alone = marriage happens but family support limited.
H11 connected to H8 = marriage fulfillment comes after obstacles.

→ TIMING APPLICATION: When the H11 sub-lord is the running AD lord
   AND its chain signifies H7 and H2, marriage fructifies in that AD.
   This is often a STRONGER trigger than the karaka AD because H11 is
   the "result/gain" house — fulfillment requires H11 activation.

DOUBLE-CUSP-SUB-LORD CASE (KSK PEAK-TIMING SIGNAL):
If the same planet is the sub-lord of TWO of the marriage cusps
(e.g., H2 sub = H11 sub = Mercury), then THAT planet's AD is a
peak marriage-firing window. Both gates open simultaneously when
that planet runs as MD/AD/PAD.

GOCHARYA (TRANSIT) MARRIAGE TRIGGERS:
KSK Reader (later editions) acknowledges Jupiter's transit as a
secondary timing confirmation:
- Jupiter transit through H1 (Lagna) or H7 = marriage trigger if
  dasha supports
- Jupiter transit through H2 = family-arrangement trigger
- Jupiter transit through H11 = marriage-fulfillment trigger
  (the "completion" signal — strongest of the four)
- Saturn transit through H7 generally DELAYS marriage during that
  ~2.5 year period
The strongest timing is when DASHA + TRANSIT both fire on the
same houses. A favorable AD with Jupiter on H11 = peak window.

SECTION 4: VENUS — THE MARRIAGE KARAKA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Venus is the PRIMARY KARAKA (natural significator) for marriage.

VENUS STRENGTH ASSESSMENT:
Strong Venus (supports marriage):
- Venus is H7 or H11 lord
- Venus appears in Ruling Planets at time of query
- Venus is in star of benefic in 2, 7, 11
- Venus is Lagna star lord (personally important for native)
- Venus signifies 2, 7, 11 directly

Weak Venus (delays marriage):
- Venus in H6 (service/enemy house) → marriage conditional
- Venus in H8 (obstacle) → marriage with difficulties
- Venus in H12 (isolation) → bed pleasures separate from legal marriage
- Venus in star of planet in 6, 8, or 12

VENUS IS CONTEXT, NOT OVERRIDE — STRICT KP RULE:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
KSK explicitly stated KP rejects Parashari karaka-centric reasoning.
Direct quote: "no planet is inherently good or bad — house signification matters above everything."

This means:
- Even DEBILITATED Venus does NOT prevent marriage if H7 CSL is favorable
- Even EXALTED Venus does NOT save marriage if H7 CSL is unfavorable
- Venus's strength is CONTEXT — it tells you about the QUALITY of marriage,
  not whether it WILL HAPPEN

The CSL of H7 is the deciding factor. Venus is contextual information ONLY.

WHEN VENUS-CONTEXT MATTERS (quality, not promise):
- Strong Venus + favorable H7 CSL → very happy, smooth marriage
- Weak Venus + favorable H7 CSL → marriage happens but with friction in romance/intimacy
- Strong Venus + unfavorable H7 CSL → person attracts partners but no legal marriage
- Weak Venus + unfavorable H7 CSL → no marriage; if forced, unhappy

DO NOT use Venus strength to "rescue" a weak H7 CSL verdict from DENIED to CONDITIONAL.
The CSL signification is what determines promise. Period.

When specific planet is H7 sub lord (from KP Reader rules):

SUN as H7 sub lord: No deep pleasure in marriage, formal relationship, noble spouse.
  Married life is dignified but emotionally distant. Little physical satisfaction.

MOON as H7 sub lord: Very pleasant, emotionally satisfying marriage.
  Quick changes in relationship dynamics. Sensitive and nurturing spouse.

MARS as H7 sub lord: Silly quarrels and displeasure, arguments in marriage.
  Energetic but confrontational spouse. Marriage needs anger management.

MERCURY as H7 sub lord: Enjoys more than one relationship. Multiple options.
  If in dual sign and signifies 2,11 → more than one wife possible.
  Clever, communicative but uncommitted spouse.

JUPITER as H7 sub lord: Pleasant marriage if signifies 2 and 11.
  Wise, learned, prosperous spouse. Traditional, dharmic marriage.
  "If H7 sub lord is Jupiter or Venus and signifies 2,11 = very happy marital life."

VENUS as H7 sub lord: Extreme pleasure, 64 leelas (full intimate happiness).
  Beautiful, artistic, luxury-loving spouse. Best for marital happiness.

SATURN as H7 sub lord: Dissatisfaction, aged husband/wife, large age gap.
  "Difference in age is very much." Duration of pleasure is short.
  Delays and karmic tests before marital happiness.

RAHU as H7 sub lord: Foreign or unconventional partner. Karmic delays.
  Unexpected circumstances around marriage. May involve foreign elements.
  In terms of signification — read Rahu as its star lord / conjunction planet.

KETU as H7 sub lord: Sudden, unusual marriage circumstances.
  Separation tendency. Spouse may have spiritual inclinations.
  Read Ketu's signification through its star lord.

Level 1: Planets occupying H2, H7, H11
Level 2: Lords of H2, H7, H11
Level 3: Planets in the star of occupants of H2, H7, H11
Level 4: Planets in the star of lords of H2, H7, H11

Then identify FRUITFUL SIGNIFICATORS:
Those that appear in BOTH the significators list AND the Ruling Planets.

FRUITFUL SIGNIFICATORS for marriage = planets that will actually trigger marriage.

NON-FRUITFUL SIGNIFICATORS:
Those that appear in significators list but NOT in Ruling Planets.
These may be involved but won't be the primary trigger.

  Check whether H5 appears in any layer of the H7 CSL 4-step chain:
    SELF (H7 CSL planet house occupation + ownership)
    STAR (H7 CSL planet star lord houses)

  If H5 in any layer = there is a structural LOVE-TENDENCY.
  If H5 absent = no love-tendency; arranged path more likely.

SIGNAL 2: 5L PLACEMENT QUALITY (THE CRITICAL OVERRIDE — most often missed)
  Where does the 5th house lord (5L) sit? This OVERRIDES Signal 1.
    5L in H5/H7/H11 own = strong LOVE marriage indicator (love affair fruits)
    5L in H1/H2/H4/H9   = mild LOVE-tendency (depends on chain)
    5L in H6            = LOVE AFFAIRS FAIL TO CULMINATE (separation house)
                          Romance phase happens but does not lead to marriage.
                          Family arrangement path takes over.
    5L in H8            = HIDDEN/SCANDAL-PRONE love affairs that do not culminate
    5L in H12           = LOVE AFFAIR ENDS IN LOSS (12th = expenditure/loss)
    5L in H10           = Romance subordinated to career — neither pure love nor arranged
    5L in H3            = Communication-based romance, often sibling/peer-mediated

  CRITICAL RULE: If 5L is in H6/H8/H12, then EVEN IF Signal 1 (H5 in H7 CSL chain)
  is positive, the love-marriage path is structurally NEGATED. Do NOT predict
  love marriage just because H5 appears in CSL chain. Check 5L placement first.

SIGNAL 3: H4 + H9 PARENTAL-MEDIATION CHECK (arranged-marriage indicator)
  Check whether H4 (mother / parental home) AND H9 (father / dharma) appear
  in the H7 CSL chain.
    H4 in chain alone   = mother network mediates
    H9 in chain alone   = father network mediates
    BOTH H4 and H9      = strong family-mediated arranged marriage signature
    NEITHER             = family role is minimal; native finds own partner

  ADDITIONAL CHECK — H4 sub lord and H9 sub lord:
    H4 sub lord signifies H7 = mother is involved in finding partner
    H9 sub lord signifies H7 = father is involved in finding partner
    Both H4 and H9 sub lords link to H7 = both parents actively mediating

SIGNAL 4: MOON / FAMILY POSITION (family-driven indicator)
  Moon represents family emotional life. Moon house position contributes:
    Moon in H2/H4    = Strong family-driven marriage decision
    Moon in H7/H11   = Marriage is emotionally joint decision
    Moon in H5/H9    = Romance + dharma blended (love-cum-arranged tendency)
    Moon in H8/H12   = Hidden/secretive romance (often ends sadly)
    Moon in H1/H10   = Self-driven or career-tied marriage decision

SIGNAL 5: 5L-7L RELATIONSHIP (the binder)
  How are the 5th and 7th lords related?
    5L = 7L (same planet)            = Strong LOVE-MARRIAGE signature
    5L conjunct 7L (within 8 deg)    = Strong LOVE indicator
    5L in 7L sign (parivartana)      = Mild love-link
    7L in 5L sign (parivartana)      = Same — mild love link
    5L and 7L in mutual aspect       = Love-conducive
    5L and 7L unconnected            = No specific love indicator

            arrangement. Native participates in selection (likes/approves
            the match) but does NOT find the partner independently.

  THIS IS THE CASE THAT IS OFTEN MISCLASSIFIED AS LOVE-CUM-ARRANGED by
