KP ASTROLOGY — MARRIAGE RULES
Source: KP Reader 4 (Marriage, Married Life and Children) + vaastuinternational.com
Houses: 2, 7, 11 (primary), with 5 for love, 3 for agreement

PRIMARY CUSP: 7th house sub lord

PROMISE OF MARRIAGE
- Sub lord of 7th cusp must signify houses 2, 7, or 11 for marriage to be promised
- Sub lord of 2nd cusp signifying 7 and 11 = confirms marriage (entering a family)
- Sub lord of 11th cusp signifying 7 and 11 = fulfillment of marriage desire
- All three cusps (2, 7, 11) ideally should have sub lords connecting to marriage houses
- If 7th cusp sub lord signifies 2, 7, 11 = strong promise
- If 7th cusp sub lord signifies only 1, 6, 10 = marriage denied or very delayed
- If 7th cusp sub lord signifies 12 = secret relationship or hidden marriage
- Weak promise = marriage possible but with obstacles, delays, unusual circumstances

2ND CUSP SUB LORD FOR MARRIAGE
- 2nd cusp sub lord signifying 7 and 11 = marriage (family bondage confirmed)
- 2nd cusp sub lord signifying only 11 = attachment/friendship but not legal marriage
- 2nd cusp sub lord well connected to 7 = second marriage possible if 1st is denied
- 2nd cusp sub lord connected to 12 = defect or secretive in marriage matters

7TH CUSP SUB LORD INTERPRETATIONS
- Sun as 7th sub lord: marriage to person in authority, or delayed due to ego
- Moon as 7th sub lord: emotional marriage, partner connected to public/travel
- Mars as 7th sub lord: energetic partner, possible conflicts in married life
- Mercury as 7th sub lord: intellectual partner, communication-focused marriage
- Jupiter as 7th sub lord: auspicious marriage, wise and knowledgeable partner
- Venus as 7th sub lord: beautiful/artistic partner, strong marriage indication
- Saturn as 7th sub lord: delayed marriage, older partner, serious commitment
- Rahu as 7th sub lord: unconventional marriage, foreign/different background partner
- Ketu as 7th sub lord: spiritual partner, possible delay or unusual circumstances

TIMING OF MARRIAGE
- Dasha AND Antardasha lords BOTH must signify houses 2, 7, 11
- Pratyantardasha lord should also connect to marriage houses for exact timing
- Ruling planets at time of query must include planet connected to 7th house
- Venus as ruling planet during marriage query = strong confirmation
- Jupiter as ruling planet = auspicious timing for marriage

DENIAL AND DELAY CONDITIONS
- 7th cusp sub lord signifying 1, 6, 8, 12 = obstacles or denial
- Saturn involvement = delay (not denial) — commitment comes after patience
- Retrograde planets as 7th cusp sub lord = repeated delays
- Sub lord in star of planet in 6th = obstacles from disputes
- Sub lord in star of planet in 8th = sudden changes, possible loss through marriage
- Sub lord in star of planet in 12th = foreign connection or secret marriage

LOVE vs ARRANGED MARRIAGE
- 5th house involvement (5th cusp sub lord connected to 7) = love marriage
- 5th lord as significator of 7th house = self-chosen partner
- 3rd or 9th house involvement = partner from different city or community
- Rahu involvement = unconventional, inter-caste, or love across cultures
- Jupiter strong and in 7th = arranged marriage with auspicious outcome

MARRIAGE TIMING PLANETS
- Venus period = natural karaka period for marriage
- Jupiter period = favorable if it signifies 2, 7, 11
- 7th lord's dasha period = strong for marriage matters
- Moon period (if Moon signifies 7th) = emotional timing for marriage

SECOND MARRIAGE
- 9th house = 3rd from 7th (bhavat bhavam) = second marriage indicator
- Sub lord of 9th cusp signifying 9 and 11 = second marriage possible
- 7th cusp sub lord signifying 8 = possible separation from first spouse

MARRIED LIFE QUALITY
- 7th cusp sub lord signifying 6, 8, 12 as well = problematic married life
- Venus strongly signifying 7th = harmonious marriage
- Saturn in 7th house or as 7th sub lord = disciplined but cold marriage
- Mars and Venus both connected to 7th = passionate marriage

HOUSE GROUPINGS FOR MARRIAGE ANALYSIS
- Houses 2, 7, 11 = marriage confirmation houses
- House 5 = love, romance, attraction
- House 3 = agreements, consent, communication between parties
- House 12 = bed pleasures, foreign connection in marriage
- House 6, 8 = obstacles, disputes, transformation in marriage
