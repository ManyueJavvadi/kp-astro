SECTION 1: RELEVANT HOUSES FOR CAREER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For Employment/Job (Service under someone):
PRIMARY HOUSES: H10 (profession), H6 (service/employment)
SUPPORTING HOUSES: H2 (income/earnings), H11 (gains/fulfillment)
ALL FOUR: Houses 2, 6, 10, 11 must all be connected for a job to materialize

For Business (Self-employment):
PRIMARY HOUSES: H10 (career/action), H7 (partners/public dealings)
SUPPORTING HOUSES: H2 (wealth), H11 (profits)

DENIAL HOUSES FOR JOB:
PRIMARY DENIAL (12th-from-relevant logic — strongest negation):
- H1 (12th from H2 — loss of income)
- H5 (12th from H6 — loss of service/employment)
- H9 (12th from H10 — loss of profession/status)
- H12 (universal loss/expenditure)

SECONDARY DENIAL (problem indicators):
- H8 (stress, sudden disruption, sometimes job loss with no fallback)
- H3 (12th from H4 — loss of stability) — minor

When CSL signifies the PRIMARY denial set (1, 5, 9, 12) AND has no relevant-house touch:
→ DENIED.

When CSL signifies BOTH primary denial AND relevant houses:
→ CONDITIONAL — apply KSK strict bhukti rule:
  - Bhuktis of relevant-house significators → job offer/promotion fires
  - Bhuktis of denial-house significators → job lost / change failed / interview rejection

"For one to earn by self-exertion, H10 sub lord must be significator of 2 (self-acquisition)
or 10 (profession). If connected with dual sign and both 6 and 7 = earns through service AND business."

INCOME TAX TROUBLE:
"If H10 sub lord signifies 7, 8 and 12 → income tax trouble/scrutiny."

SECTION 4: SERVICE JOB vs BUSINESS — KEY DISTINCTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SERVICE (Job under someone):
- H6 must be strongly signified (house of service/employment)
- H10 shows the profession/career but H6 confirms it's a job, not business
- Required houses: 2, 6, 10, 11
- H6 is the DIFFERENTIATOR — without H6, may be business or freelance

BUSINESS (Self-employment):
- H7 must be signified (public dealings, partnerships, customers)
- H10 shows the profession/career
- Required houses: 2, 7, 10, 11
- H7 is the DIFFERENTIATOR — with H7 connected = business indicator

BOTH SERVICE AND BUSINESS:
- H10 sub lord in dual sign AND connected to both H6 and H7
- Native earns through service AND has business on the side

PROMOTION IN EXISTING JOB:
Required: H2, H6, H10, H11 all connected in the relevant dasha/antardasha
H3 also relevant as boldness/effort required for promotion

NEW JOB vs TRANSFER vs PROMOTION:
- New job: H6 + H10 + H2 + H11 activated by dasha
- Transfer: H4 sub lord signifies 3, 10, 12 (change of place)
- Promotion: H6 + H10 + H11 (gains/rise) activated

CRITICAL DISTINCTION — CONTRACT / CONSULTANCY ROLES:
For short-term contracts, consultancy, or service engagements (NOT permanent employment):
- H6 is the PRIMARY fulfillment house (service contract = H6 domain)
- H6 + H2 activation in PAD = the contract/service engagement is active and REAL
- H10 and H11 confirm offer letter receipt and income crystallisation
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT say H6 activation means only "competition is running."
  H6 means SERVICE IS ACTIVE. For contract roles, H6 activation in PAD = the
  engagement is taking place. H10/H11 = formal offer/payment confirmation.
- So in Saturn PAD (if Saturn in H6 or signifies H6): contract work is ongoing
  or being initiated. The offer letter timing follows when H10/H11 activate
  (next PAD or sub-PAD where those houses are touched).
- Example: If Saturn PAD hits H6+H2, the interview/engagement is LIVE and meaningful.
  It is NOT merely "practice." The outcome confirmation (offer) crystallises when
  H10 and H11 fire — which could be early in the next PAD or via transit triggers.

SECTION 6: TECHNOLOGY AND IT CAREER SPECIFICS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Technology / IT / Software / Data roles require:
PRIMARY PLANETS: Mercury (data/logic/analysis/communication), Rahu (technology/unconventional)
SECONDARY PLANETS: Saturn (systems/infrastructure/structure), Jupiter (knowledge/wisdom applied)

Mercury-dominated H10 = alignment for analytical/technology career.
Mercury in H10 or as H10 lord = native naturally suited for tech, writing, commerce roles.

TYPES OF TECH ROLES BY PLANET COMBINATION:
- Mercury + Rahu: Data engineering, data science, AI/ML, unconventional tech, overseas tech
- Mercury + Saturn: Infrastructure, DevOps, systems administration, backend engineering
- Mercury + Jupiter: Senior/architect/consulting roles, knowledge management, tech leadership
- Mercury + Sun: Government tech, management, IT administration
- Mercury + Mars: Engineering (mechanical+tech), embedded systems, manufacturing tech
- Rahu + Jupiter: Research roles, innovative/cutting-edge fields, foreign tech companies

For overseas/foreign tech companies: Rahu + Mercury + H9/H12 connections
For senior/architect level: Jupiter + Mercury combination
For startup/innovative work: Rahu + Mercury + H5 (intelligence/speculation)

TIMING FOR TECHNOLOGY CAREERS:
Mercury AD periods: Very favorable for tech career advancement, job offers in analytics/IT
Jupiter AD: Favorable for senior/consulting/management roles
Rahu AD: Good for foreign tech companies or unconventional/startup opportunities
Saturn AD: Can bring structured/stable tech roles (infrastructure, ops, maintenance)
Sun AD: Government or authority tech positions

2. AD lord occupies H10 or H6 AND signifies 2, 11

- Mercury PAD: Quick intellectual/tech job offers
- Saturn PAD: Delayed but stable position
- Jupiter PAD: Senior or prestigious position
- Sun PAD: Government or authority role
