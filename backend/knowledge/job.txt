KP ASTROLOGY — JOB / CAREER RULES
Source: KP Readers + vaastuinternational.com
Houses: 2, 6, 10, 11 (job/service), 7, 10 (business)

PRIMARY CUSP: 10th house sub lord

PROMISE OF JOB (SERVICE/EMPLOYMENT)
- Sub lord of 10th cusp signifying 2, 6, 10, 11 = job promised
- 6th house specifically governs SERVICE (working under someone else)
- 10th house = profession, status, career
- 11th house = gains, fulfillment, salary receipt
- 2nd house = accumulated wealth from job
- If 10th cusp sub lord signifies 6 and 10 in fixed sign = stable permanent job
- If 10th cusp sub lord signifies 6 and 10 in movable sign = job with frequent changes
- If 10th cusp sub lord signifies 7 and 10 in movable sign = business/self-employment
- If 10th cusp sub lord signifies 2, 6, 7, 10, 11 in dual sign = job + business both

JOB vs BUSINESS DISTINCTION
- Job (service): 6th house strongly signified = working for someone
- Business: 7th house strongly signified = partnership, own venture
- Government job: Sun prominently signified + 10th house activated
- Private job: Mercury, Rahu signified in career houses
- Business with partner: 7th lord connected to 10th and 11th

3RD CUSP SUB LORD FOR JOB
- If sub lord of 3rd cusp signifies 2, 10, or 11 = boldness to undertake job, success
- 3rd house rules courage and initiative to compete

6TH CUSP SUB LORD FOR JOB
- Sub lord of 6th cusp connected to 2, 6, 10, 11 = employment confirmed
- 6th house = service, employment, subordinate position
- 6th and 10th both activated = job in service sector

10TH CUSP SUB LORD INTERPRETATIONS
- Sun as 10th sub lord: government job, authority position, leadership role
- Moon as 10th sub lord: public-facing job, hospitality, travel industry
- Mars as 10th sub lord: technical job, engineering, military, construction
- Mercury as 10th sub lord: IT, data, communication, writing, finance
- Jupiter as 10th sub lord: teaching, law, advisory, finance, management
- Venus as 10th sub lord: arts, luxury, fashion, design, entertainment
- Saturn as 10th sub lord: hard work required, delay then stability, labour-intensive
- Rahu as 10th sub lord: technology, data science, unconventional field, foreign company
- Ketu as 10th sub lord: research, investigation, technical niche, spiritual field

TIMING OF JOB
- Both Mahadasha AND Antardasha lords must signify 6, 10, 11
- 11th house activation = salary/gains actually received (job materialized)
- Without 11th house = interviews happening but no final offer yet
- Ruling planets must overlap with dasha lords for timing confirmation
- If AD lord is 6th or 10th lord, or placed in 6th or 10th = strong timing signal

DELAY IN JOB
- 10th cusp sub lord signifying 8 or 12 = obstacles, hidden delays
- Saturn as significator = delay but eventual success (Saturn delays, not denies)
- Rahu/Ketu involvement = unusual path, sudden changes before settlement
- 12th house activation without 11th = expenditure without gain (interviews, travel for job but no result)

PROMOTION vs NEW JOB
- Promotion: 10th house activated + 11th for salary gain + same company indicators
- New job: 6th house freshly activated + 3rd/9th for change of place
- Salary increase: 2nd and 11th prominently activated in dasha

FIELD OF WORK (by planet signification)
- Sun: Government, administration, politics, authority, medical
- Moon: Public, hospitality, travel, food, nursing, agriculture
- Mars: Engineering, military, surgery, construction, real estate, police
- Mercury: IT, data engineering, software, communication, accounts, writing
- Jupiter: Teaching, law, finance, advisory, medicine, philosophy
- Venus: Arts, luxury goods, fashion, design, entertainment, beauty
- Saturn: Mining, agriculture, labor, construction, long-term projects, oil
- Rahu: Technology, data science, AI, foreign companies, unconventional paths
- Ketu: Research, investigation, spirituality, technical niche, security

DATA ENGINEERING SPECIFICALLY (Rahu/Mercury significance)
- Rahu on 10th cusp = ideal for data, technology, unconventional analytical work
- Mercury signifying 10th = communication, data, analytical roles
- Rahu + Mercury combination = data engineering, AI, analytics fields perfectly matched
- If dasha lord connects to both Rahu and Mercury = technology career timing

HOUSE GROUPINGS FOR CAREER ANALYSIS
- Houses 2, 6, 10, 11 = job/service confirmation
- House 7 = business/partnership
- House 3 = courage to apply, short journeys for work
- House 9 = long journey, foreign work opportunity
- House 12 = working abroad, foreign company
- House 8 = obstacles, sudden job loss, transformation in career
