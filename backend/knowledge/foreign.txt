FOREIGN TRAVEL (visiting/short stay):
PRIMARY: H9 (long journeys, pilgrimage, overseas travel), H12 (foreign land)
SUPPORTING: H3 (short journeys — initial), H11 (fulfillment of travel desire)
KEY RULE: H9 + H12 connected = overseas travel strongly indicated

FOREIGN SETTLEMENT (permanent relocation):
PRIMARY: H12 (permanent stay in foreign land/isolation from homeland)
SUPPORTING: H3 (short journeys start), H9 (long journey), H11 (gains abroad)
KEY RULE: H12 prominently connected with H3, H9 = foreign settlement

STUDY ABROAD:
Houses: H4 (education/institution), H9 (higher education/overseas), H12 (foreign land)
H4 sub lord connected to H9 and H12 = education in foreign country

WORK ABROAD:
Houses: H6 or H10 (career/service) + H9 + H12
H6/H10 connected to H12 and H9 = job in foreign country

VISA / IMMIGRATION:
Houses: H3, H9, H12 — same as foreign travel
When H3 (documents/communication) activated WITH H9 and H12 = visa/immigration process active

SECTION 3: H12 — THE FOREIGN LAND HOUSE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

H12 is the PRIMARY house for foreign land, settlement abroad, permanent residence
overseas, and also for investments/large expenditures.

H12 sub lord analysis for foreign:
- H12 connected to H9 and H3 → Foreign travel or settlement
- H12 connected to H6 or H10 → Work in foreign country
- H12 connected to H4 → Property investment (also applies to buying house abroad)
- H12 connected to H8 → Dangerous foreign situation or isolation

H12 AS INVESTMENT HOUSE:
H12 = money spent for future (investment, property purchase, foreign land)
For buying property abroad: H4 (property) + H12 (foreign/investment) + H11 (fulfillment)

SECTION 4: NATIVE ALREADY SETTLED ABROAD — CONTINUATION ANALYSIS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When native is already living abroad, questions about "staying" or "visa renewal"
or "PR/permanent residency" are questions about continuation of H12 matters.
H12 sub lord's current activation in dasha = continuation or change of foreign status.

For settling permanently in any foreign country:
The combination needed: H3 + H9 + H12 all must be signified in the dasha period
H11 must also be connected for the settlement to be FULFILLING/PERMANENT

VISA AND IMMIGRATION PROCESS:
H3 (documents/communication) + H9 (journey) + H12 (foreign land) all active = visa/PR process
When H3 is strongly activated alongside H12 = document processing, application filing
H11 connected = successful visa/immigration outcome

ALREADY ABROAD — STATUS CONTINUATION:
If native lives abroad and asks about continuing there:
H12 prominently activated in dasha = continuation of foreign stay
H4 (home country) becoming activated while H12 weakens = potential return home
H4 + H2 + H11 in dasha = return to homeland with gains/satisfaction

RETURNING HOME FROM ABROAD:
H4 (home country) becoming activated while H12 weakens = return home possibility
H4 + H2 + H11 in dasha = return to homeland with gains

  relocation. Rahu in H9, H12, or as AD lord = strong foreign indication.

MOON: Long journeys, travel, fluidity of location. Especially when in water signs.

JUPITER: Long journeys, higher learning abroad, philosophical travels.
  Jupiter as H9 lord = travel opportunities.

SATURN: Long-duration stays abroad, karmic foreign connections, difficult but
  ultimately productive foreign experience.

MERCURY: Business travel, communication-related foreign trips, tech-related overseas roles.
