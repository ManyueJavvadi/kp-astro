KP ASTROLOGY — FOREIGN TRAVEL AND SETTLEMENT RULES
Source: KP Readers + vaastuinternational.com
Houses: 3, 9, 12 (travel), 8, 12 (settlement abroad)

PRIMARY CUSPS: 9th (travel), 12th (foreign land/settlement)

PROMISE OF FOREIGN TRAVEL
- Sub lord of 9th cusp signifying 3, 9, 12 = foreign travel promised
- 3rd house = short journeys, courage to travel
- 9th house = long journeys, higher purpose, foreign lands
- 12th house = foreign country, loss of home environment
- Sub lord of 12th cusp signifying 3, 9, 12 = extended stay abroad confirmed

SETTLEMENT ABROAD (PERMANENT)
- Sub lord of 12th cusp signifying 8 and 12 = permanent settlement possible
- Sub lord of 8th cusp signifying 12 = going abroad permanently
- Rahu in 12th or as 12th cusp sub lord = strong foreign connection
- If 4th house (home) is denied and 12th is active = leaving homeland permanently
- Sub lord of 4th cusp signifying 12 = education or settlement in foreign land

VISA AND IMMIGRATION
- 9th cusp sub lord connected to 12 = visa/travel documents favorable
- 3rd, 9th, 12th houses all activated = overseas travel confirmed
- If 6th house also activated = work visa/employment abroad

TIMING OF FOREIGN TRAVEL
- Dasha/Antardasha lords signifying 3, 9, 12 = travel period active
- Ruling planets connecting to 12th house = strong confirmation of foreign trip
- Jupiter transiting 9th = auspicious for long journey
- If 12th cusp sub lord is fast-moving planet = travel soon

DENIAL OF FOREIGN TRAVEL
- 9th cusp sub lord signifying 1, 4, 7, 10 = travel denied or unnecessary
- 4th house strongly activated over 12th = stays at home, roots strengthen
- Sub lord in star of planet in 4th = home country preferred

FOREIGN JOB/WORK ABROAD
- Houses 6, 9, 10, 12 all activated = working abroad
- 10th + 12th = career in foreign country
- 6th + 12th = service/employment in foreign land
- Rahu prominently signified = foreign company or unconventional foreign connection

STUDY ABROAD
- 4th cusp sub lord signifying 12 and 4 = education in foreign country
- 9th and 12th both activated = study abroad confirmed
- Mercury + 9th + 12th = intellectual pursuit abroad
