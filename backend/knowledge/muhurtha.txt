SECTION 1: WHAT IS KP MUHURTHA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

KP muhurtha selects the moment when the Lagna (Ascendant) sub-lord signifies
the houses relevant to the event being undertaken.

THE CORE PRINCIPLE:
"The sub-lord of the Lagna cusp at the moment of commencement of any enterprise
will determine the success or failure of that enterprise."

The Lagna sub-lord at the moment of the event must signify the favorable houses
for that event. If the Lagna sub-lord signifies denial/adverse houses, the event
started at that time will face obstacles.

SECTION 2: EVENT-SPECIFIC HOUSE REQUIREMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MARRIAGE MUHURTHA:
Primary house: H7 (spouse, partnership)
Supporting: H2 (family, addition to family), H11 (fulfillment, gains)
Denial houses: H1 (self over partnership), H6 (disputes), H10 (career focus), H12 (loss)
Lagna sub-lord must signify H7 for marriage muhurtha.
Avoid: Rahu Kalam, Vishti Karana, eclipse periods.
Best weekdays: Wednesday (Mercury), Friday (Venus), Thursday (Jupiter).

BUSINESS OPENING MUHURTHA:
Primary house: H10 (career, profession, business)
Supporting: H2 (money), H6 (service/clients), H11 (profits/gains)
Denial houses: H1 (personal, not business), H5 (speculation), H9 (spiritual), H12 (loss)
Lagna sub-lord must signify H10 for business muhurtha.
Best weekdays: Wednesday (Mercury/commerce), Thursday (Jupiter/expansion), Tuesday (Mars/drive).

HOUSE WARMING (GRIHA PRAVESH) MUHURTHA:
Primary house: H4 (home, property, dwelling)
Supporting: H11 (fulfillment), H12 (investment paid)
Denial houses: H3 (change/moving away), H10 (status concerns over home comfort)
Lagna sub-lord must signify H4 for house warming muhurtha.
Best: Moon in benefic nakshatra. Avoid Mars-ruled nakshatra for H4 muhurtha.
Best weekdays: Monday (Moon/home), Thursday (Jupiter/expansion), Friday (Venus/comfort).

TRAVEL MUHURTHA:
Primary house: H9 (long journeys, fortune)
Supporting: H3 (short journeys, movement), H12 (foreign lands, expenditure)
Denial houses: H2 (stay home), H8 (danger), H11 (gains tied to staying)
Best for travel: H3 (short), H9 (long), H12 (overseas).

EDUCATION/EXAM MUHURTHA:
Primary house: H4 (education institution) or H9 (higher learning)
Supporting: H9, H11 (success/fulfillment)
Denial houses: H3 (change), H12 (loss)

SECTION 3: THINGS TO AVOID IN MUHURTHA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CRITICAL AVOIDANCES:
1. RAHU KALAM — Inauspicious period every day; strictly avoid for all auspicious events.
   Rahu Kalam = 1/8th of daylight from actual sunrise; varies by weekday and location.

2. VISHTI (BHADRA) KARANA — Half-tithi period that repeats; considered very inauspicious.
   "Events started in Vishti Karana will have obstacles and difficulties."

3. ECLIPSES — Solar or lunar eclipses make the entire day inauspicious for new beginnings.

4. LAGNA SUB-LORD IN DENIAL HOUSES — If Lagna sub-lord signifies denial houses for the event,
   that time is unfavorable regardless of other factors.

5. SATURN AND MARS HORA — Generally avoided for auspicious events (though not absolute).

GENERALLY GOOD INDICATORS:
- Lagna in benefic sign (Taurus, Cancer, Libra, Sagittarius, Pisces, Aquarius)
- Jupiter or Venus in Lagna or aspecting Lagna
- Moon in waxing phase (Shukla Paksha — from new moon to full moon)
- Moon in benefic nakshatra (Rohini, Mrigashira, Hasta, Chitra, Swati, Anuradha, Shravana, Revati, Pushya, Ashwini)

SECTION 4: SCORING AND SELECTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EXCELLENT WINDOW (Score 70+):
- Lagna sub-lord signifies primary house (+40)
- Lagna sub-lord signifies one or more supporting houses (+15 each)
- No denial houses touched (+20)
- Not in Rahu Kalam, not Vishti Karana
Example: For marriage — Lagna sub-lord is Venus, Venus signifies H7, H2, H11 = Excellent

GOOD WINDOW (Score 50-69):
- Lagna sub-lord signifies primary house
- Touches at least one supporting house
- Rahu Kalam avoided
Example: Lagna sub-lord signifies H7 and H11 but not H2 = Good for marriage

FAIR WINDOW (Score 40-49):
- Primary house is touched but no supporting houses
- Should be used only if no better option exists in the date range

AVOID (Score < 40 or any of: in Rahu Kalam, Vishti Karana):
- Lagna sub-lord does not signify primary house
- OR denial houses are signified
- OR Rahu Kalam/Vishti applies

SECTION 5: HOW TO EXPLAIN MUHURTHA TO CLIENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When presenting a good muhurtha window, explain:
1. "At this time, the Lagna (rising sign) is [sign]. The sub-lord of this Lagna is [planet]."
2. "This [planet] signifies houses [list], which are the key houses for [event type]."
3. "In KP, when we start an event at a time when these houses are activated in the Lagna,
   the event begun carries the signature of those houses through its lifetime."
4. "This window avoids Rahu Kalam and Vishti Karana, making it doubly auspicious."

Example explanation for marriage:
"At 7:14 AM on this date, the Lagna is in Pisces. The sub-lord of this Lagna cusp is Venus.
Venus signifies House 7 (marriage, partner), House 2 (family), and House 11 (fulfillment).
These are exactly the three houses needed for marriage. Venus is the natural karaka for
marriage relationships, making this window particularly strong. This window also falls
outside Rahu Kalam, and the Karana (Bava) is auspicious. An Excellent choice."

When a window is in Rahu Kalam:
"This time window falls within Rahu Kalam, which is traditionally avoided for auspicious
beginnings. While the Lagna sub-lord is favorable, I recommend the next window at [time]."

When date has no good windows:
"On this date, all Lagna sub-lords that are active during daytime hours either miss the
key marriage houses or signify adverse houses (H6/H12). I recommend considering [nearby date]
which has a significantly stronger window."

SECTION 6: COMBINING WITH NATAL CHART
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For stronger muhurtha selection, cross-reference with natal chart:
- The Ruling Planets at the muhurtha time should include planets that also appear as
  natal Ruling Planets of the concerned person(s).
- If the muhurtha Lagna sub-lord is also a significator of the relevant houses in the
  natal chart, the window is doubly confirmed.
- For marriage: if both partners' natal charts have the muhurtha Lagna sub-lord as a
  significator of their H7/H2/H11, it is a three-way confirmation.

"The best muhurtha is when the moment's Ruling Planets resonate with the natal
significators of both parties — this creates a harmony between cosmic timing and
personal chart promise."
