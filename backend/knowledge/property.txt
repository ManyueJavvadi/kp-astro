PROPERTY = TIER 2 default (life-impact financial decision).

  - Bankruptcy-risk language present (cross-link to wealth.txt §)
  - Ancestral-property dispute is causing family rupture
  - Property purchase is "last chance" / "all savings" framed

STANDARD TIER 2 FRAMING:
  - Acknowledge property is a major financial decision
  - Apply RULE 44 (capability vs manifestation) — chart promises
    property; current period may delay
  - Apply RULE 46 (falsifiable timing) — give specific window
  - Apply RULE 29 (decision support for "should I buy this property?")
  - Recommend professional consultation:
    * Real estate lawyer for title verification
    * Vaastu consultant for site alignment
    * Financial planner for loan affordability
    * Civil engineer for structural inspection (used property)

ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS:
  - Never predict a specific property purchase will succeed without
    medical/legal/financial due diligence
  - Never tell client to skip legal title verification based on chart
  - Never recommend over-leveraging (loan beyond H11 EMI capacity)
  - Never frame property denial as karmic punishment
