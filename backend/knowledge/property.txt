RELEVANT HOUSES:
PRIMARY: H4 (immovable property, land, buildings, home)
SUPPORTING: H11 (fulfillment of desire), H12 (investment/money spent)
KARAKA: Mars (karaka for immovable property, land, buildings)

H4 CUSP RULES FOR PROPERTY:
"Construction of house: Houses 4, 11, and 12 — sub lord of H4 cusp."
"If H4 sub lord signifies 4, 11, and 12 → one will construct a house."
"Mars must also come into picture for immovable properties and buildings."
"Saturn also relevant for construction aspects."
"H11 = fulfillment of desire, H12 = investment of money."

BUYING vs CONSTRUCTING:
Buying property: H4 + H11 + H12 → property purchase
Also H6 involved (6th is 12th from 7th = seller's loss = buyer's gain)
H9 also considered (9th is 12th from 4th of 7th)

SELLING PROPERTY:
"To sell a house: sub lord of H10 signifies 3 and 10."
"Houses 3, 5, and 10 judged for selling; 3 and 12 for giving possession."
"H4 = buyer's property; H10 = buyer's acquisition (4th from 7th)."

PROPERTY IN FOREIGN COUNTRY:
Foreign property: H4 (property) + H12 (foreign land/investment) + H9 (abroad)
H4 connected to H12 and H9 = property purchase in foreign country
H11 must also be connected for the purchase to be fulfilling/successful

TIMING FOR PROPERTY:
Mars dasha/AD: Strong property acquisition period (Mars = immovable property karaka)
Saturn AD: Slow but steady property building (construction focus)
Venus AD: Comfortable property, vehicle acquisition
Jupiter AD: Expansion of property holdings
Rahu AD: Unconventional or foreign property
