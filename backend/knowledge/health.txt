SECTION 1: RELEVANT HOUSES FOR HEALTH
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

H6: Disease, illness, sickness — nature and type of disease
H8: Danger, seriousness, chronic illness, surgery, obstacles to health
H12: Hospitalization, confinement to bed, isolation, hidden suffering

DISEASE TRIAD: H6, H8, H12 — when all three are activated, serious illness is present
RECOVERY TRIAD: H1 (self/vitality), H5 (recovery/rejuvenation), H11 (gains back health)
HOSPITALIZATION: H6 + H12 together in dasha
SURGERY: H6 + H8 + H12 together + Mars connection

H1 SUB LORD FOR HEALTH:
"Sub lord of H1 cusp indicates general health of the native."
"If sub lord of Lagna cusp is in constellation of planet in H6 → one will be sickly."
"Sickness happens in periods of significators of H1 and H6."
"If sub lord of Lagna is deposited in constellation of planet in H6, H8, or H12 → very sickly."

SECTION 2: H6 CUSPAL SUB LORD — NATURE OF DISEASE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

H6 SUB LORD TELLS THE NATURE OF DISEASE:
"The sub lord of the 6th cusp reveals the type of disease through its placement
in the chart — through sign lord, star lord, and the houses signified."

H6 sub lord in sign of/star of:
- Mars: Blood disorders, inflammation, accidents, fevers
- Moon: Mental health, digestive issues, emotional disorders, water-related
- Mercury: Nervous disorders, skin issues, respiratory (nervous system)
- Saturn: Chronic diseases, bone issues, arthritis, depression, dental
- Jupiter: Liver problems, obesity, blood sugar, excessive/expansion issues
- Venus: Kidney/urinary disorders, reproductive system, thyroid
- Sun: Heart, spine, eyes, circulation, vital force issues
- Rahu: Mysterious or difficult-to-diagnose conditions, poison
- Ketu: Hidden diseases, spiritual crises, sudden inexplicable issues

CRITICAL RULE:
"When H6 sub lord is in 12th from Lagna AND becomes a strong significator of H6
→ The native will DEFINITELY suffer from an incurable disease."

SECTION 3: H8 CUSPAL SUB LORD — DANGER AND SERIOUSNESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

"While H6 cusp sub lord tells about the NATURE of disease,
that of H8 tells about DANGER, and fatality is finally told."

H8 SUB LORD RULES:
SURGERY:
"For one to undergo surgery, the sub lord of H8 must be connected with:
H6 (house of sickness), H8 (house of surgery), or H12 (hospitalization)
AND the same sub lord must also be connected to Mars (karaka for knives/surgery).
Operation happens in the conjoint period of H6, H8, H12."

ACCIDENT:
"Accident can happen ONLY when H8 sub lord is deposited in star of planet
signifying H8, and it happens in the conjoined period of significators of H1 and H8.
It will be FATAL only if H8 sub lord is in star of significator of Badhaka or Maraka house."

SUICIDE:
"H8 sub lord deposited in star of planet signifying either Badhaka or Marakashthana
AND connected to H8 house + Mars connection = suicidal death."

SECTION 4: DISEASE TIMING — DASHA ANALYSIS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHEN ILLNESS OCCURS:
"In the conjoining period of significators of H1 and H6 → sickness is present."
"If H12 is also connected → hospitalization."
"If H8 is also connected → dangerous illness."

RECOVERY:
For recovery, the dasha period must shift to significators of:
H1 (vitality), H5 (rejuvenation), H11 (gains back health)
When significators of 1, 5, 11 operate = recovery period

HOSPITALIZATION:
Conjoint period of significators of H6, H8, H12 = hospitalization
H4 sub lord signifying H2, H4, H11 = discharge from hospital

DISCHARGE FROM HOSPITAL:
"Houses 2, 4 and 11 — sub lord of the 4th cusp [signifying these] = discharge from hospital."

SECTION 7: ANALYSIS STEPS FOR HEALTH QUESTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1: Check H1 sub lord — general health vulnerability?
Step 2: Check H6 sub lord — what is the nature/type of disease?
Step 3: Check H8 sub lord — is there danger or surgical risk?
Step 4: Is H12 also activated? → hospitalization possible?
Step 5: Identify the disease planet from H6 sub lord's planetary nature
Step 6: Identify which body part is affected from the sign involved
Step 7: Check current dasha — are H6, H8, H12 significators activated?
Step 8: When do recovery planets (H1, H5, H11) come into operation?
Step 9: Give timeline for illness risk and recovery window
