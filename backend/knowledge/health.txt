  Saturn dominant in H6 chain

  Examples: diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, kidney disease

ACUTE CONDITION (sudden, short):
  Mars dominant in H6 chain
  H6 sub lord in moveable sign
  H6 + H8 with Mars + Rahu = sudden / accident
  Examples: fever, infection, accident, surgery emergency
