KP ASTROLOGY — HEALTH RULES
Source: KP Readers + vaastuinternational.com
Houses: 1 (vitality), 6 (disease/recovery), 8 (chronic/serious), 12 (hospitalization)

PRIMARY CUSP: 1st house sub lord (general health), 6th cusp (disease)

GENERAL HEALTH PROMISE
- Sub lord of 1st cusp in star of planet in 6th = sickly native
- 1st cusp sub lord signifying 1, 5, 9, 10 along with 3 and 8 = long life promised
- 1st cusp sub lord signifying 1 and 11 = very good health generally
- 1st cusp sub lord connected to bhadaka and maraka houses = short life concern
- Lagna sub lord becoming significator of beneficial houses = strong constitution

1ST CUSP SUB LORD HEALTH INTERPRETATIONS
- If sub lord connected to 6 = periodic illness, recovers but prone to disease
- If sub lord connected to 8 = serious health concern, surgery possible
- If sub lord connected to 12 = hospitalization, bedridden periods
- If sub lord connected to 1, 5, 11 = strong vitality, good immunity
- If sub lord connected to 3 and 8 = longevity supported

DISEASE TIMING
- Significators of 1 and 6 operating together = period of sickness
- Dasha of planets signifying 6th or 8th = health challenges active
- 6th house = acute illness, infections, digestive issues
- 8th house = chronic conditions, operations, sudden health events
- 12th house = hospitalization, confinement, loss of energy

RECOVERY AND VITALITY
- 1st and 5th house activation during dasha = recovery and vitality returning
- Significators of 1, 5, 11 active = body fights back, recovery confirmed
- Jupiter in 1st or activating 1st = healing and vitality
- 6th cusp sub lord signifying 11 = recovery from illness (11 = fulfillment, 6 = disease — combination means disease ends)

- 1st house: Head, brain, overall vitality
- 2nd house: Eyes, face, throat, speech defects
- 3rd house: Shoulders, arms, lungs (upper)
- 4th house: Chest, heart (emotional), stomach
- 5th house: Stomach, liver, heart (physical)
- 6th house: Intestines, kidneys, immune system
- 7th house: Lower back, kidneys, reproductive
- 8th house: Reproductive organs, chronic disease seat
- 9th house: Hips, thighs, liver
- 10th house: Knees, skin, bones
- 11th house: Ankles, circulatory system, gains in health
- 12th house: Feet, immune suppression, hospitalization

PLANET AND DISEASE ASSOCIATIONS
- Sun: Heart conditions, eye problems, fever, bone issues
- Moon: Mental health, digestive issues, fluid imbalances, depression
- Mars: Accidents, surgery, infections, inflammation, blood disorders
- Mercury: Nervous system, skin conditions, respiratory issues
- Jupiter: Liver, obesity, diabetes, over-indulgence conditions
- Venus: Kidney issues, reproductive health, diabetes (sugar)
- Saturn: Chronic conditions, bones, teeth, joints, arthritis, delays in recovery
- Rahu: Unusual diseases, mysterious conditions, neurological issues
- Ketu: Infections, mysterious fevers, spiritual health crises

HOSPITALIZATION INDICATORS
- 12th cusp sub lord connected to 6 and 8 = hospital stay confirmed
- Significators of 6, 8, 12 all active simultaneously = serious illness requiring hospitalization
- 12th house = foreign hospital or confinement away from home
