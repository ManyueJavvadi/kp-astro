## 1. THE CORE H6 CUSP RULE (canonical KP doctrine)

Per KP Astrology Learning (6th house bhava rules), verbatim:

> **"Retrieving Trapped Money**: 6th cusp's sub lord to signify 2, 6,
> and 11, **excluding Saturn** → entangled money returned."

> **"Monetary Receipts**: If the 6th cusp's sub lord signifies 2, 6,
> and 11, desired monetary receipts are foreseen."

> **"Saturn connected to H6 sub lord → money recovery difficulty."**

### Decoded:

**Recovery favourable**:
- H6 CSL signifies {2, 6, 11} → all three houses present in 4-step chain
- NO Saturn in any layer (CSL, Star Lord, Sub Lord, Step 4)
- Verdict: money will be returned in the relevant-house bhukti

**Recovery delayed / difficult**:
- H6 CSL signifies {2, 6, 11} but Saturn IS in chain (any layer)
- Verdict: recovery is structurally possible but Saturn introduces delay
  (months to years) and friction (legal expense, partial recovery)

**Recovery denied**:
- H6 CSL signifies primarily denial set {5, 8, 12} with NO {2, 11} touch
- Verdict: this specific channel of recovery is blocked

### Why Saturn?

Saturn is the karaka of delay, obstruction, and structured-but-slow process.
When Saturn touches the recovery gate, recovery converts from "happens
naturally" to "happens only via prolonged legal process, with significant
cost, possibly partial." Cite explicitly when Saturn fires.

> borrowing from everyone occurs."

- This is the COMPLEMENT to the H6 rule (Section 1) — both point to recovery

**Cheque received but lender (you) loses something else**:
- H8 sub lord signifies {6, 11} → you get the cheque but the structural

**Borrowing-from-everyone pattern** (when YOU are in debt — different question):
- H8 sub lord signifies {5, 6, 8, 12} = native is structurally
  borrowing-prone, debt accumulates. Apply Tier 2 framing.

## 3. THE 4-HOUSE COMPOUND READING (partner cheating)

**This is the gold-standard reading for the user's specific scenario**:
"My business partner took ₹X and won't return it."

Cite ALL FOUR houses, in order:

### (1) H7 CSL — Partner intent / structural signal

Check whether H7 sub lord signifies the partner-fraud denial set
{5, 8, 12} per `business.txt` Section 3(d) doctrine:

> "Signifying 5, 8, and 12 → benefits the partner but causes your loss."

If this fires: the cheating is **chart-embedded**, not just a personality
failure of the partner. The structural signal is that wealth flows
partner-ward + loss flows native-ward, regardless of intent.

### (2) H6 CSL — Recovery / dispute mechanism (Section 1 above)

- Does H6 CSL signify {2, 6, 11} cleanly?
- Is Saturn in the chain?
- This determines RECOVERY PROBABILITY and TIMING.

### (3) H8 CSL — Loss recovery / sudden gain channel (Section 2 above)

- Does H8 CSL signify {2, 10, 11}? → borrowed money returnable
- Does H8 CSL signify {6, 11}? → cheque arrives + partner loses too
  (mutual destruction outcome)
- Does H8 CSL signify only {5, 6, 8, 12}? → recovery blocked, native
  may be in a broader debt-accumulation period

### (4) H2 CSL — Native's own wealth resilience

- Does H2 sub lord signify {2, 6, 11}? → native's wealth gate is open,
  loss is RECOVERABLE structurally even if not from this specific
  partner (replacement income, alternative recovery paths)
- Does H2 sub lord signify {1, 8, 12} primarily? → loss is part of a
  broader wealth-denial period; recovery via THIS channel less likely;
  recommend financial counsellor + diversification

### Verdict matrix from the 4-cusp compound:

| H7 (partner) | H6 (recovery) | H8 (return channel) | H2 (wealth) | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| {5,8,12} fires | clean (no Sat) | {2,10,11} | open | **FULL RECOVERY likely; legal+time required** |
| {5,8,12} fires | clean | {6,11} | open | **PARTIAL RECOVERY; mutual loss** |
| {5,8,12} fires | Sat in chain | weak | open | **DELAYED PARTIAL; long process** |
| {5,8,12} fires | denial only | denial | denial | **RECOVERY DENIED via this channel; pursue alternative** |
| neutral | clean | {2,10,11} | open | **STANDARD RECOVERY (typical case)** |

- H12 (loss / hidden)
- H2 (wealth taken)

**Recovery probability**:
- H6 CSL signifies {2, 6, 11} (without Saturn) → recovery via police /
  insurance / legal channel
- H6 CSL signifies {12} → theft becomes unrecoverable (gone abroad,
  hidden, no trace)

**Identifying the thief (rough KP indication)**:
- If Mercury involved in H8 chain → cunning / planned theft (insider)
- If Mars involved → forceful / physical theft
- If Rahu involved → unknown / unconventional / cyber-fraud
- Frame as STRUCTURAL TENDENCY ONLY; do NOT name specific people.
  Tier 2 protective framing applies.

**Timing of recovery (if structurally promised)**:
- Scan upcoming ADs for one signifying {2, 6, 11} simultaneously
- That AD's PAD with H11 alignment = the receipt-of-recovery moment

## 7. RECOVERY DASHA-WINDOW SCANNING

For EVERY money recovery question, scan upcoming dashas:

**Step 1**: Identify all planets that signify {2, 6, 11} from the
chart's significator table (excluding Saturn for clean recovery).

**Step 2**: List upcoming ADs (next 36 months) whose lord is in that list.

**Step 3**: For each candidate AD, check:
- PAD lord also signifies {2, 6, 11}? (joint period strengthens)
- Does the AD lord/PAD lord overlap with native's Ruling Planets at
  question moment? (RP confirmation = high-probability window)

**Step 4**: The first AD where ALL conditions align = STRUCTURAL
RECOVERY WINDOW. Cite the calendar date range.

**Step 5**: If no AD in next 36 months meets all conditions:
- State explicitly: "Per the chart, the next 3 years do not show a
  clean recovery window. The closest moderate window is [X]."
- Recommend: don't wait — pursue legal channels actively; structural
  recovery support arrives in [later window if any]; otherwise the
  chart suggests writing off this loss as karmic.
