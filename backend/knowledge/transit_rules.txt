KP TRANSIT ANALYSIS (GOCHAR) RULES
====================================
Transit analysis shows how currently moving planets affect the natal chart.
In KP astrology, transit significance is determined differently from traditional Parashara methods.

---

FUNDAMENTAL KP TRANSIT PRINCIPLE
==================================
A transit is significant ONLY when:
1. The transiting planet is the current Dasha lord, Bhukti lord, or Antara lord
2. The transit is over a natal planet or cusp that signifies the relevant topic

If Saturn is transiting your H7 but Saturn is not your Dasha/Bhukti/Antara lord → LESS significant.
If Venus (Bhukti lord) transits your natal Moon (which signifies H2/H7/H11) → HIGHLY significant for marriage.

---

HOW TO INTERPRET TRANSITS IN KP
=================================

Step 1 — Find transiting planet's position:
- Current longitude of the planet
- Which natal house is the planet transiting through
- What is the sub-lord of the nakshatra the transiting planet occupies

Step 2 — Check Dasha relevance:
- Is this planet the current Maha Dasha lord? → Most significant
- Is it the current Antardasha lord? → Significant
- Is it the current Pratyantardasha lord? → Mildly significant
- Unrelated to current dasha → Minor background influence

Step 3 — Check what the transit activates:
- Transiting planet over natal planet → activates that natal planet's house significations
- Transiting planet through a house → activates that house's affairs
- Transiting planet's sublord signifies 2/7/11 → favorable for that house's topic

Step 4 — Check for triggering:
- When multiple planets transit the same point → stronger activation
- Sun and Moon transits are fast (daily/monthly) → used for precise timing
- Saturn and Jupiter transits are slow (monthly/yearly) → long-term trends
- Rahu/Ketu transits → karmic turning points

---

PLANET-SPECIFIC TRANSIT INTERPRETATIONS
=========================================

SUN (transits zodiac in ~1 year, ~1 month per sign):
- Illuminates and energizes the house it passes through
- Transiting natal Lagna or Lagna sub-lord → health, vitality, self-focus
- Transiting H10 → career in spotlight
- Transiting H2 → financial matters highlighted
- In KP: significant when Sun is Dasha/Bhukti lord

MOON (transits zodiac in ~27 days, ~2.25 days per nakshatra):
- Emotions, mind, public image
- Used for day-by-day timing in KP predictions
- Transiting natal Moon → emotional high or low
- Transiting natal Sun → identity and soul alignment moment
- Full Moon/New Moon over natal significators → events crystallize

MARS (transits zodiac in ~2 years):
- Energy, action, conflict, surgery, accidents
- Transiting H7 or H8 → relationship tensions (especially if H7 SL period)
- Transiting natal Mars → increased drive OR aggression
- Transiting H1 → energy burst, possible health concerns
- Favorable in Aries/Scorpio/Capricorn transit positions

MERCURY (transits zodiac in ~1 year, varies with retrograde):
- Communication, intellect, business deals, contracts
- Retrograde Mercury = revisit, revise, delays in communication matters
- Transiting H3/H6/H9 → travel, writing, education matters
- In KP: significant when Mercury Dasha/Bhukti active

JUPITER (transits zodiac in ~12 years, ~1 year per sign):
- Wisdom, expansion, marriage, children, spirituality, guru
- Jupiter transiting H2 → wealth expansion
- Jupiter transiting H5 → children, education, romance
- Jupiter transiting H9/H11 → luck, gains, spiritual growth
- Most auspicious transits: over natal Jupiter, Venus, or Moon
- Called "Guru Peyarchi" — major life changes

VENUS (transits zodiac in ~8 months):
- Marriage, love, relationships, luxury, arts, vehicles
- Transiting H7 → romance/marriage events when Venus is Bhukti lord
- Transiting natal Venus → beauty, pleasure, relationship revival
- Venus retrograde → reconsider relationships/finances

SATURN (transits zodiac in ~29.5 years, ~2.5 years per sign):
- Discipline, delays, karma, hard work, elderly, chronic health
- Sade Sati: Saturn transiting 12th, 1st, 2nd from natal Moon → 7.5-year karmic period
  - 12th house Saturn: mental stress, expenditure, travel
  - 1st house Saturn: health challenges, personality restructuring
  - 2nd house Saturn: financial pressure, family tensions
- Saturn transiting H8 → major life transformation
- Positive: Saturn transiting H3/H6/H11 → favorable for hard work rewards

RAHU (transits zodiac in ~18 years, ~18 months per sign, RETROGRADE always):
- Ambition, obsession, foreign matters, materialism, unconventional paths
- Rahu transiting H7 → unconventional marriage, foreign connection
- Rahu transiting H10 → career in unconventional field, fame/notoriety
- Conjunct natal Moon → mental turbulence OR ambitious new direction
- In KP: Rahu acts as proxy for its dispositor planet

KETU (always opposite Rahu):
- Spirituality, detachment, past karma, research, occult
- Ketu transiting H12 → spiritual growth, foreign settlement
- Ketu transiting H4 → property-related issues, leaving homeland
- Conjunction with natal Sun → ego transformation, spiritual quest

DOUBLE TRANSIT THEORY (Gochara)
=================================
According to traditional transit theory (validated in KP):
A house/topic gets activated when BOTH Jupiter AND Saturn simultaneously aspect or transit through houses connected to that topic.

Example for marriage:
- Jupiter transits H7 or H1 (aspect to 7th) AND Saturn transits H7 or H1 → marriage conditions activated
- This principle helps narrow down the year for major events

---

KP TRANSIT VERIFICATION METHOD
=================================
1. Check running Dasha-Bhukti-Antara lords
2. Find what houses those lords signify natally
3. Check where those planets are transiting currently
4. Verify transit sublord also signifies the same houses
5. All three agreeing → high probability of event

Example: If running period is Saturn-Venus-Moon
- Saturn natally signifies H2/H7/H11
- Venus natally signifies H7/H11
- Today: Saturn transiting H7, Venus transiting H2 → Marriage likely NOW

---

PRACTICAL TIMING USING TRANSITS
==================================
For event timing predictions, use this cascade:
1. Year: Jupiter's annual transit position (Guru Peyarchi)
2. Month: Saturn's monthly position + current Bhukti lord transit
3. Week: Sun's weekly transit, Antara lord position
4. Day: Moon's daily transit (Moon must transit favorable nakshatra/sublord)
5. Hour: Hora (planetary hour) aligned with event type

---

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS
========================
- Vakra (retrograde) planets in transit: revisits, delays, reconsideration
- Combustion (planet within 10° of Sun): planet's strength reduced
- Mutual aspects between transiting planets amplify effects
- Eclipses (Rahu/Ketu near Sun/Moon): triggers major karmic events within 6 months
