KP ASTROLOGY — CORE PRINCIPLES
Source: KP Readers by Prof. K.S. Krishnamurti

SUBLORD THEORY
- The Sub Lord of a cusp is the final authority on whether a matter is promised
- A planet gives results based on its Star Lord's house significations
- The Sub Lord decides whether those results will actually materialize
- Sign Lord = source of event, Star Lord = nature of result, Sub Lord = quality/outcome
- Sub Lord power is 3x that of planet alone
- Star Lord power is 2x that of planet alone

SIGNIFICATOR HIERARCHY (order of strength)
Level 1 (strongest): Planets occupying the house whose star lord is placed in relevant house
Level 2: Planets occupying the relevant house itself
Level 3: Planets owned by the star lord of relevant house
Level 4: Planet that is the house lord (sign lord of cusp)
- Focus on Level 1 significators first — they are strongest
- Planets without any other planets in their stars are pure significators

RULING PLANETS (at time of query)
Order of strength:
1. Ascendant star lord (strongest)
2. Moon star lord (stronger)
3. Ascendant sign lord (strong)
4. Moon sign lord (strong)
5. Day lord (weakest)
- If Rahu/Ketu are in the sign of a ruling planet, the node REPLACES that planet in RP list
- Common planets between significators and ruling planets = fruitful significators
- Event will happen during dasha of planets common to both significators AND ruling planets

PROMISE VS TIMING
- ALWAYS check promise first via cuspal sub lord
- Never discuss timing if promise is not confirmed
- Promise = sub lord of primary cusp signifies relevant houses
- Denial = sub lord signifies opposing houses (12th to relevant house negates it)
- The 12th house to any house negates that house's matters
- The 2nd house from any house amplifies that house's matters

DASHA TIMING RULES
- Event fructifies when Dasha AND Bhukti AND Anthra lords all signify relevant houses
- All three must be significators — one alone is not enough
- Ruling planets must overlap with operating dasha lords for event to manifest
- Fast moving planets (Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Sun, Ascendant) trigger events
- Slow moving planets (Ketu, Rahu, Saturn, Jupiter) govern longer periods

TRANSIT TIMING (GOCHARA)
- If event expected within same day: move Ascendant to sensitive zone
- If event expected within 27 days: move Moon to sign-star-sub of significators
- If event expected within 1 year: move Sun through significator's sign-star-sub
- If event expected within 5 years: move Jupiter through significator's zone
- Three zodiac zones: Zone 1 = Aries to Cancer, Zone 2 = Leo to Scorpio, Zone 3 = Sagittarius to Pisces

RAHU AND KETU RULES
- Rahu and Ketu have no sign ownership — they act as agents
- They represent the sign lord where they are placed
- If conjoined with or aspected by any planet, they represent that planet
- Nodes replace sign lords in ruling planet calculations
- Rahu gives results of sign lord + conjunct planets combined

RETROGRADE PLANETS
- Retrograde planet as significator = delay, not denial
- Matter will come through when planet turns direct
- If both retrograde planet AND its star lord are retrograde = stronger delay

DENIAL CONDITIONS
- Sub lord of primary cusp signifying 12th to that house = denial/negation
- Sub lord signifying 1, 6, 8, 12 for favorable matters = obstacles
- Sub lord in enemy sign = weakened results
- If sub lord connects only to unfavorable houses = matter denied

IMPROVING HOUSES (favorable)
Houses 1, 2, 3, 6, 10, 11 = improving houses in KP
These houses when activated bring positive changes and success
