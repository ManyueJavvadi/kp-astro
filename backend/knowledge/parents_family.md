# Parents, Family & Relatives — KP Analysis Methodology

**Purpose**: Specific rules for analyzing parents (mother, father), siblings,
spouse's family, in-laws, and other relatives via Bhavat Bhavam (see
`bhavat_bhavam.md` for the underlying methodology).

---

## 1. Mother's Health & Wellbeing

**Mother's house in native's chart**: H4

### Mother's relevant houses (translated to native's frame):
- Mother's body / vitality = native's **H4** (her H1)
- Mother's recovery / vitality = native's **H8** (her H5) and native's **H2** (her H11)
- Mother's disease = native's **H9** (her H6)
- Mother's chronic / surgery = native's **H11** (her H8)
- Mother's hospitalization / loss = native's **H3** (her H12)

### Marriage rule for mother's health
- **CSL of native's H4** = primary gate for mother's health verdict.
  - If H4 CSL signifies H4 + H8 + H2 → mother is healthy / recovering
  - If H4 CSL signifies H9 + H11 + H3 → mother is ill / chronic / hospitalized
  - **Mixed signification** → KSK strict bhukti rule: in bhuktis of recovery
    significators (mother's H1/H5/H11) she improves; in bhuktis of disease
    significators (her H6/H8/H12) she suffers.

### Mother-specific dasha rules:
- Moon is the natural karaka for mother. Strong Moon dasha/bhukti = mother gets
  attention/care; afflicted Moon dasha = problems with mother.
- Saturn dasha for native = often hardships for elders including mother (longevity stress)
- Sun dasha = father's themes (different)

## 2. Father's Health & Wellbeing

**Father's house in native's chart**: H9

### Father's relevant houses (translated):
- Father's body / vitality = native's **H9** (his H1)
- Father's recovery = native's **H1** (his H5) and native's **H7** (his H11)
- Father's disease = native's **H2** (his H6)
- Father's chronic / surgery = native's **H4** (his H8)
- Father's hospitalization = native's **H8** (his H12)

### Father's career analysis:
- Father's H10 (his career) = 10th from H9 = native's **H6**
- So: when CSL of native's H6 signifies father's H2/H6/H10/H11
  (= native's H10/H2/H6/H7), father's career is promised/smooth.

### Father's longevity (sensitive question):
- Father's H8 = native's H4
- When dasha lord signifies native's H4 + H2 + H8 (combined: father's chronic + disease + chart-end)
  AND running sub-period activates this combo → father's health crisis indicated.
- Always speak with care. Avoid death predictions; speak in terms of "health crisis"
  or "extra care needed during this period".

## 3. Spouse's Health, Career & Wellbeing

**Spouse's house**: H7

### Spouse's translated houses (full list):
| Their concern | From their frame | In native's chart |
|---|---|---|
| Their health | H1 | **H7** |
| Their wealth | H2 | H8 |
| Their siblings | H3 | H9 |
| Their home / mother | H4 | H10 |
| Their children | H5 | H11 |
| Their disease | H6 | H12 |
| Their spouse (= native!) | H7 | H1 |
| Their chronic | H8 | H2 |
| Their luck / father | H9 | H3 |
| Their career | H10 | **H4** |
| Their gains | H11 | H5 |
| Their losses / foreign | H12 | H6 |

### Common spouse questions:

**"Will my husband get a job promotion?"**
- Spouse's career = native's H4. CSL of H4 must signify spouse's H2/H6/H10/H11
  = native's {H8, H12, H4, H5}. If H4 CSL signifies any of these → promotion likely.

**"My wife is unwell — will she recover?"**
- Spouse's health = native's H7. CSL of H7 must signify spouse's H1/H5/H11
  = native's {H7, H11, H5}. If H7 CSL signifies recovery group → yes.

**"Is my husband's family wealthy?"**
- Spouse's H2 (their wealth) = native's H8. Father-in-law's wealth (spouse's H2 + her H9)
  = native's {H8, H3}. CSL of H8 + H3 analysis.

## 4. Children — Specific Child Analysis

**First child / progeny in general**: H5
**Second child**: H7 (3rd from H5)
**Third child**: H9 (3rd from H7)

### First child's wellbeing (using H5):
| Child's concern | Their frame | Native's chart |
|---|---|---|
| Their body | H1 | **H5** |
| Their wealth | H2 | H6 |
| Their education | H4 | **H8** |
| Their disease | H6 | **H10** |
| Their career | H10 | H2 |
| Their marriage | H7 | **H11** |

**"Will my child's education succeed?"**
- Child's education = native's H8. CSL of H8 must signify child's education group
  (their H4/H9/H11) = native's {H8, H1, H3}. If H8 CSL signifies these → yes.
- BUT: also check native's H5 + H4 + H9 (general education houses) — there's
  overlap because child's education benefits native via "family expansion".

**"Will my child marry?"**
- Child's marriage = native's H11. CSL of H11 must signify child's marriage group
  (their H2/H7/H11) = native's {H6, H11, H3}. CSL of H11 analysis.

**"Will my daughter's pregnancy go well?"** (granddaughter analysis)
- Daughter = native's H5
- Daughter's child = 5th from H5 = native's H9 (grandchild promise)
- Daughter's pregnancy/delivery = daughter's H5 = native's **H9** with focus on
  daughter's H11 (recovery from delivery) = native's H3.

## 5. Siblings

**Younger siblings**: H3
**Elder siblings / friends / supportive groups**: H11

### Younger sibling's marriage
- Younger sibling's H7 = 7th from H3 = native's **H9**.
- CSL of H9 must signify sibling's marriage group (H2/H7/H11 in their frame
  = native's H4/H9/H1).

### Elder sibling's career
- Elder sibling's H10 = 10th from H11 = native's **H8**.
- CSL of H8 + connection to elder sibling's H2/H6/H10/H11
  = native's {H12, H4, H8, H9}. CSL of H8 analysis.

## 6. In-Laws

### Mother-in-law
- Mother-in-law = wife's mother = wife's H4 = 4th from H7 = native's **H10**.
- Her health = mother-in-law's H1 = native's **H10**.
- Her disease = 6th from H10 = native's **H3**.

### Father-in-law
- Father-in-law = wife's father = wife's H9 = 9th from H7 = native's **H3**.
- His health = native's **H3**.
- His career = 10th from H3 = native's **H12**.

## 7. Boss / Employer / Authority Figures

### Boss
- Boss = native's H10 (authority over native).
- Boss's career = 10th from H10 = native's **H7**.
- Boss's disposition toward native = relationship between H7 and H10 in native's chart.
- Boss's losses (which could mean his job loss = your boss change) = 12th from H10
  = native's **H9**.

### Employees / Subordinates
- Subordinates = native's H6 (those who serve native).
- Subordinate's wellbeing = native's H6 health.
- Subordinate quitting (loss of subordinate) = 12th from H6 = native's **H5**.

## 9. Critical Rules

1. **Always identify the relationship first** before applying any CSL analysis.
2. **Use Bhavat Bhavam translation** — don't apply native's house list to relative's matters.
3. **CSL gate logic still applies** to the TRANSLATED house.
4. **KSK strict bhukti rule applies** — relevant-bhuktis fire, denial-bhuktis block.
5. **For HIGH-STAKES questions** (parent's surgery, child's serious illness),
   recommend the relative's own chart for confirmation.
6. **Speak with care** about parents' / spouse's longevity — never predict death,
   speak in terms of "health crisis", "extra care needed", "advise medical attention".
7. **70% accuracy from native's chart alone** via Bhavat Bhavam. **85-90% with relative's chart**.
   Be honest about confidence level.
