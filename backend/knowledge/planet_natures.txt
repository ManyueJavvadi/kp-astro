================================================================
PLANET NATURES IN KP ASTROLOGY
================================================================

CRITICAL RULE: In KP, a planet's HOUSE SIGNIFICATIONS matter above everything.
Traditional concepts of benefic/malefic, exaltation/debilitation, and
friendships between planets are IRRELEVANT in KP.

What DOES matter in KP:
  1. Which house the planet OCCUPIES (primary signification)
  2. Which houses the planet RULES (as sign lord of cusp)
  3. Which planet's star the planet is in (colours the result)
  4. Which planet's sub the planet is in (decides the gate)

PLANET IN OWN STAR — Special Significance
================================================================

When a planet is in a nakshatra it rules, it gives PURE significations:
  Sun in Krittika, Uttara Phalguni, or Uttara Ashadha
  Moon in Rohini, Hasta, or Shravana
  Mars in Mrigashira, Chitra, or Dhanishtha
  Mercury in Ashlesha, Jyeshtha, or Revati
  Jupiter in Punarvasu, Vishakha, or Purva Bhadrapada
  Venus in Bharani, Purva Phalguni, or Purva Ashadha
  Saturn in Pushya, Anuradha, or Uttara Bhadrapada
  Rahu in Ardra, Swati, or Shatabhisha
  Ketu in Ashwini, Magha, or Mula

Effect: The planet gives its significations without the colouring or
modification that another planet's star would introduce.
This is considered one of the PUREST placements in KP.

================================================================
EXALTATION AND DEBILITATION IN KP
================================================================

RULE: Exaltation/debilitation signs are IRRELEVANT in KP.
The house occupied matters infinitely more than the sign quality.

A debilitated planet in a "yes" house = still a good significator.
An exalted planet in a denial house = still causes denial for that topic.

Example: Mars debilitated (Cancer) but in H11 → Mars still signifies H11.
For any topic requiring H11, Mars is a valid significator regardless of debilitation.

Example: Jupiter exalted (Cancer) but in H12 → Jupiter signifies H12.
For a topic needing H12 (like foreign travel), Jupiter is a significator.
For a topic needing H7 (marriage) and Jupiter rules no "yes" houses → neutral.

================================================================
COMBUSTION IN KP
================================================================

TRADITIONAL VIEW: Combust planets (within 8° of Sun) lose their power.
KP VIEW: Combustion has REDUCED significance. The planet still signifies its
houses but with slightly diminished independent expression. It does not negate.

KP Rule: A combust planet acts somewhat like the Sun's wishes — it tends to
bring its results through authority figures, father, or government channels.
But its house significations remain valid for prediction purposes.

================================================================
RETROGRADE PLANETS IN KP
================================================================

TRADITIONAL VIEW: Retrograde reverses results.
KP VIEW: Retrograde means DELAY, RECONSIDERATION, or RE-ATTEMPT.

Retrograde planet as CSL:
  The event signified by that cusp will happen but with unusual timing.
  Often there is a missed opportunity first, then a second chance.
  The person may refuse initially but reconsider later.
  Or external circumstances delay then clear up.

Retrograde in dasha:
  The dasha lord being retrograde does NOT cancel its results.
  It brings the results in a roundabout way, often after initial setbacks.
  Timing may be the last portion of the dasha period rather than the first.

The event DOES happen in retrograde CSL/dasha — just not in the expected way.

================================================================
INTERCEPTED PLANETS AND SIGNS
================================================================

In KP using unequal houses (Placidus), some signs get intercepted (fully within
a house without cusp). An intercepted planet:
  - Still signifies the house it occupies
  - But if its sign is intercepted, it rules no cusp → reduced lordship signification
  - This can make a planet a "lone significator" with no house lordship support
  - Such planets need star-lord chain to build their signification strength

================================================================
PLANET STRENGTH HIERARCHY IN KP
================================================================

For any topic, when multiple planets are significators, their strength is:

STRONGEST: Planet that OCCUPIES the relevant house (L1)
  → Its connection to the topic is direct and unmediated

STRONG: Planet that RULES the relevant house's sign (L2)
  → Ownership creates inherent responsibility for that house

MEDIUM: Planet in the STAR of an occupant (L3)
  → Channels the occupant's energy, secondary connection

MODERATE: Planet in the STAR of the house lord (L4)
  → Even more indirect, needs other support to be decisive

When a planet has BOTH L1 and L2 (occupies AND rules) for the same house:
  → This is the STRONGEST possible single-planet connection to that house.
  → Almost guarantees that topic's results in that planet's dasha.
