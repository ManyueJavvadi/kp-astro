## 1. THE FOUNDATIONAL DOCTRINE

### Primary house

**H4** — vehicles, comforts, conveyances, moveable assets.

### Karaka

**Venus** — vehicles, luxury, comfort. Strong Venus + favorable H4 chain
= structural vehicle promise.

### Secondary houses

- **H3** — short journeys (commute / daily use)
- **H9** — long journeys (travel / road-trip vehicles)
- **H11** — fulfillment of desire (the vehicle delivers what you want)
- **H2** — wealth allocation to the vehicle
- **H6** — vehicle as service (commercial), or vehicle-related disputes
- **H8** — vehicle accident risk (cross-link to `health.txt` §10)
- **H12** — vehicle theft / loss / expenses on vehicle

### The standard vehicle-purchase signature

H4 sub lord signifies H2 + H4 + H11 + Venus favorable in current AD →
vehicle purchase structurally supported.

> signs → vehicles and vehicular comforts."
>

## 2. VEHICLE TYPE BY SIGN-CHARACTER OF H4 CUSP

The sign and modality of H4 reveal the type of vehicle most likely:

### Sign modality

| H4 sign type | Vehicle type tendency |
|---|---|
| **Moveable** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) | Cars, motorbikes — primary moveable transport |
| **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) | SUVs, large vehicles, durable transport |
| **Common/Dual** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) | Multiple vehicles, motorcycles, hybrid use |

### Sign element

| Element | Vehicle character |
|---|---|
| **Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) | Sporty, fast, high-performance |
| **Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) | Practical, sturdy, family-oriented |
| **Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) | Modern, technology-rich, efficient |
| **Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) | Comfortable, family-sized, luxury |

### Planet-character of H4 sub lord

- **Sun** → authority / status vehicle (Mercedes / BMW / luxury car)
- **Moon** → family-sized, comfortable, possibly minivan / family SUV
- **Mars** → fast, sporty, motorbike-friendly, red/aggressive colors
- **Mercury** → modern, tech-heavy, efficient (hybrid / EV)
- **Jupiter** → expansive, comfortable, premium-class
- **Venus** → luxury, comfortable, design-rich (Venus is the karaka,
  so strong Venus = the vehicle quality stands out)
- **Saturn** → durable, second-hand acceptable, work-vehicle, truck
- **Rahu** → unconventional (foreign brand, electric, novel), shadow
  market possibility
- **Ketu** → small, simple, perhaps inherited or gifted
