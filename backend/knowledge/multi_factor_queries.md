# Multi-Factor Queries — Combined Question Analysis

**Purpose**: Many client questions involve MULTIPLE topics simultaneously:
- "When will I get married AND have a job?"
- "Will I have a happy marriage AND children easily?"
- "When will my career AND finances both be stable?"
- "When will I move abroad AND get a good job there?"
- "Will my mother's health AND my career both be okay this year?"

These questions cannot be answered by analyzing one topic in isolation.
This file teaches the AI how to handle compound questions properly.

---

## 1. Recognizing Multi-Factor Queries

### Patterns to detect:
- Conjunctions: "AND", "along with", "while", "during"
- Comparatives: "between job change and marriage timing"
- Causality chains: "Will my job loss affect my marriage?"
- Sequential: "After getting married, will my career change?"

### Identify each topic separately first:
Before answering, list:
- Topic 1: [marriage / job / health / etc.]
- Topic 2: [...]
- Topic 3 (if any): [...]
- Relationship between topics: [simultaneous / sequential / causal]

---

## 2. The Combined-Period Method

**Principle**: For TWO events to happen in the same period, the running dasha
lord(s) must signify the COMBINED house set of both topics simultaneously.

### Example: "When will I get married AND have a stable job?"
- Marriage houses: H2, H7, H11
- Job houses: H2, H6, H10, H11
- COMBINED set: H2, H6, H7, H10, H11
- Find a period where MD/AD/PAD lord signifies AT LEAST ONE house from BOTH groups

**Strong simultaneous fire** = MD/AD/PAD lord signifies houses from BOTH topic
groups, especially if it signifies H11 (universal fulfillment).

**Weak simultaneous** = MD signifies only marriage; AD signifies only job — they
happen in same MD but different bhuktis → "first job in March, then marriage in October"

## 3. Sequential Event Analysis

**"After X, will Y happen?"** type questions require timeline reasoning.

### Method:
1. Find the dasha period when X is promised + likely
2. Identify the next dasha periods (after X) where Y is also promised
3. Express timeline as: "X around [date], Y around [date], gap of [N] years"

### Example: "Will I have children AFTER getting married?"
- Marriage period: e.g., Mars MD bhukti X (signifies H2/H7/H11) — say 2026-2028
- Children period: when next dasha activates H2/H5/H11 — say Mars MD bhukti Y (2028-2030)
- Verdict: marriage in [window 1], children promise activates in [window 2]
- KSK strict bhukti rule: enumerate the specific bhuktis for each event

## 4. Causal Chain Questions

**"Will my [bad event] affect my [other area]?"** — checks whether ONE topic's
denial bhukti coincides with ANOTHER topic's relevant bhukti.

### Example: "Will my health issues affect my career?"
- Health denial period: where MD/AD lord signifies H6+H8+H12 (disease)
- Career timing in same period: does same MD lord ALSO signify H10 negation (H9, H5, etc.)?
- If YES → health issue likely disrupts career simultaneously
- If NO → health issue, but career remains stable (parallel concerns)

### Example: "If my marriage breaks, will my finances suffer?"
- Marriage denial period (H6+H10+H12 + Maraka activated)
- Wealth check during same period: does H2 CSL also signify H1/H5/H9/H12 (wealth denial)?
- If yes → financial stress alongside marriage break
- If no → marriage break but finances stable (or improve via different income)

## 5. Conflict Resolution — When Topics Compete

Sometimes the chart's CSLs give DIFFERENT verdicts at different times for
related topics. Need to navigate gracefully.

### Example: "Do I prioritize career or marriage?"
- If career period (Saturn AD) starts now AND marriage period (Venus AD) starts in 2 years
- Honest answer: "Career window is current; marriage window opens in 2 years.
  Both are promised, but timing favors career first."

### Example: "Should I accept a foreign job offer if it delays marriage?"
- Foreign career = native's career CSL + foreign houses (H9/H12)
- Marriage in foreign land = H7 CSL + H9/H12
- Check if BOTH align in some period → "yes, foreign job will lead to marriage abroad
  in [specific period]"
- Check if foreign job period blocks marriage temporarily → "yes, foreign job from
  [date] but marriage would happen domestically in [date] if you stay"

## 6. Family + Personal Combined Questions

**"Will I AND my family both be happy this year?"**

Use Bhavat Bhavam to translate family member's matters, then combine with native's
own analysis:

### Example: "Will my parents be healthy AND will I get a job this year?"
- Parents' health: native's H4 (mother) + H9 (father) CSL analysis
- Native's job: native's H10 + H6 CSL analysis
- Find current dasha bhukti — does it support BOTH simultaneously?

If MD lord signifies:
- H4 + H9 favorably (parents' health OK) AND H10 + H11 favorably (job promised)
  → Both yes
- H4 + H9 (parents OK) but H1 + H5 (job denied)
  → Parents OK but job delayed
- H6 + H8 (parents ill) but H10 + H11 (job good)
  → Career blooms while parents need attention

### Pattern recognition:
"Period where everything aligns favorably" = MD/AD/PAD lord signifies multiple
positive house groups
"Period of mixed blessings" = some areas thrive while others struggle (most common!)
"Difficult period for everyone" = MD lord signifies multiple denial groups

## 7. Quality + Promise Combined

**"Will I have a happy marriage AND children easily?"** — combines promise (yes/no)
with quality (smooth/rough).

### Method:
1. Marriage promise: H7 CSL → STRONGLY PROMISED
2. Marriage quality: Venus condition + H7 CSL strength + H11 fulfillment
3. Children promise: H5 CSL → PROMISED
4. Children quality: easy/hard via H5 affliction + Jupiter strength

### Compose response:
"Marriage is strongly promised in [period] with [Venus assessment].
Children are promised but with [easy/hard pattern] — Jupiter [assessment].
Combined period for both fully blossoming = [specific dasha bhukti window]."

## 8. Long-Term Life Trajectory Questions

**"What will my life look like over the next 10 years?"**
**"When will my golden period start?"**

### Method:
1. List all upcoming MD/AD periods (already in chart data)
2. For each major bhukti, identify which house group is most activated
3. Build a timeline:
   - 2026-2028: Career peak (H10 + H11 active via Saturn AD)
   - 2028-2030: Marriage window (H7 + H11 via Venus AD)
   - 2030-2032: Children + property (H4 + H5 + H11 via Jupiter AD)
4. Highlight "golden period" = bhukti where multiple positive house groups align
   simultaneously

### "Golden period" definition:
A multi-year window where MD lord signifies the native's relevant houses for
MULTIPLE major life areas (career + family + finance + health).
Signaled by: MD + AD lords being Ruling Planets at moment of query.

## 9. AI Response Template for Multi-Factor Queries

1. **Identify topics** explicitly: "Your question involves: [marriage] + [career]"
2. **Address each topic** separately first (verdict + houses)
3. **Then synthesize**: how they relate in time
4. **Specific timeline**: bhukti-by-bhukti when both align
5. **Honest caveats**: "If quality of one matters, [other] may delay slightly"

> **Marriage**: Strongly promised — H7 CSL Sun signifies 2/7/11 cleanly. Window: Saturn AD 2026-2028.

> **Career**: Promised with stability — H10 CSL Mercury signifies 2/6/10/11. Window: Saturn AD 2026-2027 also activates.
>
> **Combined window**: Saturn AD with Venus PAD (mid-2026 to early-2027) supports BOTH.
>
> If you want career stability BEFORE marriage, accept Mercury PAD (early 2026 — career-only).
> If you want marriage during stable career, target Venus PAD within Saturn AD."

## 10. Common Multi-Factor Question Examples

### "When will good days start?"
This is the user asking for the **golden period**. Method:
1. Scan upcoming MD periods
2. Identify when MD lord signifies H1+H5+H11 (general wellness)
   AND H10+H11 (career success)
   AND H2+H11 (financial gain)
3. The window where ALL three align = "good days"

### "Will I get PR / settle abroad / get foreign job?"
Combines:
- H12 CSL (foreign land settlement)
- H9 CSL (foreign movement)
- H10/H6 CSL (career abroad)
All three need to activate in the same dasha for "foreign career settlement".

### "Why is my life difficult now?"
Reverse analysis:
- Identify which house GROUPS are activated by current MD/AD/PAD
- If denial groups dominant (H6+H8+H12 + H1+H5+H9 + Saturn) → period is genuinely
  difficult; explain WHICH life areas are pressured
- Suggest when relief comes (next bhukti where positive groups activate)

### "When should I start a business?"
- Career promise (H10) + business specifically (H7) + finances (H2+H11)
- Plus: avoid Mercury retrograde periods if possible (Mercury rules trade)
- Plus: ascending dasha (vs. descending) for risk-taking ventures

## 11. Critical Rules

1. **Never analyze topics in isolation** for compound questions
2. **Always express the synthesis** explicitly at the end
3. **Bhukti-level precision** required when KSK strict rule applies
4. **Honest uncertainty** when multiple topics conflict — say "this period favors X but
   delays Y; trade-off"
5. **Golden period concept** — clients love this; identify it when asked broadly
   "when will good days start"
6. **For 5+ topic combined questions** (rare but possible), pick the 2 most important
   and address those primarily; mention others briefly
