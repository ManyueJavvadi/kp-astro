# Profession & Career Field Matching — KP Detailed Analysis

**Purpose**: Answer specific questions like "which profession suits me?",
"will I succeed in IT?", "should I become a doctor?", "is government job
right for me?", "will I do business or service?".

This file goes deeper than `job.txt` (which covers job promise/timing).
This file is about **WHICH FIELD** suits the chart.

---

## 1. Foundational Rule

In KP, the **profession is determined by the H10 cusp sub lord's signification chain**:
- The H10 CSL's NATURE determines the field
- Specifically: which planet IS the H10 CSL + which planet's STAR it sits in +
  which planets it CONJOINS WITH

**The 10th cusp's planetary influences = the profession**.

## 4. Service vs Business — H10 CSL Direction

### Indicators the native should be EMPLOYED (service):
- H10 CSL signifies H6 (service house) prominently
- H10 CSL = Saturn or Sun (authority, structure-loving planets)
- H6 CSL agrees with H10 CSL on service-related significations

### Indicators the native should run BUSINESS:
- H10 CSL signifies H7 (partners, public dealings)
- H10 CSL = Mars, Mercury, or Rahu (independent, risk-taking planets)
- H7 CSL is strong and signifies H2 + H10 + H11

### Mixed signals (consultant / freelance / hybrid):
- H10 CSL signifies BOTH H6 and H7
- Native works as freelancer, consultant, gig worker
- H6 + H7 + H10 all activated in current dasha

## 5. Field-Specific Confirmation Rules

### "Will I succeed in IT?"
Required: H10 CSL = Mercury OR in Mercury's star OR conjunct Mercury within 3.33°
Strong if: Mercury also signifies H10/H11
Tech sub-field from second-planet combination (see Section 3)

### "Will I become a doctor?"
Required: H10 CSL signifies H6 (service to sick) AND H8 (life/death matters)
OR Sun + Mars influence on H10 (medicine + surgery)
Specialty:
- Sun-influenced = General medicine, internal medicine
- Mars = Surgery, orthopedics, dental
- Moon = Pediatrics, gynecology, mental health
- Saturn = Geriatrics, orthopedics, chronic care
- Mercury = Pharmacy, lab medicine, diagnostics
- Jupiter = Psychiatry, education / teaching medicine

### "Will I be successful in business?"
Required: H10 CSL signifies H7 + H2 + H11
Plus: H7 CSL signifies H10 + H11 (cross-confirmation)
H6 should NOT be the primary connection (= service-leaning)

### "Will I work for government?"
Required: H10 CSL = Sun OR Saturn
Plus: Sun OR Saturn signifies H10 + H11
Ruling Planets often include Sun for government jobs

### "Should I work abroad?" (foreign career)
Required: H10 CSL signifies H9 + H12 (foreign travel + foreign land)
Plus: Rahu involvement in H10 chain
Cross-check: Foreign houses (3, 9, 12) AND career houses (2, 6, 10, 11) both
activated in dasha

## 6. Dasha-Period Career Change Indicators

### New job timing (employment):
MD/AD lord must signify H6 + H10 + H2 (or H11)
Plus: at least one of these should be a Ruling Planet at query time

### Promotion timing:
MD/AD/PAD lord must signify H10 + H11
H6 connection = service-side promotion
H7 connection = partnership / business expansion

### Career change to NEW FIELD:
Look for change of MD where new MD lord = different planet from
current career field.
Example: Saturn MD (heavy industry) ending → Mercury MD starting
suggests possible shift to communication / tech roles.

### Job loss / unemployment:
MD/AD lord signifying H1 + H5 + H9 + H12 (denial set) WITHOUT relevant
houses → unemployment likely.
Following sub-period activates 1, 5, 9 = job departure / change-failed.

## 7. Honest Confidence Notes

- **Profession field matching = ~75% confidence** without natal D10 chart
  (Dashamsha — Parashari concept; KP doesn't strictly use it but practitioners
  often consult)
- **With D10 chart added = ~85-90%**
- **For specific role title** (e.g., "will I be a software architect specifically?"),
  KP can suggest field but title-level prediction is unreliable. Be honest.
- **For "should I switch careers?"**, look at whether the next dasha period
  activates DIFFERENT planets than current career-determining planets. Strong
  signal of natural transition.
