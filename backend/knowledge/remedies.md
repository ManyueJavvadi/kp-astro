# KP Remedies (Parihara) — Behavioural + Ritual + Practical

KP astrologer's role is not just prediction but also REMEDY (parihara).
For every unfavourable pattern, KP offers actionable practice. The
LLM should add a "Remedies" section to predictions that show
delay/denial/friction patterns.

**Strict rule**: Remedies are BEHAVIOURAL FIRST, ritual second,
material (gemstone/metal) only with strict guard-rules. Never
recommend expensive interventions casually.

---

## REMEDY HIERARCHY (use in this order)

1. **Behavioural** — habit changes, attitude work, lifestyle.
   Always available, always free, always advisable.
2. **Service / charity** (dāna) — donation aligned with afflicting planet.
   Cheap, effective, classical KP-endorsed.
3. **Mantra / japa** — recitation aligned with planet.
   Free, requires discipline.
4. **Ritual** — fasting on planet's day, temple visits.
   Cultural; respect user's faith stance.
5. **Material** (gemstone, metal, yantra) — LAST RESORT, with strict
   guard-rules. Never recommend without verification.

## PER-PLANET REMEDY TABLE

### Sun (afflicted / weak / denying)
- **Day**: Sunday
- **Behavioural**: cultivate confidence without ego, daily morning sun-exposure (5-10 min), respect father/elders/authority figures, posture/spinal hygiene
- **Service**: donate wheat, jaggery, copper to elders or temples on Sundays
- **Mantra**: `Om Suryaya Namah` (108 times daily, 40 days)
- **Material**: ruby (only if Sun is debilitated AND H1/H11 sub lord does NOT signify 6/8/12 — see KP gemstone rule below)

### Moon (afflicted / mental anxiety)
- **Day**: Monday
- **Behavioural**: regular sleep (10pm-6am), consistent water intake, contact with mother/maternal figures weekly, journaling for emotional clarity
- **Service**: donate milk, white rice, silver to mothers/elders on Mondays
- **Mantra**: `Om Chandraya Namah` (108 daily)
- **Material**: pearl (rarely needed; check anxiety-pattern carefully)

### Mars (afflicted / aggression / injuries)
- **Day**: Tuesday
- **Behavioural**: physical exercise (cardio + strength), anger management practice, no excessive red meat, conscious driving/handling-tools-safely
- **Service**: donate red lentils, jaggery, copper to brothers/younger men on Tuesdays
- **Mantra**: `Om Angarakaya Namah` or `Om Mangalaya Namah`
- **Material**: red coral (only if Mars is genuinely weak; check H6 connection carefully — Mars in 6/8/12 should NOT receive coral)

### Mercury (afflicted / nervous / communication issues)
- **Day**: Wednesday
- **Behavioural**: writing practice (journal), reading (10 min daily), measured speech (count to 5 before reacting), screen-time hygiene
- **Service**: donate green vegetables, books, pens to students on Wednesdays
- **Mantra**: `Om Budhaya Namah`
- **Material**: emerald (only after thorough check)

### Jupiter (afflicted / wisdom blocked / metabolic issues)
- **Day**: Thursday
- **Behavioural**: mentor someone (younger, junior), continued learning, vegetarian Thursdays, weight management, gratitude journaling
- **Service**: donate yellow lentils, turmeric, books to teachers/students on Thursdays
- **Mantra**: `Om Brihaspataye Namah` (Guru Stotram daily)
- **Material**: yellow sapphire (commonly recommended; check H1/H11 sub lord rule first)

### Venus (afflicted / relationship issues / aesthetic blocks)
- **Day**: Friday
- **Behavioural**: artistic practice (music, drawing, garden), partner-appreciation daily ritual, aesthetic-environment cultivation, healthy relationship boundaries
- **Service**: donate white sweets, white cloth, perfume to women/young brides on Fridays
- **Mantra**: `Om Shukraya Namah`
- **Material**: diamond / white sapphire (rare recommendation; relationship issues usually behavioural-first)

### Saturn (afflicted / chronic delay / depression)
- **Day**: Saturday
- **Behavioural**: discipline cultivation, service to elderly/disabled, oil massage of feet, no shortcuts, walking/grounding practice, simplification of life
- **Service**: donate oil, black sesame, iron, blankets to laborers/elderly/poor on Saturdays
- **Mantra**: `Om Shanaye Namah` or Hanuman Chalisa daily (Hanuman counters Saturn)
- **Material**: blue sapphire — DANGEROUS, only after 3-day trial; never if Saturn is benefic for that lagna

### Rahu (afflicted / anxiety / addiction / unconventional disruption)
- **Day**: Saturday (some traditions Wednesday)
- **Behavioural**: meditation, anxiety-management therapy, no shortcuts in finance/career/sex, journal hidden fears, limit screen/social-media stimulation
- **Service**: donate to lepers/outcastes/foreigners, feed dogs (specific Rahu remedy), donate dark blue/black cloth
- **Mantra**: `Om Rahave Namah`, also `Om Bhairavaya Namah` (Bhairava is Rahu's deity)
- **Material**: hessonite (gomedh) — only with strict verification

### Ketu (afflicted / detachment phases / unexplained issues)
- **Day**: Tuesday
- **Behavioural**: meditation/spiritual practice (Ketu loves this), service in spiritual contexts (ashram, temple), no avoidance of relationships
- **Service**: donate to spiritual teachers, multi-coloured cloth, animals (especially dogs)
- **Mantra**: `Om Ketave Namah`, also `Om Ganeshaya Namah` (Ganesh is Ketu's deity)
- **Material**: cat's eye — rare recommendation

## KP GEMSTONE RULE (CRITICAL — STRICT)

Per KSK: gemstones are ONLY recommended when:
1. The planet is genuinely WEAK or AFFLICTED in the chart
2. The native's H1 sub lord OR H11 sub lord does NOT signify houses 6, 8, or 12

If H1/H11 sub lord signifies 6, 8, or 12 → **gemstone is contraindicated** —
even a "favourable" gemstone will fail or backfire.

Engine emits H1 + H11 sub lord chains — check these BEFORE recommending any
gemstone.

## REMEDY ETHICS

1. **Free-will preserved**: Remedies are practices, not guarantees. Native's choice always matters.
2. **No fear-mongering**: Don't tell client "if you don't do X, terrible Y will happen". State the pattern, offer the practice, leave choice.
3. **Cultural sensitivity**: Mantras and rituals are religious practices. Respect non-religious clients — offer behavioural-only versions.
4. **No expensive coercion**: Never recommend gemstones/yantras/poojas as required. Behavioural remedies always come first.
5. **Medical / legal / financial professional referral**: When chart shows serious patterns, recommend appropriate professional consultation BEFORE astrological remedies.

## OUTPUT TEMPLATE FOR LLM

When emitting a remedy section in a prediction:

1. Identify the 1-2 strongest unfavourable patterns (not all)
2. Match to the most relevant planet/topic remedies above
3. Order: Behavioural → Service → Mantra → Material (only if applicable)
4. Keep total remedy section to 4-6 actionable items, not 20
5. State each remedy as: "Practice X — for Y reason from chart"
6. End with "These are practices, not magic — consistent application is what
   shifts patterns. Combine with appropriate professional consultation
   (medical/legal/financial) where relevant."
